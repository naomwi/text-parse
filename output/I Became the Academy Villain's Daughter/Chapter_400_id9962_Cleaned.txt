# 400 - Don't Do That, Please Stop

Xing and Mob headed to the cafeteria where they found Dobi brazenly sitting and eating his meal.

"Dobi."
"This is my seat. If you're looking for a place to sit, I suggest you go elsewhere."
"That won't do. You were the one who coveted what's mine first."

Xing poured cold water over Dobi's tray.
Mealtime was the only sacred time respected even among the aggressive Academy students.
Xing's direct violation of this unspoken rule caused murmurs to spread throughout the cafeteria.

"You choose. Be humiliated in front of everyone or quietly follow me somewhere private."

Just until recently, Dobi had been nothing but a failing student on the verge of expulsion.
Yet despite Xing's blatant provocation, rather than trembling in fear, Dobi simply smiled and stood up.

"I know a suitable place. Follow me."

First-year advanced class student Xing challenged first-year lower class student Dobi to a fight, and Dobi accepted!
Excited by this thrilling news, the first-years put down their meals and followed them out.
While watching a fight was exciting in itself, curiosity was further piqued by Dobi suddenly going crazy enough to accept what amounted to suicide.

"Doesn't Xing, despite his nasty temper, rarely pick fights with others first?"
"Right. What is Dobi thinking, accepting Xing's challenge when he should be worried about failing? Is he trying to convince himself he didn't fail but was killed by a classmate for some honorable death?"
"Don't talk nonsense. What's the point of dying when you came to the Academy to advance in life?"
"But Dobi has been acting strange lately. There's something oddly charismatic about him."
"He must have gone mad after visiting the professor's lab."
"Maybe he was forcibly injected with a third-year's soul."
"What? Dobi ate a senior student's soul?"
"They say he's the second soul-eater in the first year after Oknodie!"

As always at the Academy, false rumors spread like wildfire!
Eventually, a distorted rumor spread that soul-eater Dobi was trying to eat Oknodie's soul to become stronger because they shared the same class, and pedophile Xing had challenged him to stop this.

"Black Knight Mob. Is this rumor true?"
"I heard Xing and Dobi are fighting up ahead. You know something, don't you?"
"You commute daily to an organization that plays with Oknodie. Is Xing really a pedo swordsman?"
"Does Oknodie eat souls?"
"What do you think about the theory that Xing is trying to prove Dobi's weakness first to prevent him from being eaten by Oknodie?"

Faced with the barrage of bizarre rumors, Mob drew a line.

"Don't ask me. I have nothing I can answer."
"I knew it...! Xing really is a pedo swordsman."
"Both Dobi and Oknodie must be soul-eaters!"
"Like a true Black Knight who swore never to lie, he's evading the truth!"
"Did Mob ever make such a vow?"
"All knights in fairy tales did!"

Of course, Mob knew the truth.
These were just baseless rumors.
Still, he didn't bother to deny them directly.
Xing was a powerful rival he would have to overcome someday.
There was no reason to dislike his opponent's reputation deteriorating on its own.

"However, I cannot let anyone pass through here."
"Who are you to stop us!"
"...If you want to pass, defeat me first."

When Mob revealed his killing intent, recalling Xing's earlier move, the students flinched noticeably.

"Tch. Let's go back."
"What a bore."
"I'm not running away because I know I can't win. I just don't feel like fighting, so don't get the wrong idea!"

When Mob glared fiercely from under his helmet, the student who had spoken last squeaked and ran away.

'What a waste of time.'

The Dobi that Mob remembered was the weak Dobi who had joined the library expedition.
While the doomsday cult gathering and the incident with the second-years being enchanted seemed strange, it never occurred to him that Dobi could defeat Xing.
That's why, after sensing an ominous flow of mana from inside, Mob expected Xing to walk out.

'His opponent is that Eastern swordsman Xing. In one-on-one combat, he's an absolute powerhouse with few rivals among first-years except the hero and Oknodie.'

As he was thinking about how hard it would be to hide his murderous intent while looking at that shameless face, footsteps approached.
Not regular steps, nor perfectly balanced weight distribution ready to strike down an enemy at any moment, but the gait of a madman with irregular stride and balance.
Surprisingly, the person who emerged from the alley was not Xing but Dobi.

"You, how..."
"Are you trying to interfere with me too?"

Dobi's pupils flickered ominously.
Mob realized.
Whatever he had done, this guy had defeated Xing.
He himself would be no match.

"Answer me one thing. What did you do to Oknodie in a swimsuit with those tentacles? Depending on your answer, I'll report you to the student council and Professor Mahabharata."

Dobi, who had shown no reluctance to fight and hadn't even averted his gaze as if daring Mob to come at him, suddenly looked like he'd been hit hard as he ran his hand over his face.

"I didn't do anything. Lady Oknodie wore the swimsuit of her own will and communicated with the monster of her own will. She used a curtain as a medium to communicate in a secret language of a different species, then put the monster in a mobile tank and left. That's all."
"You expect me to believe that?"
"If you don't believe me, go directly to Lady Oknodie's lecture tomorrow and ask her yourself. She took in the monster for her own purposes."

Mob was shocked.

"Tomorrow is... Saturday?"

Isn't Saturday a day off?
Why would there be a lecture?


* * *


Oknodie, whom Mob had expected to be depressed after suffering terrible treatment, greeted him perfectly normally.

"Oknodie. Are you okay?"
"I'm completely fine?"
"Didn't Dobi do something terrible to you?"

Oknodie tilted her head and answered innocently.

"I'm fine, but the seniors might not be!"
"That guy... did he do something to the seniors too!"
"Taking away your summon was too much, wasn't it?"
"Even an animal!!!"
"Oh, it wasn't an animal. It wasn't an animal-type summon but a mineral-type summon!"
"That man even does it with non-living things!! To think that such a delicate and servile face was hiding the perverted instincts of inhuman sorcery!!"

In the end, the conversation with Oknodie only increased Mob's worries rather than reassuring him.

"Are you really okay? Wearing a swimsuit and doing that kind of thing with tentacles and curtains..."

Seeing Mob unable to continue speaking, Oknodie's confusion grew even more.

"Does Mob want to see it too?"
"Stop saying such shameless things!!"
"Huh?? I don't understand why Mob is getting angry..."
"I'm sorry... for raising my voice. But Oknodie, you shouldn't say such things carelessly."
"Really?"
"You need to value yourself more."
"I guess so. Showing precious things to just anyone isn't right, is it?"
"Of course not."
"But Mob is my apprentice. You're not just anyone."

Time momentarily stopped for Mob as Oknodie put her finger to her mouth, pondering.
This scene might be etched in his memory for the rest of his life.

"Okay. From now on, I won't carelessly show my things to newbies!"
"Y-yeah..."
"Are you okay? Your face suddenly turned red. Dobi was like that earlier too. Is there a flu event going around? Geez. You should have diligently raised your disease resistance."

As Mob was being swept along by Oknodie's pace as usual and about to be left behind in a daze, he snapped back to attention when he noticed the wheeled box behind Oknodie.

"Tentacles!!"
"Yes?"
"Why are you still carrying those tentacles around? You should submit such things to the student council immediately and use them to report that Dobi guy."

Oknodie slapped Mob's hand as he reached for the box.

"Don't!!"
"Argh!!"

<Sturdiness>
<Toughness>
<Impact Resistance>
<Heavy Armor Defense>

Despite all his defensive abilities cultivated through rigorous training, Mob's arm bent 720 degrees backward in his armor, and he was thrown back with a roll.
It was fortunate he managed to break his fall; if he had hit the ground head-first with that rotating backward collision, he might have been knocked out for a full day from just one slap from Oknodie.

"This is a baby kraken. I didn't bring it to give to someone else; I brought it to use myself. How could I submit it to the student council!"
"O-Oknodie... that's not right... a child like you already using tentacles..."
"Ugh, Mob saying such naughty things should just pass out!"

Oknodie squatted in front of the fallen Mob, bringing her knees together.
As Mob's gaze was distracted by the soft part where her calves and thighs folded, a powerful flick landed on his helmet with a "twang!" sound.
Unable to withstand the tremendous impact resonating inside his helmet, Mob fainted with a squeak.
