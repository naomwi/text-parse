# 383 - Bullied Oknodie

Tetrapos was going crazy with frustration.
If he avoided the first-years and their clicking footsteps by taking the next best shortcut, monsters would appear guarding the path.
After fierce battles, when he tried to enter the fastest route, the clicking footsteps that were clearly behind him would draw closer.

'This energy consumption is concerning. Even more so, I can't predict Velvet's elusive movements.'

Velvet, the absolute powerhouse who stood out overwhelmingly among the numerous members of the Western Nobility Union.
Her pursuit—a student whose promotion to fourth year was already guaranteed—made Tetrapos, who had spent considerable time advancing to third year, too afraid to attempt a direct confrontation.
That fear drove him further into a corner.

Creeak, creeak.

"Damn wooden puppet."

When his pace slowed, Dorothy's puppet would rush in for a suicide attack.

"Found him!"
"That senior can't kill us. Let's hit him with long-range sniper magic!"

The fearless first-year wizards fired magic from a distance, further irritating him.
If he endured and pressed forward, he'd repeatedly encounter boss monsters on the shortcuts, and whenever he found a path he could avoid them on, those ghostly footsteps would follow in succession.
No matter how he looked at it, this was strange.

'Even for Velvet, this is too much. Everything is falling into place too perfectly.'

He had personally experienced Velvet's infamous ability to pursue with those click-clack footsteps no matter how quickly he fled or how cleverly he hid.
However, using surrounding traps to bind his feet wasn't Velvet's style.
Typically, third-years set traps.
Velvet would get caught in them, slowing her down.
Tetrapos's role was to be surprised that she still managed to break through and pursue him.
But now, no matter how he looked at it, Tetrapos himself was the one falling into traps.

"I've been patient long enough."

After single-handedly defeating boss monsters seven times, Tetrapos stopped fleeing and directed all his energy toward detecting his enemy.

—Click.

A clear sound echoed in his ear.
Tracing the reverberations, his head snapped toward the ceiling.

"Velvet. Let's see that precious face of yours."

With a bang, the ventilation duct cover fell away.
A small figure dropped through the open duct.

"Ouch."
"You're... not Velvet."
"That hurt!"
"I've heard about you. A girl under 140cm. The unprecedented Dark Princess who serves as the foundation's first scholarship student with top honors. So it was you."
"Wow. Really? I'm famous even among third-years?"
"Anyone who saw you throw the Physical Education Department Head Barksalr during the sports festival couldn't forget."
"Hehe. I was pretty strong, wasn't I?"
"Still not Velvet's level though."

Bloodshot light gathered in Tetrapos's eyes.
Looking closely, veins bulged on the back of his hand, and the threads wrapped around his fingers writhed like snakes.

"You bastards. Don't tell me you used me to clear out the annoying boss monsters on the way to the library. I'll drain your blood to the brink of death as payment for depleting my precious blood supply!!"

Thoroughly enraged, all the threads in Tetrapos's hand melted down and formed into a blood-red orb.
A high-speed rotating sphere that pulverizes everything it touches, growing larger with the blood of enemies—an all-purpose killing technique effective against humans, monsters, and magic users alike!
Facing this ultimate blood manipulation technique, Oknodie rummaged through her backpack and pulled something out.

"Ta-da! What's this?"
"A blood pouch? And it's completely full... You, don't tell me!"
"I collected a lot of blood too!"

<Cruel Blood Manipulation Technique>
<Oknodie-style Blood Ring Counter>

Oknodie launched a sphere identical to Tetrapos's but in the opposite direction, perfectly neutralizing it.
Seeing the blood splatter from both blood rings spreading in the air, Tetrapos reached out, but Oknodie was faster, pulling a long stick from her backpack.

<Vacuum Cleaner (+5 Enhancement)>

She sucked up the blood with a machine equipped with area absorption functionality!
Tetrapos, unaware of the foundation's abundant resources and the treasures in Oknodie's backpack, couldn't have anticipated such a device.
He had no way of knowing his opponent would counter with the same technique, so his reaction speed suffered.
First-year versus third-year.
Despite his superior specs, he was completely outmaneuvered in the psychological battle, and his opponent's thorough preparation exploited that gap, producing the astonishing result of stealing blood from the blood thief himself.

[You have stolen the blood thief's blood right in front of him.]
[Stealing experience +10]
[Boldness experience +3]
[Provocation experience +3]
[Action Prediction experience +1]

Tetrapos was truly furious now.

"As the top student of your year, you must be physically strong too. As someone who walks the same path of blood magic, you must understand the significance of stealing blood. I'll show you my killing technique with the genuine intent to kill."

Threads combining offense and defense instantly spread in all directions, cutting off Oknodie's escape routes.

<Cruel Blood Manipulation Technique>
<Sealing Technique - Heaven and Earth Net>

As the net-like threads covered the sky and ground, leaving no escape route, <Metal Skin> was applied, bringing back the blood-red color.
This time, the threads were filled with high-density blood that couldn't be neutralized by amateur power!

"Hmph. I never liked Tetrapos, who doesn't belong to any club but suddenly appears to bully lower-year students by stealing their blood. You've met your match today!"

Category - Dark Magic
Activation - Electricity Generation
Specialization - Synchronization
Auxiliary Formula - Conductivity, Protection

<3rd Tier - Electric Trap>

Oknodie applied the magic of the conductive trap she had used between swords when facing Instructor Russo to the threads.
She exploited an unexpected weakness in the metallic effect of 'Metal Skin'.
Tetrapos, realizing her intention, panicked greatly.

<Metal Skin Release>
<Blood Technique - Blood Control>

Just as black current raced fiercely across the blood-red threads, Metal Skin was released, and threads in several sections where blood had disappeared went limp.
With the conductive medium gone, the electricity was cut off in mid-air.
Tetrapos avoided electrocution from the dark electric generation magic by making these changes at different timings in multiple places almost simultaneously.

<Blood Technique - Blood Control>

Seizing the gap when his control was broken, Oknodie used blood manipulation to pull the blood from the threads closest to her.

[You have stolen the blood thief's blood right in front of him twice in a row.]
[Stealing experience +20]
[Boldness experience +5]
[Provocation experience +5]
[Action Prediction experience +2]
[Psychological Prediction experience +2]

Tetrapos had his blood stolen before his eyes twice in succession, defeated by a mere 3rd-tier Electric Trap magic.
He now felt a sense of duty beyond anger.
He would kill this damn brat.
If he couldn't, sleepless nights would follow.

"You ill-mannered little thief, try stealing this if you can!!"

A three-hit combo of blood magic: a waterfall-like Cruel Blood Bubble area attack, mixed with a single-target Blood Wire attack that converged into one, followed by consecutive Bloody Shooting spells!

—Click.

The moment his offensive began, another click sound that clearly didn't come from Oknodie made Tetrapos's boiling rage instantly cool.

'Is it the real one this time!?'

Tetrapos hastily recalled over 70% of the energy he had released and surveyed his surroundings.
With his enhanced perception, he spotted a different ventilation duct than before.

"...Bloody Sphere."

A spear of blood penetrated the ventilation duct cover, instantly corroding the ceiling, and a black-haired girl slightly larger than Oknodie, dressed neatly like a doll, dropped down.
Naturally, she held another pair of shoes in her hand, just as Oknodie had when she appeared.
Unable to hide his dismay, he watched as Oknodie blocked all of his attacks—which contained only 30% of his power—and sucked up his blood with the vacuum cleaner again.

[You have stolen the blood thief's blood right in front of him three times in a row.]
[Stealing experience +30]
[Boldness experience +10]
[Provocation experience +10]
[Action Prediction experience +5]
[Psychological Prediction experience +5]

I'll really kill them.
I'll definitely kill them.
Even if I die, I'll make sure they die too!!

<Cruel Blood Manipulation Technique>
<Forbidden Summoning - Blood Gate Opening>

"Oknodie. Isn't that... really dangerous?"

Tetrapos literally made blood gush upward, creating a magic circle drawn in blood in mid-air.
A magic circle of exchange opened, borrowing large amounts of inferior blood in return for offering delicious blood to creatures living in another dimension made of blood.

"I'll offer Professor Pinkberry's blood that I stole using another blood mage's mosquitoes. Give me the massive amount of blood I need for the great magic to defeat those damn brats!"

Woooong.
The magic circle vibrated intensely, signaling the completion of the deal.

—Click.

Amidst the chaos, the sound of shoes clicking again.
Being fooled twice was enough.
He wouldn't be fooled a third time.

Click-clack, click-clack.

Despite the growing sound of footsteps, he didn't stop gathering power.
They must be using some auditory illusion magic.
Clearly another trick to make him lose control of his blood and steal it.
He wouldn't be fooled again.
This time he would pulverize them.
And then he would drain all the blood they'd stolen, all the blood they possessed, and all the blood in their bodies!

"Blood thief Tetrapos. Ignoring my presence? You've grown quite bold while I wasn't looking."
"Haha, not a chance. You can imitate Velvet's appearance, Velvet's voice, and Velvet's presence, but I won't be fooled again. This attack will absolutely not stop!"
"Boldly violating school rules and declaring you'll use great magic against first-years right in front of a student council officer. You've really grown confident. Let's see if your skills have grown as much."

The moment Velvet's shoe kicked the air as if stomping on something, the large blood gate completely collapsed.

"Huh?"

Right after Tetrapos's dumbfounded utterance, Velvet's heel firmly pressed the ground.
Before the great magic he had been flamboyantly preparing with borrowed blood could reveal its true power, a massive shadow fell over the entire formation.

<Spatial Synchronization>
<Magnification 1:1000>

A foot magnified a thousand times larger crushed the blood gate, the great magic, and Tetrapos all at once.
In the depression left behind after the giant shoe disappeared, Tetrapos could be seen squirming, having barely saved his life by deploying a blood armor.
