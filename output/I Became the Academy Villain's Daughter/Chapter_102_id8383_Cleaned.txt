# Enhanced Translation: Chapter 102 - Friends Doing Homework

The obligation of students to follow the Academy's rules.
The obligation of freshmen not to wander around late at night.
The obligation of students to diligently complete their assignments.

"How am I supposed to follow the rule about not wandering around at night when I need to set up targets at 50m, 100m, 150m, and 200m distances to complete my assignment?"

'Looks like I won't be getting to bed early tonight either.'

Her master had warned her that wandering around late at night would stunt her growth, and had made her promise to get proper sleep once she entered the Academy.
It seemed her title as the third shortest student among the 981st class would remain for a long time.

"Hey, have you seen Oknodie?"

'She must be planning to do her assignment outside and come back later.'

Oknodie knew an unusually large amount about Gift Academy.

The Room of Wisdom, where the wisdom of seniors was written on double-sided sticky notes.
A secret place that could only be entered at specific times and locations.
The Room of Double-Sided Notes.

'There must be hints there.'
