Plus, you can use the <Teleportation Magic Circle>, the special facility on the 60th floor, to escape to outside cities, buy and gather plenty of enhancement materials, and return.

Heroes, their companions, or players—the structure is properly designed for the strong to grow stronger quickly.

But shortcuts exist everywhere in the world.

The Living Armor shortcut is a path reserved only for veterans. Xing, who had benefited from this advantage, has been keeping his mouth shut and vigilantly watching his surroundings for quite some time.

Well, after experiencing the Living Armor's strength firsthand, it's no wonder he can't relax. Each of those armors is as strong as a hero or even more dangerous. If we had tried to pass through normally, we would have struggled even as corpses.

[Entering B4F <Surplus Resource Storage Floor>.]

If there's one item that escapes this fate, it's cloth backpacks!
Even Papa, with his considerable power, couldn't hide his shock at seeing backpacks spread throughout the entire fourth floor.

"This place... has nothing but backpacks."

And my backpack is a backpack-storage specialized backpack that can hold up to 99 backpacks per slot.
