# Hector's Vassals 2

A merchant is the treasure goblin of the human world.
They carry valuable goods in abundance, yet their combat power is so weak they die with a single hit.
In this land, there were far too many men who couldn't sleep at night thinking about the treasures they let slip away if they didn't rob these delicious prey.

"That's why your father has spared no expense to prepare this solution."
"An artifact? Couldn't we just hire mercenaries instead of spending on something so expensive?"
"Remember this, Yui. Mercenaries can abandon their employers and flee when situations turn unfavorable. In the end, only you can protect yourself."

Betrayal by subordinates.
Betrayal by business partners.
Betrayal by mercenary escorts.

Having grown up watching her father suffer through countless betrayals, Yui craved power more than any other merchant.
That's why she possessed numerous artifacts.
Shoes of marching that never tire the feet.
The eagle's mark that sees far distances.
Ogre gauntlets that grant superhuman strength.
These were items she couldn't use during the entrance exam.
In that fierce competition right before admission, there were too many stronger opponents, and too many plunderers trying to kill her and steal her artifacts.
Using artifacts remained difficult during the first semester as well.
Points were required for "possession permits" to carry artifacts around, and additional points were needed for "usage permits" to actually use them.
She couldn't waste points like that when she needed capital for her business.
But now in the second semester.
Now that she had infiltrated the organization called Hector's Vassals and gained the opportunity to exert her influence.
Yui had long since acquired both "possession permits" and "usage permits" for her massive collection of artifacts.

Possession permits to carry her artifacts in case of emergency.
Usage permits to use artifacts for academic purposes like exams and assignments.

Having obtained both, Yui had regained the powerful strength that had once driven her to lead her merchant group into one of the Three Great Forbidden Forests.

"That's the power of treasures!"
"Where did all those treasures come from?"
"I've heard treasures choose their owners. To meet all those demanding conditions and still be able to handle them!"

Treasures are items enhanced to +5 or higher.
Even those unaware of this imperial taboo know about the side effects caused by such enhancements.
The enormous mana consumption required by highly enhanced items.
To compensate for this, each item has restrictions added to reduce or eliminate mana consumption.

Restrictions that prevent removal once equipped.
Restrictions requiring the death of one life before a drawn sword can be sheathed.
Restrictions that make the wearer appear repulsive to the opposite sex.

The rarer the item, the greater the mana consumption it eliminates, and thus the greater the accompanying restrictions.
That's why artifact collectors who gather items for actual use carefully select only artifacts with similar or manageable restrictions.
There are even people who make their living as artifact trackers, specializing in identifying the owners and locations of artifacts with specific restrictions and introducing them to clients!

Since not everyone was born with tremendous mana like Titoso, anyone equipped with numerous artifacts was bound to be extraordinary.
It was proof they either trained hard enough to overcome the many restrictions or possessed enough wealth to carefully select artifacts with similar restrictions to minimize penalties.

Yui belonged to the latter category.
With all her hidden artifacts equipped, she exerted her strength directly, causing the massive roots to give way rather than crushing her.

Boom boom boom!

"Gasp! The side! The ground is shifting from the side!"
"I'll try to block one side!"

The enraged pet tree pushed the soil with different roots.
Hector infused his sword strikes with magic to expand their range and increase their power, causing the soil to recede like waves hitting a breakwater.

"We'll try to block this side!"

Deiphobus and the few students still able to move placed their hands on a mana barrier to hold back the soil advancing from behind.

"Nobody move!"

Three rings on Yui's hand simultaneously emitted light.

<Ring of Mana Amplification>
<Ring of Phase Transition>
<Ring of Output Enhancement>

She supplemented her insufficient mana, changed the position of the advancing soil, and increased her output to expand the effect range.
As a secondary effect of the spatial exchange, dazzling light burst forth as sand and rocks colliding like bullets and cannonballs.

<Bracelet of Projectile Velocity Adjustment>
<Gloves of Auto-Targeting>
<Magic Spear Beads> × 25

The beads she pulled from her pocket by the handful transformed into magic spears, flying out to counter the pet tree Popi's follow-up branch-piercing attacks with equal force.
The enraged Popi began lifting even its deepest roots that had been spread across the forest floor, revealing the massive figure hidden beneath the ground.
Even Yui, who had been fighting on equal terms momentarily, darkened her expression.

"At this rate, it's large-species class...?"

Everyone despaired at the signs of the tree, which had been at medium-monster level, growing even larger, thinking they had no choice but to give blood to the untrustworthy third-year senior.
Tetraphose smiled gleefully.
After blood stones were accidentally released in the Academy and he made significant progress in refining his cruel blood techniques.
After further improving his skills during the break, he began to crave the blood of other students, prompting persistent intervention from the student council.
First-year students who came on their own to this place where he was catching his breath away from the rampaging student council.
To his eyes, it was better than an all-you-can-eat buffet.
Of course, upperclassmen cannot initiate confrontation with first-years.
Even if they do, the permissible range is limited.
However, if the juniors themselves seek help, if they express their willingness to pay blood as a fair price, even indirectly.
If the student council isn't present at the scene, he could easily extract their blood.

'Keep thrashing about, tree. I will become the most prosperous one in this forest. Your fertilizers will become my blood pouches!'

If not for the unexpected performance of the student named Yui, he could have drained their blood until they were nearly mummies, but now they would only suffer anemia.
Still, that was enough.
If he pushed the first-years to their limits, there would be plenty of opportunities to extract blood.

As the second battle approached.
In the moment filled with the ecstasy of the strong dominating the weak.

"!?"

The pet tree Popi stopped all movement and looked somewhere.
As if an overwhelming predator had appeared.
Tetraphose quickly realized what the direction of that intense stare meant.

'That place is a trial gateway connected by dimensional doors, like this shortcut...?'

The difference would be that it's far from the pet tree Popi, and one could safely exit the forest if sufficiently cautious.
However, the number of prey would be much greater there.
There would be no reason for a small number of people to use such a large path that just takes more time.
Yet the tree monster remained vigilant.
It was evidence that an incredibly powerful being was there.
At least as strong as Tetraphose himself when revealing his true nature.
As far as he knew, such an opponent would have to be at least an executive of the student council even among third-years.
The undisputed leader commanding the Western Nobility Alliance.
The possessor of an atmosphere like a noble gem, combining strength and beauty, the noble lady Velvet.

'Tch. Lucky break.'

Instead of enjoying his entertainment, Tetraphose urged the disoriented first-years.

"The monster's attention is elsewhere. Shall we pass through?"

Only then did Hector's Vassals breathe sighs of relief as they escaped through the gateway.
After exiting, they stopped to rest.
Tetraphose grew anxious.
He never knew when he might hear Velvet's footsteps.
No matter how quickly he fled or how cleverly he hid, the experience of hearing those click-clack footsteps pursuing him without growing distant made one nervous.
Especially for someone with something to hide.

"Luck was on our side, but I never expected we'd safely pass through three gateways. You've all been training hard, haven't you?"
"Does this look safe to you, senior...?"

As Yui pointed out, none of the first-years were uninjured.
Even Hector was sweating profusely, his long hair sticking to his cheeks.

"This is as far as we go. If the next gateway is stronger than this, someone could actually die. Honestly, we almost reached that point already."

At least with Yui's skills, she couldn't guide them without sacrifices.
Even breaking through the Monster Forest, one of the Three Great Forbidden Forests, had required the efforts of a much stronger merchant group's direct forces, and she wasn't confident in passing through gateways of rapidly increasing difficulty with these weaker companions.
Of course, Tetraphose wasn't about to let his juniors go so easily.

"Turning back now? Haha, juniors. Have you forgotten what path we just came through?"

In response to his reference to the third gateway, Yui snorted and pointed to the route guarded by instructors that everyone had consciously ignored.

"We can go to the instructor and ask to be escorted back to the dormitory."
"Hmm. Is that so? I wasn't aware of that option."

Tetraphose nodded and quickly stretched out his hand to collapse the ceiling of the narrow path.

"Not anymore."
"..."
"Come on, let's hurry. Don't worry too much. I'll faithfully show you the fastest shortcut through the fourth gateway too. Hahaha!"

Yui and Hector had no choice but to follow him, even as they glared at him.
In this gloomy atmosphere, worlds apart from the cheerful picnic of the organization playing with Oknodie, Yui used her fingernail in her pocket to carve words onto a note.
The note passed through the hands of several vassals before reaching Hector.

━━━
Want to drag this senior to Oknodie's group.
Nod if you agree
━━━

Using the enemy's strength against the enemy.
Hector nodded with great pleasure.
