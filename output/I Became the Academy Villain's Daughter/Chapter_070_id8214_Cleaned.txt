# 70 - Schola, Descendant of the Divine Archer vs. Oknodie, the Archer of Superhuman Strength

Mob's passionate sprint affected the two runners at the front as well.

'Those guys are catching up faster and faster!'
'Impossible! What on earth are they doing to catch up at such a ridiculous speed?!'

The desperate sprint of a man who had abandoned everything, fueled by rule-breaking, and Oknodie's sincere running inspired by deep emotion created a fierce pursuit. With each track section they passed, the gap narrowed, causing Schola and Live to repeatedly feel the urge to look back.

"Don't look back!"
"Aren't you curious too, Schola?"
"It's hard enough just focusing on running forward!"

When Oknodie went back to save her teammate, Schola thought to himself: rescuing fallen allies on the battlefield was a foolish act that only endangered oneself. So he made a decision. Even if she realized her mistake belatedly and abandoned her teammate to run ahead alone, he wouldn't even allow second place to such a foolish person.

Live suppressed his anxiety at Schola's advice.

'The descendant of the divine archer is matching my pace. He must have expectations for me!'

Among the 981st class students, not a single person could shoot a bow better than Schola. No one could deny this fact. That's why Schola's advice carried special meaning in this "Ranged Weapon Mastery" class. The top student at the Academy was looking after him. That meant he expected Live to be second place. Above that A-group prodigy. That he could surpass even the youngest genius Oknodie who had overcome formidable frontier nobles!

<Course 3>
<Cliff Climbing>

A steep cliff nearly at 90 degrees.
Protrusions throughout that could be used as footholds or handholds.
A course lined with ropes hanging from above and safety equipment that could be securely attached to the body.
Some targets could be shot from below, but others were placed in extremely challenging positions that could only be hit from above.

"Use the safety equipment if it's too difficult!"

Saying this, Schola nimbly clung to the cliff at full running speed and climbed up with agility.

'F-fast!!'

Agility is one of the basic qualities for an archer.
The same goes for grip strength that can support your entire body weight with just fingers inserted into small crevices.
His heart was saying: I want to run up like that too.
But his head was saying: You know that's impossible for you.

'Damn it, those guys have been pushing themselves to chase after us too!'

Live stubbornly persisted.
Without safety equipment, he forcefully grabbed protruding rocks and quickly climbed up the cliff. He ventured forth with his bare body, neither tying the rope around his waist nor attaching safety equipment.

Thump-thump

Yet he could feel it.
The burning energy from two lanes over that made his skin tingle, the pressure of being chased by a giant who seemed to be 230cm tall.
Though he knew he shouldn't look back, his head eventually turned toward Oknodie's lane 5.
At that moment, Oknodie had already climbed much higher.
He knew immediately upon seeing her.

'This is impossible.'

That he could never win.
Faced with the limits of his talent, Live's hands froze.
His dream of placing second in the group competition slipped away before his eyes.


* * *


Live fell to third place in the group competition.
He gave up.
Challenging those ahead of him.
Oknodie was different.
She pursued Schola, who was climbing the cliff just ahead of her, at a terrifying pace.

'Argh, so frustrating. If only my arms were a little longer!'

Yet the gap wouldn't close.
No, it was actually widening slightly.
Unlike Schola with his long limbs and tall stature, Oknodie's body, though well-proportioned, had shorter limbs, creating an unavoidable difference in reach.

"Ohhh!"
"Number 5 is pretty good!"
"Number 1, don't let her catch you!"
"Chase him, Number 5!"

She erased all the seniors' shouts from her mind.
The reason she was running now wasn't for the sake of the seniors' point betting.
It was for that man who wanted his teammate to advance ahead.
To repay the burning determination of a man who risked all penalty points to return a favor.
Oknodie, who had always played like a giant with excessive muscles and unnecessarily large stature in every round, admired such passionate dedication more than anyone.

'My body may be small, but my heart is bigger than anyone's!'

Schola quickly rotated his body as soon as he reached the top of the cliff and fired five consecutive shots.

<Lane 1, Course 3 complete!>
<Lane 5, Course 3 complete! +0.08.61>

Oknodie, who succeeded in climbing 8 seconds later than him, strung her bow.
There were no visible aiming points.
No convenient automatic magic arrows.
She utilized all her accumulated knowledge and trained senses to manually nock and shoot each arrow.

[You conquered Course 3 in one go without stopping once.]
[Boldness experience +10]
[Climbing experience +5]
[Terrain analysis experience +2]

[You hit all targets in Course 3.]
[Rapid fire experience +3]
[Concentration experience +3]

The time gap had widened further, but looking at the upcoming Course 4, she couldn't help but smile.

<Course 4>
<Lift Action>

A descending lift extending diagonally from the cliff top down to the forest below.
Shockingly, the lift made of ropes and biclo had no foothold, only bar-shaped handles to hang onto.
Of course, shooting arrows requires hands.
This lift was boldly challenging them.
Asking if they could move without using their hands while hitting targets with a bow.
If they weren't confident, they could simply hang on and pass through.

"Hyah!"

As she quickly jumped and grabbed the bar, the Lane 5 descending lift began to operate with a jolt.

[You hung onto the descending lift without a second of hesitation.]
[Boldness experience +3]

Clunk, rrrrrumble!

The descending lift shook violently like a downhill roller coaster without safety equipment.
Even knowing the instructor would catch her if she fell, her heart pounded with chilling tension.
The fear was momentary.
While maintaining an inverted position with her legs wrapped around the handle, she spotted Mob in the distance, limping toward the cliff.
Flash.
She raised her hand in response to Mob, who raised his fist as if to say he was watching her.

'If I had known he was such an impressive person, I would have charged more for the flag.'

Courage recharged.
With the time gap reduced due to a moment's hesitation, she entered an inverted aerial shooting competition with Schola on the descending lift.

Thwack-thwack-thwack!

As expected, Schola displayed terrifying rapid-fire capability with magic arrows.

Twang!

In response, she fired simple power shots, manually loading each arrow and shooting with all her might.
There were ten targets to hit in mid-air.
Considering the speed of the descending lift, there was only one chance per target.
The rapid-fire shooter could afford to miss two out of three shots, but as a power shot user, she had no such luxury.

Whoosh

'Oh no.'

The lift jolted from the wind blowing in mid-air.
Her aim wavered, causing her to miss the shooting timing for a moment.
Schola also missed all three of his arrows.

'Don't panic. If you crumble after missing one beat, you'll miss all the remaining targets.'

During extremely difficult battles that make your head spin, there are plenty of crazy things that make this lift seem trivial by comparison.

[You quickly recovered from a momentary mistake with iron-like mental strength.]
[Mental strength experience +10]
[Concentration experience +10]
[Moving shot experience +5]
[Acrobatic shooting experience +5]
[Aerial shooting experience +5]

Good.
I hit nine out of ten.
As she was about to think that while shooting her last arrow, an arrow flying from the side knocked hers away.

"You've got to be kidding."

That ruthless guy.
He's struggling enough to focus on his own targets in mid-air, yet he attempts interference and even succeeds?
She almost shot an arrow at his lift rope out of anger, but stopped herself.

'Mob wouldn't want that kind of competition.'

She threw her bow and quiver onto the sand, rolled sideways for landing, then got up to retrieve her equipment and sprinted with all her might toward the final goal point.

<Lane 1, Course 4 complete!>
<Lane 5, Course 4 complete! +0:01:57>

Her arrival time was slightly slower, but she got up faster.

<Course 5>
<Full Sprint>

Though her legs were trembling and her body aching, she ran with everything she had.

"Ugh!"

Schola's voice was heard, expressing frustration at his body not responding as he wished.
Without looking back at him, she ran at full speed toward the destination.
50 meters to the finish line. 10 meters. One step.

<Goal!>

Schola and Oknodie.
The two reached the finish line almost simultaneously.

"Woooow!"
"Both of them are amazing!"
"D! I finished earlier and was watching. That was totally awesome!"
"Not very assassin-like. But somehow cool."
"Hehehe. Land dwellers can be pretty good sometimes, huh?"

Arcadia, Zuang, and even Jigoku.
Even students practicing other weapons had gathered to watch the spectacular match.
Now it was time to announce the results.
