# Enhanced Translation: Curse Resistance

The longer you stay at the Academy, you'll occasionally encounter powerful curses.
Whether it's mutant curses created from lower-level curses mixing in laboratories, curse creatures born from unauthorized experiments without student council approval, or falling victim to curses left by seniors in treasure chests.

'I was planning to raise my Curse Resistance in second year, but getting to practice early is great!'

Of course, I don't start by enduring a blindness curse and living as a blind person for a week.
The itchy sole curse.
The curse that makes you conscious of blinking.
The curse that makes you wonder whether to place your tongue on the roof of your mouth or against your lower teeth.
Building curse resistance typically begins with such trivial lower-level curses and progresses step by step.

"Let's check the bulletin board first. It said there would be instructions posted there."
"...If it's a scary curse, I'm not doing any resistance training."
"Yes, yes. Let's check first."

As I brought the nervous Tito-soga to the bulletin board, I noticed some female students already gathered there.
It's fine that they're surrounding the board with serious faces having discussions, but I wish they'd move aside after reading it.
I can't see anything because of my height!

"Ngh."

Even standing on my tiptoes with all my might, or jumping up and down while holding onto Tito-soga's shoulder (who whimpers in pain), all I can see are various colored heads.
They look just like candies.
Makes me want to bite them.
While harboring these impure thoughts, I suddenly felt weightless as my body was lifted into the air.
As I flailed in surprise, a calm, soothing voice came from behind me.

"It's me. Hestia."
"Hestia! You scared me."
"Can you see better now?"

I can see well.
Not just the bulletin board, but also the piercing gazes of the students gathered in front.

"Ah, I shouldn't laugh."
"She looks like she's being carried by her mom."

Unlike the students trying hard not to laugh, there was one who burst into laughter openly.

"Pfft! Isn't it hilarious that someone's so short they need to be lifted to see the bulletin board~?"
"Grrrr... Mesugakki princess."
"Shorty♡ Academy's shortest student♡ Maybe you should carry your own personal ladder♡"
"You're the second shortest in the Academy yourself!"
"I can't hear words from someone shorter than me♡"

Being subjected to Mesugakki's undodgeable piercing mockery first thing in the morning makes my blood boil.

"Be quiet, princess. What do you know about Oknodie?"
"Huh? Are you talking to me~?"
"Oknodie has enough hardships without you. This child has reasons why she can't grow taller. Got it?"

Hestia suddenly became aggressive.
I appreciate the help, but I don't understand what's happening.
What reason would I have for not growing taller, and how would Hestia know about it?


* *


If one considers Oknodie's hard work, staying up late to help her friend, it becomes clear.
A child in her growth phase who doesn't get adequate sleep naturally can't grow properly.

"Reasons why she can't grow taller~?"

Mesugakki princess thought about it.
A warning delivered with such a serious expression at such timing.
I see.
So that's it.
Oknodie must be under a curse that prevents her from growing taller!

"...I didn't mean to. I wasn't trying to mock someone's misfortune. Please believe me."

Being short herself, Mesugakki princess realized how harsh her words had been.

"Apologize to Oknodie directly."
"I'm sorry, Oknodie."

I was worried she might become intimidated, but Oknodie responded with a face that pretended not to understand what was happening.

"If you're sorry, give me points!"
"...How much?"
"Hmm. As much as you're sorry?"

The princess looked at her with distaste before tapping her magic watch.
When someone tells you to apologize with money, it makes you want to fight even if you were apologizing. But the wealthy princess coolly decided to just send money and be done with it.

"Sent it♡"
"Wow. 100 points? Thank you!"
"I lost out by talking to you♡ I'm going back♡"

As Mesugakki princess turned to head toward the Group B lounge, the upper-class female students followed her in a line.
Even Yayoi, the third princess who wasn't part of Mesugakki's faction, glanced around before slightly bowing her head in greeting and following the crowd.

"That's a relief. Now we can read the bulletin board comfortably."
"This is embarrassing, could you put me down now?"

Oknodie feeling shy?
That's rare.
So if I want to see this face of Oknodie again, I just need to lift her up from behind by putting my hands under her armpits?
The effect would probably be better with more people watching.
I should experiment with this again later.
While Hestia was having these sinister thoughts, Oknodie finished reading the bulletin board.
Then she sighed, saying it was disappointing.

"What? It's just the usual random weekly event happening a bit earlier."
"Random weekly event? You think this is just one of the events the Academy prepares every week?"
"Of course. If it were a truly serious curse outbreak, they wouldn't just hand out protective charms and call it a day—they'd issue a lockdown order."

Can we really expect such measures from this chaotic Academy?
At the beginning of the semester, water rose so high that we could see fish swimming outside the first floor of the dormitory from our rooms, yet we still had to attend classes. Considering that, it's hard to agree.

"Why don't you come with us, Hestia? The three of us together?"
"Go where?"
"To where the curses are!"
"Ehhh~? I have to go too?"

Tito-soga's face fell.


* *


Artificially spread curses at the Academy are accompanied by blatantly suspicious visual effects.

"Look over there, Tito-soga. There's light sparkling in the bushes for no reason. Let's put our hands in!"
"No. The plant sap might sting my hand, and the curse is scary."
"Come on."
"Waaah! I said no!"

Itchy itchy.
The urge to scratch my palm after putting it into the bushes revealed the nature of the curse.

"We got one! It's the lowest-level Itching Curse!"
"That's not winning, it's losing! It's so itchy. Hiing. It's uncomfortable like a mosquito bite."
"But the Itching Curse breaks if you scratch the itchy area a certain number of times! Or if you endure it, your curse resistance increases. What will you do, Tito-soga?"
"Can't I just scratch it?"
"That's fine. But you won't gain resistance, so next time you encounter a similar curse, you'll be itchy again."

Close your eyes and endure once to gain curse resistance vs. scratch satisfyingly and lose the chance for resistance.
While Tito-soga was caught in fierce deliberation, Hestia suddenly thrust her hand into the bushes.

"Gasp! Why did you put your hand in!...sir!"
"You can speak informally to me."
"I-I'll stick with formal speech. It's scary..."

Tito-soga, feeling intimidated by the large Hestia, became noticeably more cautious.

"War mercenaries often face harsh conditions on battlefields. We sometimes fight dark mages who command evil forces, and occasionally get cursed."
"Wow... being a mercenary must be really tough."
"The Itching Curse might seem trivial, but it was quite challenging. It breaks your concentration."

If you're cursed with an unbearable itch during a tense situation, taking your eyes off the enemy for a moment to scratch could cost you your head.
After hearing Hestia's words, Tito-soga seemed to make up his mind and clenched his fist firmly.

"Oknodie. I'll endure it too."
"Good decision!"

[You endured the Itching Curse for 1 minute and gained Curse Resistance.]
[Curse Resistance experience +1]
[Endurance experience +1]

The curse disappeared like a lie in just a moment.
We felt a strange sense of accomplishment, like we had become comrades.

"Now I can go back proudly. When I return to the dorm, who am I? Tito-soga, the one who endured the Itching Curse! I'll brag about it."
"That's cute."
"Aren't you happy, Hestia?"
"I feel good."
"I'm glad you both feel good! Then let's quickly find the next curse."

Tito-soga turned his head with a creaking sound as if he'd heard something he shouldn't have.

"Next curse?"
"This is a rare chance to easily experience harmless, safe curses. The protective charms they gave us at the dorm will block any dangerous curses, so we can't stop after just one!"
"...I want to go back."
"Ah, look over there. The fountain water is sparkling purple!"
"I really want to go back!"
"Come on!"
"Waaah!"

I've been worried about you because you seem too weak.
If we're going to attend the Academy together for a long time, you need to build up resistance at times like this.
Hang in there, Tito-soga!

"Hmm. A curse that causes respiratory swelling? What do you think is the instant solution?"
"It's a curse that's cured by blowing your nose with a 'hmph'! But if you don't blow your nose, you'll develop resistance, right?"
"This is a curse Mesugakki princess could never endure."

Imagining Mesugakki princess sniffling, we spent quite some time walking around the campus building various curse resistances.
During our excursion, we eventually encountered someone I'd been expecting to meet.

"Is the ritual complete?"

A man with such a dangerous aura that even Hestia flinched and moved to shield us.
The lonely Eastern swordsman.
...And it was Xing, whose face had swollen tremendously from a face-swelling curse.
