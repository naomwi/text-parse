# <100 - How to Overcome Fear>

The giant king crabs scuttled beneath the tree while torrential rain created a curtain of sound as it fell through the leaves. We sat in silence on the tree branch.

Sleep doesn't always come just because you want it to, so my mind began to wander.

"This reminds me of the old days."

When I absentmindedly blurted this out, Zuang and Titosoga looked at me like I was crazy.

"Don't misunderstand. I don't mean I've been chased by giant king crabs and tried to sleep in a tree before!"
"Then what experience are you referring to?"
"I'm curious too. What kind of memories do other assassins have?"

Since both of them showed interest, I shared a bit of my past.

"It wasn't rain but heavy snow, and I once found myself hanging from a cliff after taking a wrong step."
"Why were you in such a dangerous area?"
"To make the legendary snow flower shaved ice."

Zuang looked at me like I was an idiot.

"I could have wanted to eat it! It's a legendary food with amazing collection effects!"
"That kind of thing only exists in fairy tales."
"The legendary snow flower shaved ice really exists!"

Titosoga patted my shoulder.

"I understand. I believed in Santa Claus until last year too."
"Santa Claus exists too!!"

He appears as an event NPC during Christmas!
My outburst, stemming from player experiences, must have seemed strange to these NPCs.
Titosoga looked amused, while Zuang seemed concerned.

"Listen. At ten years old, you should be preparing to become an adult. Village children your age are digging potatoes, pulling weeds, and carrying firewood. You need to grow up."
"Why am I being called immature when both the legendary snow flower shaved ice and Santa Claus really exist?"
"Adults don't believe in such things. Listen, Oknodie. For an assassin, money is everything. The world is full of scammers who try to lower their fees by promising suspicious things!"

Somehow I was getting scolded now.

"Neither of you believes me?"
"Hahaha. I think it's fine. It's cute when girls believe in such things."
"Stupid Nodie. You'll regret not listening to me when you get scammed later."
"I hate both Titosoga and Zuang!"

They really do exist.
I've seen them. Hmph.

"Haha. Sorry, sorry. Don't be mad, okay? I'll shine a happy light for you later!"
"...In pink?"
"You like pink?"
"...Yes. It increases the skill proficiency coefficient."
"I like pink too!"

After chatting like this, I felt much better.
Now, what were we trying to do again?

"Geh-geh-geh. You brats are no better than goblins with your scattered concentration."
"Ugh. Sorry. We'll go to sleep quickly."
"No, it's fine. You would have woken up soon anyway even if you had fallen asleep."
"What?"
"Giant king crabs, you see, can actually climb trees!"
"Whaaaat?!"

When the skeleton instructor splashed water with a strong shrimp smell onto the tree, the giant king crabs swarmed toward us.

Click-clack-click.

They tore at the bark with their pincers, chewed it with their jaws, and even shook the tree back and forth as if it were a shrimp tree.

"Kyaaah! I-I'm going to die. I'm really going to die!"
"Stop making a fuss, Titosoga. You won't fall from the tree as long as you maintain your balance."

Whenever Titosoga's posture and balance seemed precarious, Zuang would gently push her shoulder or poke her leg to keep her from falling.

"Oknodie, what should we do? We're fine since we've had survival training, but Titosoga is in danger. That instructor definitely won't help us."
"Animals and monsters run away when they hear the cry of their natural predator!"
"Natural predator? Do giant king crabs have natural predators?"

Not many.
Even regular king crabs have few natural predators, which is why they reproduce easily and fill the ocean floor. Giant king crabs are even worse.
But king crabs aren't the only monsters in the ocean.

"Octopuses are natural predators of king crabs!"
"With that size, they'd just slice up a regular octopus. Giant octopus...? Is there such a thing?"
"It's called a kraken!"
"I see. A kraken... Wait, a kraken? That crazy octopus that can swallow large ships whole?"
"Yes."
"How would you know what sound it makes?"
"Should I try making it?"
"You... have you seen a kraken too?"
"Of course. I've hunted all the rare monsters."
"Hunted?"

Oops. I accidentally bragged about my player experiences.

"I mean, not me but my master! I watched my master hunt it."
"Wow. Your master is amazing, Oknodie. I didn't know assassination schools had secret techniques for catching krakens."
"Hmph! Whatever, just help me chase them away quickly!"

Titosoga was about to burst into tears.
I had no choice.
I had to take the initiative to help my weaker friend.

"Ahem."

I cleared my throat and rounded my mouth.
The key is using the internal oblique and rectus abdominis muscles for vocalization!

"Muooooong!"
"Pfft."
"Oknodie, this isn't the time for jokes... Huh?"

The giant king crabs that had been clinging to the tree suddenly startled and fell off.

Click-clack.

One giant king crab approached the tree again with a suspicious look.
How dare it!

"Muooooong!"

I imitated the kraken's cry loudly to establish dominance, and the fearless giant king crab jumped back five steps.

"Haha, Oknodie. That's so funny!"
"It may sound funny, but it's working!"
"Don't just laugh, help me! They probably think we're baby krakens because we're so small. Even natural predators can be attacked when they're young!"
"What should we do then?"
"We need to make the sound together!"

A chaotic battle began.

"Muooooong!"
"Muong! Muong!"
"Muooong."

We tried our best to make kraken sounds from the tree branch to drive away the giant king crabs, while they retreated momentarily but kept approaching with their click-clack sounds.
After this intense battle of nerves, I decided this wouldn't end and pulled out my secret weapon.

"Everyone, raise your arms and wiggle like cephalopods!"
"What effect will that have?"
"To them, it looks like we're trying to eat them!"
"Haha! This is so much fun!"
"Ugh. I can never tell anyone I did this at night. This is going in my dark history."

With Titosoga smiling brightly and Zuang making a sad face, we all stretched our arms and imitated tentacle movements, which finally made the giant king crabs run away.

"How dare you chase away all the giant king crabs I gathered!"
"Hmph. We just need to sleep, right? Is there a rule against driving away gathered monsters?"
"No, there isn't."

The skeleton instructor admitted it readily.

"What now?"
"Let's just sleep."

Finally feeling safe, we closed our eyes and rested on the tree.
After about ten minutes with our eyes closed, the sound of rain tapping on leaves and pouring beyond the tree made for nice background music as sleep began to creep in.

Click-clack-click-clack.

"Geh-geh-geh. There's also no rule against gathering the king crabs that ran away!"
"...Oknodie. Is there a sound that drives away skeletons?"
"No such sound exists."

I survived the "Adventurer's Night Activities" lecture after much hardship.

If we had agreed with even a polite "us too," we might have ended up hanging from trees all night, so we kept our mouths shut and said nothing.

"Instructor, that was too much. Do you know how scared we were?"
"Geh-geh-geh. Only freshmen who overcome adversity can become sophomores. Thanks to today's lecture, you'll be able to sleep peacefully without sleep-talking no matter where you sleep at night."
"I think I'll have nightmares about this."

Titosoga didn't seem to find this lecture very useful. Zuang looked equally dissatisfied.

"Oh dear. That's too bad."

The skeleton instructor suddenly became dejected.

"I didn't want to give this kind of lecture."
"Don't tell me Professor Sadako ordered you to...?"

Zuang, still wary of Professor Sadako, asked cautiously.

"I only wanted to make students realize how uncomfortable their weak flesh shells are and guide them to become skeletons."
"Hieeeek!!"
"Y-you psycho!! You're insane!!"
"If you shed your flesh and become a skeleton, you won't have to suffer through unnecessary activities like sleeping. Why worry about sleep-talking? Geh-geh-geh."

The evil skeleton instructor revealed his true colors.

"Well? Do you feel like becoming a skeleton now? Just say the word, and I'll teach you the 'Skeletonization' spell anytime. It's a spell that will never let you return to being human! Geh-geh-geh!"
"Waaah!"
"Oknodie, let's attack together!"

Between the loudly laughing skeleton instructor, Titosoga who finally burst into tears, and Zuang who was hissing like an angry cat and ready to attack—
Just as chaos was about to erupt in the lecture hall, a magic circle suddenly appeared around the skeleton instructor.

"Ah, this is Professor Sadako's summoning magic circle? How frustrating. If I had known, I would have ended the lecture 10 minutes earlier and passed on the spell..."

The magic circle folded space like origami and swallowed the skeleton instructor.

"...Is he gone?"
"Yes."
"If I see that skull face instead of Professor Sadako at the next lecture, I'm running away immediately."

Zuang emphasized repeatedly that he was serious.
I'll keep it a secret that a summon's personality tends to resemble its master's.
