# 371 - Pandora's Box

The Foundation would never let Oknodie go free.
Especially if they knew her talent.
Zuang had been present both on the cruise ship and at the chairman's mansion.
She had even faced the chairman directly.
Seeing with her eyes and experiencing with her body.
All the data accumulated through experience told her this.

"The only reason I could think of for why he couldn't hide the existence of the letter box despite knowing about it was that. And judging by your reaction, it seems my guess was correct."
"Didn't Master Rung teach you this? Don't meddle in another organization's affairs and bring trouble upon yourself."

Jonna's killing intent intensified.
It wasn't sincere at all.
Just a reflexive action, like waving away an annoying mosquito buzzing around.
Even with that slight instinct alone, Zuang felt the invisible domain she had established being mercilessly crushed.

"...!"

This was the extent of his strength.
This was the caliber of man he was.
Even such a butler couldn't escape the embrace of the Foundation, the pressure of the chairman.
The Wiheomhae Foundation was a far more dangerous organization than everyone knew.
She had forgotten that fact when she was by Oknodie's side.

'How could I not forget?'

Honestly, it didn't feel real.
Because she only ever praised Jonna.
Especially after visiting him.
To the extent that any schoolgirl would have wished at least once to have a butler of her own.

"Oknodie always wears that whistle."
"......"
"She says she needs it to confirm she's connected to her butler. She never loses it and carries it on her body instead of putting it in her bag."
"......"
"Oknodie has innate qualities as an assassin, but she hasn't fully blossomed yet. Because she still has too many emotions."

Jonna acknowledged that Zuang was certainly no ordinary first-year student, as she tenaciously defended even just an inch of her domain while being suppressed.
Zuang's uniqueness was proven not only by her domain but also by the words coming from her mouth.

"If even this letter is part of that heartless chairman's plan, Oknodie would surely be deeply hurt. She would feel a tremendous sense of betrayal from the adult she trusted and relied on most."
"That's..."
"There's no use telling me. An assassin lets the night's work fade away in the shadows of dawn."

Zuang pushed Pandora's box, which he had been avoiding, back to him.

"You decide. Whether to burn this box and get rid of it. Or to make her open her eyes to true emotionlessness at the end of her betrayal."

He couldn't stop the small footsteps leaving the office while pushing back against his aura.
As always, he was weaker than anyone else in front of those small footsteps.

Coward.
