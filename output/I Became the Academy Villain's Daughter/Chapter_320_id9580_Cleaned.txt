# Enhanced Translation: The Trap of Superiority

━━━
Chairman's Mansion
41F - Floor of Wailing Ghosts
━━━

Following the cutting wind of the 39th floor, this floor was filled with wailing ghosts that brought physical destructive power.
Xing used his newfound enlightenment of equanimity to counter the telekinetic waves that struck unexpectedly from different directions with balanced force.

'This definitely helps with training. It would be extremely difficult to find a facility like this outside the Training Tower.'

Even the Academy's training rooms couldn't show this level of efficiency.
Creating virtual environments and immediately engaging in battles that would otherwise require risking one's life or actively searching for monsters in reality was advantageous.
However, the Academy's training rooms weren't designed for growth in such special environments.

'The Academy's training rooms are too kind.'

Even if it's just a mental virtual battle with illusions, training ends when mental strength falls below a certain percentage.
Even if you change the settings to experience something infinitely close to death, it's never directly connected to actual death.
Treatment facilities are nearby, and there are plenty of instructors or other students to help in emergencies.

'This place is different. If I die, even Oknodie a few floors above might not be able to recognize my crisis.'

I must calculate thoroughly.
If I take one more step forward, can I run to the exit without dying?
Should I retreat from the current floor now to avoid returning too late?
Will the increased difficulty from enduring one more minute cost me my life?
This naturally develops an objective eye for assessing one's abilities.

'That's why I need to calculate until the most precarious timing.'

After gaining the enlightenment of equanimity, Xing's combat ability improved noticeably.
The murderous sword strikes that had been wielded with uncontrolled anger, simply fast and strong, now had power distribution.
A sword wielded with cold reason, even when filled with killing intent, never loses the thread of rationality deep within.
By firmly grasping and following that thread, one can always regain reason and respond rationally.
Distance control.
Speed control.
Breath control.
Numerous adjustments made him stronger, but paradoxically, Xing realized his inadequacies through these adjustments.

'The wall of the 49th floor is too high.'

49th floor, the Floor of the Dead.
There, all elements from floors 41 through 48 manifest simultaneously.
To break through this floor where all undead-related themes converge requires the ability to thoroughly understand and overcome the elements of each floor.

"Those who don't lie and keep their promises receive credit from the world bit by bit. Inner energy, mana, divine power. Though different in kind, the amount of credit gradually increases."
"Keeping promises with the world isn't just about keeping your word."

Oknodie's Papa.
And the Chairman of the Foundation.
Jeil Wiheomhae had told him.
The way to become stronger.
Xing thought coldly and concluded.
Reaching the 50th floor of the tower would be more beneficial for growth than returning early to the Academy.
There was plenty of time.
Returning before the end of vacation would certainly be possible.
He realized how arrogant that was when he faced the wall of the 49th floor.

'Unless I develop the skill to endure at least 60 minutes on every floor, I can't break through the 49th floor.'

Even while increasing his challenge time daily as if fighting himself, there were routines Xing couldn't miss.
The magic watch he obtained from the Academy rang its alarm at the same time today.

[Lunch time]

When he went down to the dining hall on the 20th floor, Oknodie, who had already arrived, was sitting with a fork in one hand and a knife in the other.

"You always make sure to eat lunch even though you've received plenty of food."
"Free meals are too good to miss!"
"That's true."

The point pressure from buying food for every meal was no small matter.
Due to his high activity level, the 5p buffet meal wasn't enough for Xing, so he paid 10p for unlimited access every day, resulting in a fixed monthly food expense of at least 300p.
There were point expenditures on the cruise ship too, but he didn't participate in auctions.

'This is practically eating for free.'

Meals were also connected to what the Chairman occasionally mentioned—promises with the world, acts of gaining credit.
Even Xing, who wasn't picky about food, gradually realized the difference as he encountered various meals.

<Popcorn Potato>, a rare dish that weakens the components of demon realm potatoes that normally explode in your mouth, allowing you to enjoy their popping taste.
<Vegetable Mimic Meat>, a rare dish that utilizes the mimic's property of imitating objects, looking like vegetables but actually being meat.
After meals that were fun to eat and enjoy, he clearly felt changes in his abilities, such as his body becoming lighter or depleted strength being replenished.

"If you use up all your strength before coming here, such changes become even more noticeable. Only those who are diligent with themselves can notice their own changes."

The time spent at the Foundation wasn't as terrible or painful as he had thought.
Each day was a succession of growth.
Xing honestly began to enjoy this time.
He could feel himself getting stronger every moment.
Every day he took one step closer to revenge.
After a month of continuous training, even the Chairman, who had once seemed so frightening, could now be faced without trembling.

"Functions have special secrets. Even the same function can develop different higher functions depending on how you gain experience. Take swordsmanship, for example. Xing, how did your higher swordsmanship function manifest?"
"Eastern Swordsmanship manifested."
"Oknodie?"
"I haven't unlocked it yet! I chose <Sword Dancing> as a trait enhancement."
"Hehe. That's also a kind of difference. Those who try to incorporate higher enlightenment into swordsmanship acquire higher swordsmanship, while those who try to implement special abilities have their traits enhanced. So if you want to know someone, investigate the functions they've learned."

Because you can glimpse the life they've lived and the goals they'll pursue.
At the Chairman's words, Xing asked a question at the dining table for the first time.

"Chairman. What is your higher swordsmanship function?"

The Chairman answered playfully.

"That's a secret."
"..."

Sometimes he acts like a sage who has awakened to the world's secrets, but at times like this, he seems no different from a child like Oknodie.
Sometimes like an adult, sometimes like a child; sometimes like a good person, sometimes like a villain.
The Chairman that Xing had observed directly was difficult to define.

"Oknodie. Don't you already have the skills to conquer the 49th floor?"
"Shh. That's a secret!"
"Why don't you want to return to the Academy?"
"Well, my food encyclopedia is filling up at an incredible speed! Even if I go back to the Academy, the events I'll experience are all the same, so there's no need to rush. I already completed the affection encyclopedia on the cruise ship!"
"Didn't you promise to join Jigoku's pirate games during vacation?"
"We agreed that saving him on the cruise ship made us even!"

...Well, it should be fine.
The desire to become stronger is the same.
The only difference is that Xing's means is the Training Tower, while Oknodie's is high-grade cooking.
Even so, with a month and a half gone out of the two-month vacation, it was time to see the end.
They would have to return before the second semester began.

━━━
Chairman's Mansion
49F - Floor of the Dead
━━━

A floor that even the hero Ishtar ultimately couldn't overcome.
In the trial of this floor, which was like an undead theme park where themes from floors 41 to 48 all appeared, Xing realized what was most troublesome.
Any kind of attack allowed would accumulate curses.
Even if not allowed, it was the same.
With each passing minute, curses like those from the 45th Floor of Curses randomly accumulated at a rate of 1 per minute.
Of course, extremely vicious curses don't apply from the start.
Before being afflicted with <Blind> and losing sight, first comes <Lesser Vision Curse> that makes the eyes cloudy.
Even if blindness comes suddenly, the phenomenon is limited to one eye.
Other curses are similar.
Before <Right Hand Seal> arrives, it starts with <Hand Trembling> and curses steadily pile up and overlap.
However, the 1000m, just 1km of space that must be traversed to break through the 49th floor, is not so easy.
As you break through the gimmicks that fill the interior one by one, time is gradually consumed, and eventually you have to self-diagnose.

This is as far as I go.
If I don't turn back now, the curses will worsen uncontrollably.
If I push too hard, I'll die.
If I'm unlucky, I'll die.
The risk to be taken is too great.

The warning from the body cannot be ignored, and eventually reason pulls the reins and turns back.

'Not today.'

If there are days with bad luck, there are also days with good luck.
Days when curses don't overlap in the same area and are limited to minor curses across all directions.
Today, going far wasn't pushing it.
Both the zombie attack pattern and the wailing ghost pattern overlapped in direction, making them easy to overcome.
I can break through.
He had such an intuitive certainty.

'If everyone has an optimal day, there's a possibility to break through the 49th floor. One chance granted only to those with high average stats who have thoroughly eliminated weaknesses rather than developing just one strength. Such a day has come.'

Ishtar far surpasses Xing in overall combat ability.
However, she had something, one thing critically lacking to overcome the 49th floor.
Just as Xing had been blocked by his own power due to a lack of <Equanimity> on the 39th floor.

'In this moment, I stand above the hero. It's only in this highly limited moment, with considerable luck, but that fact doesn't change.'

If he proceeds, he can continue to hold this sense of superiority.
If he returns to the Academy and boasts about this fact, Ishtar would feel inferior.
The fact that someone weaker than her accomplished what she couldn't.
She would regret returning to the Academy a month early as foolish.
Conversely, Xing could cherish his month of special training as worthwhile.
But his feet wouldn't move.
He didn't feel like moving forward.

"Why didn't you go?"

Xing finally returned to the starting point of the 49th floor.
Oknodie, who had been watching from behind, handed him a towel to wipe his sweat and asked.
Looking at that innocent face, he finally understood.
Xing instinctively felt it.
Seeing Oknodie's nonchalant attitude.

"You. You don't plan to return to the Academy right away even when the second semester starts, do you?"

If he achieves his goal and leaves now, Oknodie will be left alone.
Just as he feels superior for surpassing the hero's month with his own month of special training, Oknodie might spend a much longer time here.
He felt such a vague anxiety.

"Ah! You caught me."

Oknodie's heart had begun to drift away from the Academy.
