The Chairman's Mansion
1F - Entrance Cut Floor
━━━

Isabel spoke while looking at the ominously foreboding sign.

"It's just like an archaeological site."
"Similar indeed. The first floor exists to repel those who haven't been formally invited or those who infiltrate with the purpose of trespassing, as they occasionally appear at the mansion."

The butler inserted a card into a rectangular groove in the wall, and sounds of devices shutting down and traps being deactivated echoed throughout the long corridor.

"Of course, you all are formally invited guests, so you can rest assured."

The corridor was quite long.
It would take a full 10 minutes to walk through.
The students remained vigilant, sensing that climbing the floors would be no easy task.

━━━
The Chairman's Mansion
2F - Floor of Wooden Dolls
━━━

Upon climbing the stairs and opening the door, wooden dolls similar to those found in training facilities for Knight Department aspirants appeared.
Inside the second floor, which resembled an actual training ground, wooden dolls made creaking sounds as their joints moved, wandering around with various weapons.

"Passing through here is simple. Defeat all the attacking wooden dolls and reach the opposite side of the passage to climb the stairs. I'll clear the way ahead, so follow me."

Oknodie watched as the butler she called "Incompetent Butler" began walking as if out for a casual stroll.

'That skill level is incompetent?'

Of course, that was just Oknodie's thought.
To the other students, the skills of butlers other than Jonna seemed quite impressive.
The scarecrows, armed with everything from short swords to long spears, approached the butler with their distinctive jerky movements and swung their weapons.
From behind, it was a terrifying sight that made one's heart race, but the butler easily deflected the weapons with light taps.
The distance between the students and the incompetent butler gradually increased.
It was no time to just stand and watch.

"Aren't you following?"

At Oknodie's urging, they snapped back to reality and followed, which helped ease the students' tension a bit.

"I didn't think about it because the butler was deflecting them barehanded, but we can fight with weapons too."
"Right?"
"Since there are many of us, we don't have to handle multiple opponents at once—just one per person."
"Besides, aren't these weak?"
"They're easier than I thought."

Their lightheartedness was short-lived.
From somewhere in the corridor came the sound of [Level Up].
Suddenly, the scarecrows' attacks became heavier.
The attack intensity was different.
The joint movements became smoother.
Everyone sensed the change and had a foreboding feeling about what was to come.

"Are they... getting stronger bit by bit?"
"Probably? Don't you think so?"
"Why don't you know? It's your house."

In response to Ishtar's grumbling, Oknodie answered cheerfully.

"It's my first time visiting Papa too! If I had known he lived in such a fun house, I would have come to play much sooner! What a shame!"
"Ishtar..."
"I-I didn't know. So I can ask, right? Don't look at me like I'm trash..."

While Ishtar floundered after being unintentionally treated like garbage, the butler leading the way continued at a sluggish pace that irritated the students.
Unable to bear it any longer, Son O-cheon urged him from behind.

"Can't you go a little faster?"
"You seem to have forgotten. I am a guide and butler. The mansion's butlers have a duty to test visitors' capabilities. In addition to the floor's test, you could say you're simultaneously undergoing the butler's test to confirm whether you possess adequate skills."

This meant that while they could easily pass through before the difficulty increased if they moved quickly in a straight line, the guide was deliberately allowing the difficulty to rise to a certain level.
Even so, the students felt they had more than enough capacity to not only endure but also throw around the attacking wooden dolls.

"I have a question. Can we take their weapons?"
"Why don't you try?"

Without hesitation, Hestia snatched a greatsword from one of the wooden dolls.
The color of the disarmed doll suddenly turned bright red.

Bang!

Surprised by the wooden doll charging at her at tremendous speed, Hestia swung her hammer.
With a loud bang, the doll shattered.
The impact sound that made one's ears ring as the doll shattered made it seem like it was made of metal rather than wood.

"As you can see, dolls that have their weapons taken trigger a berserk mechanism."
"What about ownership of the seized weapon?"
"You may keep it if you wish. If you can handle other mechanisms, that is."

Hestia thought for a moment before putting down the greatsword.

"Why did you put it down?"
"We're invited as Oknodie's friends. If invited guests steal things from the mansion, the household members won't just let it slide."

At Hestia's sensible point, students who had secretly thought they could take items by force felt pangs of conscience.
Breaking into someone's house, stealing household items, and boldly walking out with them was neither friendly nor guest-like behavior.
Hearing Hestia's words, Arcadia and Gisele, who had been sneaking glances, hurriedly placed their stolen weapons on the floor.

"How many wooden dolls did you defeat in that short time?"
"To be precise, I first destroyed the fallen wooden dolls and then took their weapons."

Gisele revealed his business method as he dusted off his hands.
Seeing the changing atmosphere, the hero also put down the weapon he had sneaked away.

"You have a cheat weapon like a holy sword, yet you still want other people's weapons?"
"...I was just collecting them as enhancement materials. I thought it might work."

Ishtar, remembering that the enhancement conditions for the Light Sword were more generous than expected, had hoped that stealing the iron swords here might be useful for holy sword enhancement.
Of course, even though RPG heroes typically break into people's homes, steal items, and justify it as requisitioning for world peace, taking things from a friend's house was crossing a line.
Having set aside their greed, the group only defeated the attacking wooden dolls. By the end, the wooden dolls had become strong enough to exchange blows or cause annoyance when approached.

"I wonder how much stronger they'd get after another 10 minutes. I'm curious, though I don't want to experience it."

Setting aside the possibility that was half curiosity and half fear, everyone continued climbing the floors.
After the wooden dolls came arrow dodging.
They had to walk at an excruciatingly slow pace through increasingly fast-flying arrows.
After arrow dodging came a trap room where the floor suddenly opened, connecting to the floor below.
As time passed, the speed at which traps opened increased, and the number of open sections grew.

"This is kind of fun?"
"Good training too."

A mansion where climbing floors was so entertaining that they thought it might be nice to visit occasionally!
But as time passed, even the advanced class students began to adapt to this unfamiliar environment.
And they started to gauge their capabilities.

"It's not that difficult to endure for the time the butler requires?"
"I wonder how long we could last if we really tried to hold out?"

The challenge-seeking nature of veterans, the competitive spirit of the strong was stimulated by this structure!
Jigoku was the first to get fired up.

The Chairman's Mansion
9F - Target Floor
━━━

"This is the target floor. Targets gradually approach from a distance, and if you allow them to get too close, they automatically explode. Equipment needed for shooting can be borrowed at the floor entrance and returned at the exit."

For Jigoku, a genuine trigger-happy suffering from chronic trigger-pulling syndrome, this was an irresistible temptation!

Bang bang bang!

As soon as he heard the butler's words, Jigoku grabbed a gun and began blasting the approaching targets with frightening intensity.
His speed and accuracy were so fast that even the butler who was about to take the lead was taken aback.

"Shooting. Shooting! Endless ammunition and infinite targets! Shooting! Gyahaha! This place is absolute heaven!"

Even after reaching the exit, Jigoku remained at the rear while everyone else passed through.
Unable to bear it any longer, the butler spoke up.

"How long do you plan to just shoot there? Shall we leave you behind?"
"I'm going to live here. From today, this is my home."

Gisele expressed genuine concern.

"Have you perhaps fallen victim to some hidden trap of the foundation and suffered mental contamination?"
"He just wanted to shoot guns! I understand your excitement at finding an opportunity to gain lots of skill experience, but please bear with it for now. Papa might be waiting for us in the dining room!"
"Ugh. If it's your request, Oknodie, I have no choice... but promise me we can come back later."
"Butler sir, is that okay?"
"Sigh. It's possible after the meal. The only duty and directive given to guests is to attend the dining table. That's all I know."

Jigoku followed with dragging steps, like a child being pulled away from the toy section by his mother.
Given their physical appearances, it should have been the other way around, but he was being dragged by the childlike Oknodie.

"Hehe. Oknodie seems more like the adult."
"If you're getting that kind of comment from Titoso, that says it all."
"What? Zuang, what's wrong with hearing that from me?"
"It feels like having my shoe bitten by a harmless squirrel?"
"Grrrr. I'll bite you!?"
"But can this really be called a home? To me... it looks more like a soldier training facility."

The warm atmosphere was short-lived.
With one cold remark from Northern Grand Duchess Irene, as frigid as the northern winter, the mood turned icy.
