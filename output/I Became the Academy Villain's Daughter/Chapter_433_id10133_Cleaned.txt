Unlike the bold challenges at the beginning, attempts after 15 minutes became extremely hesitant, with barely one person trying every three minutes.

"This is cheating."
"There's no rule against cheating."
"Tee-hee. Stupid♡ Loser♡ Why do you even have that thing on your neck~? If the second-years collude, we can collude too♡"
"Oh, that's a method!"
"As expected of our brilliant princess!"

Mesugakki found a breakthrough.
One student from the Imperial faction cautiously stepped up to challenge.

"I'd like to challenge Senior Lu of the White Holy Knight to a knowledge contest. The question is: What is the signature dish of the Raclette family and what is the origin of its preparation method?"

Senior Lu seemed somewhat taken aback, as if he hadn't expected such a question.

"Hmm. If it's neither a battle of strength nor intellect but simply a contest of knowledge, then victory is determined by who knows the answer. You've thought this through well."
"You don't know, do you? Then it's my turn to answer!"
"No. I never said I didn't know. And your priority is the right to choose your opponent and the type of challenge. Not the right to answer first. Remember that."
"!!"
"Mandela Castella, daughter of the Marquis. How much do you know about the origins of noble family cuisines?"

Mandela, with her ever-present smile, showed an insurmountable wall as she answered.

"I know everything from the dishes of the Empire's three great meritorious families to those of minor houses in the border countries. Despite appearances, I received early education in heraldry. I know all the emblems that contain the origins, symbols, and signature dishes of each family. There is no dish unknown to me!"
"T-that's a lie! Our family isn't even that prestigious!"
"The Raclette Baronial Family. In an environment contaminated with demonic toxins, it became impossible to hunt and eat prey on the spot. Feeling the need to create preserved food locally, they made cheese from demon realm cattle."
"Y-you really do know?!"
"Purifying this cheese each time consumed mana, but the head of the Raclette family had the groundbreaking idea of cutting the cheese with a holy sword, saving mana used in purification magic and helping with local supplies."
"No, stop it!"
"This purified cheese was placed on other toxin-filled meat, and the method was named 'Raclette.' My tutor kindly taught me this as a case study of a mere swordsman's meteoric rise!"

Despite the tearful expression of Lady Raclette, the White Holy Knight Lu mercilessly picked up where Mandela left off.

"That's correct."
"Correct answer. Lady Raclette, please return to your seat."

At Professor Mahabharata's declaration, Raclette hung her head and returned to her seat.
Subsequent attempts to showcase family specialties were repeatedly thwarted.

"Guess how many dolls are on the bed in my room♡"
"Questions that I, as the judge, cannot know the answer to are not permitted."

Even Princess Mesugakki couldn't easily overcome this obstacle!

"Hmph~ Then this should work♡ I'll write the answer on this paper and submit it to the teacher, so the teacher will know the answer~? If Senior Lu can steal the answer without touching it, it's his victory♡"
"There's no issue with the rules, but such a trick requires the opponent's consent. Do you accept this challenge?"

The White Holy Knight Lu, though reluctant, ultimately accepted the challenge.

"The divine would want me to face difficult battles for the sake of educating juniors. Though not an ideal contest, I will participate."

Lu engaged in the challenge, using his search eye to stare intently at the reverse side of the paper.

"...Hmm."

Lu glanced around at the other second-years.

"Hmm."
"Ahem."
"Ahem-hem."

The second-years cleared their throats, looking around to see if anyone knew the answer.
All eyes turned to Mandela Castella, as children seek their parents in times of trouble, and Lady Mandela answered with a bright smile.

"I have no idea!"

Lu immediately declared surrender.

"...I admit defeat."
"You gave up too quickly♡ That's how you fail to satisfy a woman, Senior~"
"You say that after using specially made paper that blocks all magical vision."

The appearance of paper that frustrated all the second-years finally overcame their collective intelligence.

"Don't strain your brains, you loser followers. Just write your answers on this paper and pass♡ But you can only target Senior Lu♡"
"Wooow!"
"I'll be loyal to you forever, Princess!"
"We admire you, Princess!"

After helping her faction pass, Mesugakki waved the paper that was essentially a cheat key for passing.

"How foolish. If all the second-years were blocked, she could have earned more points by targeting other seniors."

In response to Isabel's envious criticism, Gisele shook his head.

"That's uncertain. The White Holy Knight Lu serves the divine Immanuel. He has restrictions against committing unjust acts. Other second-years might discover the answer through methods Lu cannot use. Since Lu was the opponent this time, others might have techniques they couldn't use despite being able to win."
"Either way, when a path opens, relying on connections and passing through established methods is the most certain and safe approach. It's underhanded, but I suppose it's one way to do it."

Oknodie tilted her head at Isabel's grumbling.

"It's not really that much of a cheat key though? We can learn mana coating from Professor Bronze in the first semester."
"Then can you make one for us?"
"Do we really need to? There's an easier way."

Oknodie winked one eye.

"!"
"The little lady seems to have a plan. Let's wait and see."

<Holy Mirror>
<108 Gates as One>

<Single Strike Technique - Heart Spear>

<Mana Arrows>

<108 Gates as One>

"Take down at least one of them!"
