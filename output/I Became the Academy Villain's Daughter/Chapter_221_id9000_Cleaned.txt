<221 - How to Save Her>

"I'll be going now."

As Arcadia turned around with grace and dignity, those who were once her followers felt an inexplicable anxiety.
They had believed that when her family fell, Arcadia's influence would end too, and that they could gather fringe forces like her and replace her as number one.
Contrary to their expectations of the glamorous lady's downfall, Arcadia was enduring with remarkable composure.

"It's just temporary anyway."
"Could she really remain so composed if her family truly collapsed?"
"If she doesn't find allies, the young lady is finished."

While guiding Arcadia, Jigoku realized that Gisele's request to escort and protect her wasn't entirely unfounded.
If no one had been there to protect her, those ungrateful blonde beasts might have tried to humiliate her by force.

"As expected of a Sebiche oddball, she's got guts."
"...I'm glad it appeared that way."

A trembling voice. Unsteady footsteps.
Jigoku, who had been walking ahead, scratched his head, regretting his thoughtless comment.
Not everyone has a strong mentality like Oknodie.
Even those who want to appear strong aren't necessarily truly strong.
She was simply trying her best.
Jigoku didn't speak to Arcadia anymore.
He didn't look back either.
On the way to Gisele.
Whatever expression Arcadia wore, whatever sorrow she suppressed, that time belonged to her alone, unobserved by anyone.
This was the minimum respect Jigoku showed to Arcadia, who, despite being born into an enemy family, didn't turn away from her family's karma.

"Here you go. Escort request completed. I've done your favor, so we're even on the ammunition supply issue."
"Thank you for your hard work, Mr. Jigoku. I won't bother you again about this matter. If you're willing, for additional supplies, the next request..."
"No need."
"I see. Then you may leave."

He had shown proper respect. Anything more would be excessive. After all, Arcadia was neither his friend nor anything else.
After bringing Arcadia to Gisele, Jigoku turned away without hesitation.
After confirming Jigoku's departure, Gisele locked the door, took careful security measures, and only then spoke.

"Lady Arcadia. Are you aware of the current situation?"
"I know that my family's disgrace has been exposed to the world, and we're facing a crisis of extinction."
"If you don't respond immediately, you'll be caught in the power struggle of competitors trying to eliminate even one rival, and you'll be expelled from the Academy. I've thought of a solution."
"Please tell me."
"The most critical issue now is the slave trading ledger containing House Sebiche's disgrace. The ledger is currently owned by Professor Destroyer's guild. Stealing it is virtually impossible."
"An Academy professor...?"

It was unbelievable.
Wasn't the Academy supposed to protect its students?
The rules of the outside world should mean nothing before the Dragon Headmaster's stubbornness.

"The headmaster will maintain his policy of protecting students. But I can't say all the professors below him share the same mindset."
"I have no intention of leaving the Academy. And if the Academy doesn't expel me, I won't leave."
"But if dishonorable rumors persist to the point where Lady Arcadia cannot endure at the Academy, and public humiliation continues, could you still hold on then?"
"That's..."
"Don't waver. Neither the foundation nor the Academy cares about your well-being. If you don't pull yourself together, not only will you leave the Academy, but you might also struggle to preserve your life."

Arcadia's eyes filled with determination at the harsh reality Gisele awakened her to.

"I heard from Titosoga that rumors about the slave ledger are circulating. How can we deal with that ledger?"
"We must purchase it. Both the Academy and Professor Destroyer are open to negotiation. If you have enough points, a deal is certainly possible."

Gisele was rather confident.
A deal was exactly what Professor Destroyer wanted.
Otherwise, he wouldn't have leaked information so freely that even students were hearing rumors.
This is a test.
Would she tremble in fear and leave the Academy of her own accord?
Or would she overcome her family's disgrace and make a deal to help herself?

"A ledger containing my family's disgrace... Is it really possible to purchase something that might cost an unknown amount of points?"
"By giving up your shares in the Dark Market, I can support you with 200,000 points, half of the public fund."
"I'll give them up. Those shares mean nothing if I can't survive this crisis anyway."
"Then... by my calculations, you'll need at least 300,000 more points. I estimate the ledger's value at around 500,000 points."
"How much time do we have?"
"Twelve hours at most."
"...I can't think of a way."
"There are two methods that won't take twelve hours—not even one hour."

Gisele looked straight into the young lady's eyes and said:

"Collect donations from your followers. This is the easy path."
"...That's impossible. If it were yesterday, I would have confidently chosen that path, but they were just clinging to my family's influence, not to me."
"Then we have no choice but to take the difficult path. Sell information about House Sebiche members' hiding place to Professor Destroyer."
"Are you asking me to betray my family?"
"That expression is incorrect."

Now is not the time to talk about betrayal.

"From the moment House Sebiche engaged in slave trading prohibited by Imperial law, they could be branded as 'Mine.' Don't miss the opportunity to draw a line between yourself and such forces ahead of time."

It's a false accusation.
She knows it.
Even if the family elders were money-obsessed misers who neglected the people's welfare, they weren't desperate enough to sell their souls to demons.
She also knows:
Though that's the truth, no one will believe it.
The Imperial faction and the Fringe faction.
The delicate balance within the Academy foreshadows the balance of power among world powers after the 981st class graduates.
They can eliminate one axis—the possibility of House Sebiche's revival strengthening the Fringe faction's cohesion.
The Empire won't miss this opportunity.
Neither will the opportunists from the Fringe.
A golden opportunity has appeared to completely destroy the center of the Fringe forces, firmly established by Grand Duke Andersen and Lady Arcadia.
Many ambitious individuals are ready to stab her in the back without hesitation.

'I didn't realize the situation was this precarious.'

One wrong step could send her life plummeting into the abyss.
She could end up alive but not truly living.
The deal Gisele proposed.
This is the lesser evil.
A measure to avoid the worst-case scenario.
It's the only way Arcadia might preserve her life and remain at the Academy.

"If there truly is no other way..."

There's no choice.
She must endure the humiliation and accept it.
As she was about to agree while suppressing her sorrow, a clear voice unsuited to this place was heard.

"Hmm~ So that's the problem?"

Oknodie.
The voice of the audacious girl came from a nearby table.

"Little lady? Since when, no, how did you get here...?"

She had definitely secured the room to prevent eavesdropping.
Ignoring Gisele's bewildered gaze, Oknodie sat at the table, resting her chin on both hands, and met Arcadia and Gisele's eyes.

"Just now. But more importantly, you don't have to do that."

A small yet bright face.
A mischievous glint appeared on that little rascal's face.

"If we can just steal the House Sebiche slave trading ledger from the thief guild that Professor Destroyer employs, we won't need to buy it with points, right?"
"Oknodie. Surely you're not..."
"Yep. Leave it to me!"

She volunteered to take on the most dangerous task.
It was incomprehensible.

"I understand why Gisele would help me. He's my business partner. If my reputation is ruined, the Dark Market is essentially finished too."
"I agree. Please refrain from such reckless talk, little lady."
"Cute D. You don't have that kind of contractual relationship with me. Why would you take such a huge risk?"

Oknodie hopped down from the table.

"We don't have a contract. But it's about me and Arcadia. Doesn't it hurt your feelings when you say it like that?"

The child's eyes showed a pure heart.

"I can help Arcadia without getting anything in return. Helping Arcadia is an event more precious than the weekly event!"

Arcadia embraced the girl before her tightly.
She couldn't forgive herself for doubting this small child's pure heart.

'Oknodie is different.'

She made a decision.
She must repay this child's purity.

"Mr. Gisele, you presented me with two options. Actually, there's a way to purchase the ledger without stealing it. We can steal from somewhere else."
"Where do you intend to steal from?"
"There's a secret warehouse where House Sebiche has hidden assets for emergencies."
"Lady Arcadia. Those assets...!"
"I know, Gisele. They could serve as capital to help rebuild House Sebiche once this crisis passes."

Nevertheless, she had decided.

"Even if it means giving up the chance to restore my family's name and a wealthy future, I will not put this pure child, Oknodie, who wants to help me, in danger."

Instead of the ledger from the thief guild that could endanger Oknodie, she would steal House Sebiche's secret funds.
