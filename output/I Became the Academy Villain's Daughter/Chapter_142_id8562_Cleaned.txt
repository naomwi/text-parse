# 142 - Lower Class Mana Verification Exam

The Mana Verification Exam is divided into five categories.

Knight Department - Crossing the Bridge.
Magic Department - Destroying the Scarecrow.
Administration Department - Resolving Petitions.
Production Department - Object Structure Analysis.
Adventure Department - Finding the Certificate of Acceptance.

Lower class students can attempt two of these categories, and passing just one is enough to pass the Mana Verification Exam.
Of course, Professor Van Snake, who believes that failing as many first-year students as possible is actually doing them a favor, made sure that not a single test would be easy to pass.

"Knight Department applicants, come this way."

They were so tall that if you tried to climb over and failed the landing, you'd break your limbs or hit your head on the ground—perfect for instant death.
Unless you could demonstrate superhuman combat ability using mana, this was a test that would be difficult to pass without an extremely well-trained body.

When striking a light object with a weapon, the impact is fully transferred to the object, so there's no recoil.
However, when hitting something immovable and hard like a wall or the ground, all the impact returns to the attacker, making fingertips tingle and weapons vibrate.
The student who struck the massive boulder was experiencing exactly this kind of shock.

Mob had naturally adapted to the power of his high stats through days of weight training.
Even someone with a stat of 20 doesn't always exert 100% of their strength 20 ability.
In normal daily life, 10% of strength is sufficient, and in training, one typically exerts 30% on average.
In actual combat, one squeezes out 50% of their strength and can consciously exert up to 70% when needed.
However, drawing out 100% of one's strength is difficult unless one's life is in imminent danger.

'Hehe. It was worth it.'

He had worried that Mob might remove the weights and relax when sleeping or going about daily life, away from his sight, but it was an unnecessary concern.
Mob's strike was clearly close to 90%, if not 100% of his maximum.
As his body became accustomed to exerting strong force and enduring the weight, strength came naturally.

"Huah!"

The subsequent series of strikes continued to produce loud noises as the spear collided with the boulder.
He was drawing out exactly the necessary force at the necessary timing, and enduring the recoil with his increased health from the stat boost.

"Yah!"

The concentrated strikes that didn't miss made the boulder shudder.
The momentum that wasn't fully dissipated with one shudder grew larger with the second and third impacts.
As the boulder teetered precariously, almost rolling but not quite, he realized this was his chance.

"Release the weights on my arms!"

Mob immediately removed the weights from his elbows, shoulders, and wrists.
In that brief moment, the boulder's movement decreased significantly, but Mob's right arm became much lighter than before.

"Tahaaah!"

With a powerful battle cry, his thrust finally lifted the house-sized boulder, raising its flat bottom surface until it rolled over the bridge with a thud.
