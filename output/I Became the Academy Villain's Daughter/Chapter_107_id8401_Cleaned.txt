"Aaaah!"

What are you looking at, you obese lizard?

And blew out a puff of air.

"Ugyagyagak!"

-You seem to have a lot of discontent in your brow. Shall I smooth it out for you?
"No!!"

-There are several facilities on campus that are difficult for first-years to handle.
-If you wander around too carelessly, serious accidents could occur, so don't follow along when you see unfamiliar facilities.

"What's the 0.1%?"

The Principal was nonchalant, but the students were surprised.
Garden of the Gods.
Divine space.
Sacred sanctuary.
In these places, which you can reach by receiving a god's call or by chance, you can receive a god's voice, favor, divine objects, or powers.

Even players can enter with a very rare probability.

'But not all gods are benevolent gods.'

As a player, I remained detached knowing that being called to a divine sanctuary isn't necessarily a good thing, but the other students were fired up as if their hearts were ablaze.

-That's enough preaching.
-Today's "Principal's Teachings" lecture will teach you how to cook monsters.

The Principal has his own educational philosophy.
If you create a disaster event, you should also provide a solution!

But he underestimated the 981st class students.
The students who had already grilled, steamed, stir-fried, and tasted Giant King Crab in all sorts of ways were bewildered.

"Principal. We've already had a full meal with Giant King Crab."

"Pathetic crab shell♡ Just a big-sized delicacy♡ Become crab meat salad and get eaten up♡"

At this, a subtle expectation appeared on all the students' faces.
Even the Eastern Swordsman Xing, Northern Grand Duchess Irene, and Fusion Experiment Kasia, who would rank 1st, 2nd, and 3rd in taciturnity, showed expectation on their faces.
Will today's lecture end early?

"Did you really have to go that far with the lecture..."

Is there some seafood that washed ashore along with the crabs?
