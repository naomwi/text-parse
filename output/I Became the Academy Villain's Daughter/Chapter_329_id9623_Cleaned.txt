# Enhanced Translation: Chapter 329 - Good Intentions That Kill

Butterflies flutter down onto the flower bed.
The sunlight is warm and the breeze is cool.
The desserts are soft and the coffee is bitter.

"What a lovely day, isn't it?"

Oknodie, who had been squirming in my arms eating cookies, said with a bright smile.

"Liar."

Oknodie's smiling face transformed into a cold, expressionless mask.
No.
I'm not a liar!

"Huh...!"

Arcadia bolted upright, her upper body drenched in sweat.
She didn't feel relieved that it was just a nightmare.
This was a dream born from reality.
For her, reality itself was an unending nightmare.
Even as she stood under the shower's downpour, the sticky sweat might wash away, but her sticky feelings would not.

-Arcadia doesn't have 100 affection, does she?
-She doesn't have 100 affection.
-Liar.

Voices echoing in her mind.
She knew the reason.
Oknodie, who had given her so much.
Oknodie had seen through her true feelings—that she couldn't purely accept Oknodie's kindness and harbored a trace of fear and anxiety.
Isn't there a saying that children are sensitive to emotions?
Nothing could be more shameful and painful than having her bare face exposed like this.

"Pull yourself together, Arcadia..."

She had already apologized at the very moment she heard those shocking words from Oknodie.
Yet Oknodie's distrust hadn't disappeared.
It meant her distrust wasn't light enough to vanish with a single apology.

"It's not too late yet."

Oknodie had only said she couldn't fully trust her, not that she had completely rejected her.

-If it's not 100, how much do you think it is?
-80!

Honestly, she didn't think that was wrong.
No matter how hard she tried not to be conscious of it, she still had lingering uneasiness.
Oknodie's connection to the Foundation.
The danger posed by the Foundation's chairman, Oknodie's Papa.
Oknodie's occasionally skewed common sense.
If her fear and aversion toward Oknodie was 20, then her affection and fondness was 80.

"Distrust will disappear with time and effort. If no one else, at least you should trust me."

That was the duty of being human.
Even if Oknodie were to walk a crooked path, she would set her straight.
Just as calluses form after hard training, determination hardened in Arcadia's heart.

Looking at the gaps scattered throughout her course schedule, she felt something was missing.

The Wiheomhae Foundation had raided the Beast-Human Revival Society!

Such a chance wouldn't come easily even in higher grades.
Most students think the Academy is just about attending lectures diligently, but each semester, the chapter boss's scheming or rampage turns the Academy upside down.
At the very least, there's guaranteed to be tremendous damage within the same grade.

'I should earn as many credits as possible while I can!'

I can help with the assignments.

I should also suggest this to the friends who came with me on the boat trip to Papa's house.
They'll all be delighted for sure.
