# Graduating from the Academy by Luck - Chapter 421: Baby, I'm Here

The Plant Club president Orchatch and his three members had fallen into a terrible trap.

"Even if the spell is difficult, we can solve it if we just spend enough time!"
"President. The three of us will help. Let's not give up! To be honest, you brought us here, so how can you talk about giving up now?"
"Oknodie must be tied up dealing with the ambush from the other third-years, right?"

The club members still hadn't grasped the situation.
Orchatch reminded them of a very sad fact.

"What if the mandrake wakes up while we're trying to dispel the magic?"
"Oh."
"That's..."
"We can just put it back to sleep!"
"You know that once a status effect is removed, resistance increases for a certain period, right?"

Let's say they could put it back to sleep once.
But what about twice? Three times?
The effort required to inflict status effects increases exponentially, while the duration decreases.
This meant they couldn't conveniently keep the mandrake subdued forever.

"Let's just dunk it completely?"
"Then do you think Oknodie would let us live?"
"..."
"Even before Oknodie, I couldn't allow that. How could I kill a mandrake that I captured alive?"
"But we can't die here either. I'm scared of what Oknodie might do to us if we're caught trapped here, President!"

Dispelling the magic surrounding the private room would take a long time, but if the mandrake woke up during that process, they might all die from the genuinely angry mandrake's scream.
If they focused more on subduing the mandrake, they wouldn't have enough time to dispel the magic.
To make matters worse, another pessimistic outlook emerged.

*Twitch.*

"What the hell? Why is it trying to wake up already?"

Even though the president had personally applied so many status effects, it was already trying to wake up.
Orchatch could immediately guess the reason.

"This is insane... its status effect resistance is ridiculously high."

Observing Eye. Pierce Eye.
The advanced technique of Insight that was used to spy on the true nature hidden by a living being's natural mana revealed the mandrake's cruel specs.

━━━━
Burn Resistance 45
Paralysis Resistance 275
Sleep Resistance 280
Freeze Resistance 177
Confusion Resistance 95
Weakness Resistance 111
━━━━

These were specs that couldn't naturally exist.
This was clear evidence of training.


* * *


Alice, who had trapped her enemies in the room by pretending to scream and making even the countdown fake, was feeling quite satisfied now.

'All that effort of playing nanny and applying status effects whenever Baby tried to walk out of the private room has paid off.'

No matter how much it wanted to go outside and play, the Academy was too dangerous for Baby.
A passing senior might snatch it up to eat for health benefits, or a professor might lock it away in a laboratory thinking it was interesting.
So every time it tried to escape through the door or window, she prevented Baby's escape attempts by applying status effects like <Paralysis>, <Sleep>, and <Freeze>.

'Because of that, it developed the strange habit of trying to escape by digging through walls with its roots... but I guess it's fortunate since that strengthened its physical power?'

The walls were enchanted to automatically restore themselves over time, so she just left it alone.
When Baby later realized this and tried to escape through windows or doors during Oknodie's lecture hours, Alice would subdue it again with status effects.
While its resistance grew steadily, Alice's skill at applying status effects improved just as much.
With both the caster and the resistor growing equally, Baby's escape attempts were doomed to fail.
Eventually, Baby seemed to conclude that the only remaining method was to physically defeat Oknodie and escape, so it diligently practiced the fighting techniques it learned from Hestia.

'Your effort is commendable, but at that growth rate, escape is impossible in your lifetime.'

Baby's efforts were utterly meaningless for its original purpose of escape.
However, they proved remarkably effective in the unexpected situation of fending off the Plant Club's attack.

"Waaah!"

When Baby finally broke free from the status effects and started rampaging, the Plant Club members changed their strategy. Rather than subduing it by force and risking Oknodie's wrath if the mandrake got more injured, they decided to put up defensive magic and endure until it ran out of energy.
But thanks to its excellent training results, Baby's roots shattered all their vine shields, bark shields, and wood skin magic one after another.

'I guess its functional training is achieving greater results because the opponents are strong.'

Seeing its combat abilities improve dramatically compared to when it practiced against bare ground or walls, Baby the mandrake became even more excited and extended its roots.

<Consecutive Stab>
<Single-Point Stab>

Not only did it immediately use the techniques it had learned, but as it happily attacked, it seemed to gain some insight and began testing new skills.

<Throwing>
<Choking>

"Aaargh, we're all going to die at this rate!"
"I can't take it anymore, President!!"
"Whether Oknodie gets angry or not, we need to beat that thing until it can't rampage anymore, or we'll die first!!"

Unable to endure any longer, the members took out seeds again and rapidly grew plant monsters.

<Rapid Growth - Parasitic Grass>
<Energy Absorption>
<Forced Curse of Weakness>

<Rapid Growth - Thorn Grass>
<Burrowing>
<Thorn Explosion>

<Rapid Growth - Stomping Grass>
<Kicking>
<Trampling Rampage>

But Baby the mandrake was no longer the weak Baby that had been helplessly enduring until now.

<Life Drain>
<Wood Skin>
<Raising Thorns>

"Para-para-parasitic?!"
"Tho-tho-thorns?!"
"Sto-sto-stomping!!"

It reversed the drain from the parasitic grass that had burrowed into its roots, defended against the thorn grass's thorns by adapting the wood skin magic that the Plant Club members had used, and even punished the stomping grass that had hurt it the most by raising thorns on its roots!

"This is crazy. Even though mandrakes are known for high learning aptitude and being ultra-rare monsters, they were never this good...?"

Unlike the confused Orchatch, Alice, who had received early education from the Foundation, had an idea.

'Jonna once said that children adopt the temperament and abilities of their parents or guardians.'

Baby mandrake's parent and guardian was none other than Oknodie.
She was the top scholarship student of the Wiheomhae Foundation and had proudly claimed the top position in the 981st class at the Gift Academy, where the world's greatest talents competed.
Her talent and capabilities were unquestionably unparalleled!
Moreover, she wasn't one of those fake heroes randomly selected by the 24 deities to be their apostles, but showed growth surpassing even Ishtar, the hero of the era chosen by the one true deity <Sophemia of the Sun>.
With such a guardian as a role model, it was inevitable that Baby would turn out extraordinary, like father like son.

'That's why Jonna's teachings were even more special. Enough to create a masterpiece like Oknodie.'

It was disheartening to see the stark difference between herself and Oknodie despite having the same guardian.
At least Baby mandrake didn't seem likely to fall into sadness like she had.

"All our natural mana is being used for defense, so we have no mana left to use..."
"Ugh, the aftermath of removing the magic from our ears and forcibly recycling it into internal mana for battle... our mana isn't regenerating anymore..."
"Why doesn't that crazy mandrake's mana ever deplete...?"

Having grown robustly by drinking special cultivation fluid every day for months without fail, Baby mandrake even had the advantage in a prolonged battle with more mana than the third-years.

"Ack!"
"Urk!"
"Argh!"

Unlike the members who got beaten more severely for resisting, only Orchatch, who had early on assumed a surrender position with both hands raised, was safe from Baby's violence.

"President, have you no pride?!"
"Are you more concerned about pride or bruises?"
"..."

Finally realizing the president's wisdom, the members also raised their hands to show they had lost the will to resist.

"Waaah!"

Baby mandrake let out a victory cry.
From Alice's perspective, judging by the increased stiffness and shine of its roots, Baby mandrake's fighting aptitude seemed to have grown even more from this victory.
It truly was remarkable growth.

"Baby, I'm here!"

As soon as the door burst open and the barrier was released, Baby had developed enough confidence to charge at Oknodie with bulging eyes!

<Body Slam>
<Eight-Direction Stab>
<Raising Thorns>

"Waaah!"

Seeing Baby mandrake's charge, Oknodie tilted her head and then extended her hand.

"Yap."

<Spider Web Binding>
<Throwing>

She wove mana like thread to create a net, tightened it to grab Baby with one hand, and then swung it violently in all directions, slamming it against walls.
After colliding with the walls and ceiling about ten times per second, Baby's eyes went dizzy and it fainted.
Baby's rebellion ended in just three seconds...

"Why has Baby become so violent? Where did it learn such bad techniques... ah."

Oknodie discovered the seniors kneeling in the corner of the room with their hands raised, sitting obediently in a row.

"So our Baby learned bad habits from the seniors?"

The Plant Club members, who realized that the owner was scarier than the pet plant, broke out in cold sweat.
