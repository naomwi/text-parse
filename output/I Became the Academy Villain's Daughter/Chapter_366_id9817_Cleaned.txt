# 366 - Investigation of the Truth

A mask is convenient.
Because it allows you to hide your expressions.
You won't reveal your thoughts through small muscle movements or give away your attack timing.
It's an advantageous tool for both offense and defense.
To Zuang, a mask was such an existence.

'It's been quite a while. Wearing a mask again in front of people around Oknodie.'

From the moment she received Gisele's call, she felt an inexplicable sensation.
Like the calm before a storm or a picture frame falling on a day of bad luck, the branch she usually climbed on her way to school snapped with a crack.

'I didn't expect Gisele would be the one I needed to be wary of.'

Gisele was a good person.
He saved students from senior Bellocasio's exploitative contracts and purchased many items at much more generous prices.
He didn't monopolize ingredients to exploit others, and he helped first-years navigate academy life by announcing changes to lecture times or locations that imperial professors and instructors failed to communicate.
His support within the grade was at the highest level, no exaggeration!
If Ishtar was the strongest in their year, Mesugakki the most powerful, and Oknodie the top student, even imperial students would point to Gisele as the next student council president.

"How did you become friends with those from the Three Great Noble Families?"
"Doesn't everyone miss food from home during difficult times? I procured ingredients and cooking utensils they needed."
"Hmm~ Is that so? You're clever."

He doesn't easily make enemies and lowers his guard to create good impressions.
Even realizing his hidden agenda to narrow communication between the frontier and the empire to himself alone and profit from that position, Zuang never pointed it out.
Whatever his intentions, many students including herself were having an easier academy life thanks to Gisele's help.

"Investigation request. Target: Jonna Wiheomhae. You know how unprofitable this job is, don't you?"

When people benefit from each other, they can treat each other comfortably.
But can that relationship continue when one side demands one-sided sacrifice?
Zuang's mask already knew the answer.

"It's a difficult request, but you're the only one I can rely on, Zuang."
"That's your problem."
"It's related to Oknodie's butler. Isn't that enough?"
"No, it's not. Whatever that person does or however they've lived, it's none of my business."
"This isn't about the past. It's about investigating a disappearance that just happened."
"That makes it even more dangerous. Digging into something that formidable expert has deliberately done."

Zuang contemplated something briefly.
Gift Academy.
Her reason for coming to this remarkable educational facility wasn't just to grow further as an assassin.
It certainly wasn't to make friends like Oknodie or Titoso.

"The numbering ticket from the entrance exam. Did you get it?"
"Numbering...? If you mean the advanced class preferential ticket, I'm afraid I failed to obtain it."

Gisele felt something strange.
The numbering ticket was a kind of guaranteed check.
It was like a spare chance for advanced class candidates to take the lower class exam if they failed the entrance exam.
Obtaining a numbering ticket could leave a strong first impression.
It would be evidence that you showed promising qualities different from others from the beginning.
But more than half a year had passed since admission.
Asking about possession of a long-past ticket now seemed somewhat odd.

"I guess you don't have it. Then our conversation is over."
"I can procure a numbering ticket holder for you. As you know, the Dark Association's intelligence network is the best among first-years."
"Better than Oknodie's personal intelligence?"
"...She's outside standard specifications."
"Hehe. I know. I was just being a little mischievous. It's nothing compared to the mischief you've caused."

Her laughter was cold.
Gisele could feel the distance with Zuang rapidly growing.
Showing agitation here would be third-rate.
Gisele inwardly pictured a cold lake.
He submerged all his distorted expressions trying to surface into that cold lake.
Imagining them sinking to the bottom, freezing cold, never to rise again.
That was Gisele's own mask, different from Zuang's.

"Since my credibility is in question, let me share some information to earn your trust. Our Dark Association is attempting to make contact with one of the current student council members. We're providing points and labor in exchange for information about the council's power structure, revenue model, and key rights."
"And?"
"It means I can find out at any time what secret the numbering ticket holds, contrary to common perception."

If you want to receive what you desire, this is your only opportunity to negotiate right here.
Beneath her mask, Zuang smiled crookedly at Gisele's negotiation.

"Alright. I'll propose my terms. Find the numbering ticket holder. And have the ticket transferred to me. With these conditions, I'll investigate the missing person. Of course, you must decide right now. There won't be another chance."

Transferring a numbering ticket whose value was unknown.
Perhaps it was an unreasonable price with tremendous value.

"I'll do it."
"!"

Zuang was honestly surprised.

"Don't merchants consider profit?"
"If I only thought about profit, I wouldn't have entered this academy."

His enrollment was solely for the sake of one child's welfare.
And this decision was no different.
Beyond the mask, some of the coldness in Zuang's face diminished.

"You'd better not change your mind. Unless you want to meet a dangerous woman like me alone at night."


* * *

In a world where all non-objective assassins are dead, it might be obvious, but Zuang objectively understands her own abilities.
When it comes to assassination, she can kill silver-plate level experts, and in stealth, she can deceive even gold-plate level experts.

Silver-plate. Top 1%. Regional evacuation order.
Gold-plate. Top 0.1%. National disaster.

Even by monster risk classification standards, there are extremely few experts in the world who can detect her stealth and prevent her assassinations.
However, as the only disciple of the Life Thief, one of the Ten Continental Thieves, her targets had to be those rare masters.
Professor-level experts are all at least gold-plate.
Professors like the retired former hero Destroyer are counted among platinum-plate level experts.

Is Jonna Wiheomhae that level of master?
What she witnessed on the cruise ship during summer vacation was certainly extraordinary.
He fought single-handedly on equal terms against the demon contractor Low, who had swept away countless students helplessly.

The massive total mana deployed.
The sturdy body and mind to withstand it.

With the right environment, his maximum potential might challenge platinum-plate level.
But objectively, Jonna Wiheomhae's ability could never reach platinum-plate.
His strength stems from environments where he can freely use "Metal Manipulation."

'How much metal could he carry around the academy anyway?'

During lectures, instructors bring plenty of metal for practice, but they wouldn't normally escort him with boxes full of iron ingots.
As she stealthily entered the gap, her confidence grew.

'As expected, he doesn't know.'

This wasn't the first time Zuang had spied on Jonna.
The first time was when Destroyer deployed his boundary transformation domain to block Jonna who was visiting Oknodie at the academy.
She had burrowed in reverse from the exit of that harsh barrier where massive gravity pressed down in double and triple boundaries, and observed.

<Mana Cultivation Method>
<Iron Armor Qigong>
<Self-Defense Technique - Silver Mountain Iron Wall>

Jonna's metal manipulation was sturdy enough to resist the oppressive gravity constraints originating from the gaze.
He had built his own domain so thoroughly that his existence remained unshaken and firmly maintained its properties no matter which domain he invaded.
A man with mana and domain like tempered metal.
He eventually broke through Destroyer's domain and successfully visited Oknodie.

'The second time was on the cruise ship and the deserted island.'

She continued to observe Oknodie's butler, Jonna Wiheomhae, while concealing her presence through stealth.
And she realized several things.
That Jonna Wiheomhae had gained insight during the process of overcoming Destroyer's domain.
His domain, unlike before, was not singular.
Countless domains were compressed like springs, overlapping.
So they could withstand even if pressed down by strong pressure.
So that after enduring and persevering, explosive release would be possible.

'If that man enters a suspicious place, he would surely use that domain for detection.'

Even Oknodie, before getting her backpack, would always look around nervously while hiding food in tree hollows or burying spoils from attacking wild students in the sand.
The butler's vigilance was just on a different scale compared to Oknodie's.

<Mana Cultivation Method>
<Iron Armor Qigong>
<Detection Technique - Metal Tuning Fork>

A domain that spreads like the sound of struck metal.
The layered emanating domain forcibly pushes away the surrounding space and all magical influences, revealing every form to its master.
There was only one way to avoid detection in this domain.
To thoroughly conceal one's magical reaction, disguise oneself as another object, and deceive the senses by showing only the minimum vibration that an inanimate object would display.

<Advanced Stealth Technique>
<Boundary Deception - Object-Self Unity>

Zuang mimicked the vibration frequency of a box full of assignment papers that could be found anywhere in the academy.
Jonna, completely fooled by her perfect disguise, put his hand into the wall.

Creak.

The metal wall opened wide through metal manipulation.
Jonna disappeared down the stairs inside.
Zuang was certain.
There was indeed a secret he wanted to hide through such ingenious means.
As she contemplated entering through the closing wall, someone bumped into her shoulder as she approached the door.

"Ouch."
"!?"
"Eek! How dangerous to pull out a dagger first!"

Zuang had taken a stance with a dagger at the throat, ready to cut off breathing with one strike.
The person who met her eyes was another stalker approaching the wall stealthily with an upside-down wooden box with its bottom removed—Oknodie with her blonde hair and ribbon headband.

"What are you doing here?"
"Exploring a new event!"

...with a box over her head?

"This week's assignments?"
"All done!"

Looking closely, the box was full of assignments.
The assignment box that could be found anywhere turned out to be the one Oknodie was wearing.

"You're so strange no matter when I see you."
"What are you doing here, Zuang?"
"Investigating your butler."

Seeing her face still making incomprehensible, odd statements or innocently blurting out rude comments, it was clear she wasn't someone disguised as Oknodie.
So... how would Oknodie react?
Would she get angry that someone tried to spy on her friend's butler? Maybe she'd draw a line saying they're no longer friends. That would honestly hurt a bit.

'If she really says that, I'll stab Gisele out of spite.'

As she fingered the knife in her pocket, Oknodie suggested with a bright face:

"Want to go in together?"
"Sure."

Jonna's surveillance team expanded to a two-person party.
