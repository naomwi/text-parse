<399 - Shocking News>

Mob heard some shocking news.

"I heard that a guy named Dobi cast some weird illusion on Oknodie and messed with her."
"That Oknodie?"
"Is that even possible?"

Oknodie had been played by someone.
Finding it hard to believe, Mob sought out the student who had spread the rumor.

"That story just now. Could you tell me more about it?"
"D-don't get so angry... I was just repeating something I heard somewhere..."

<Rage Explosion>
<Fighting Spirit>
<Charisma>

Mob had unintentionally developed a fighting spirit from his daily weight training, on top of his intimidating full-body armor.
The ordinary lower-class student couldn't handle the pressure of Mob's fluctuating energy that followed his emotional turmoil and shrank back.
Seeing the student's terrified face, Mob belatedly realized his mistake and pulled back his energy.

"Sorry. So what's this about Oknodie?"
"They say this guy Dobi made Oknodie wear a swimsuit and move a box full of tentacles."
"WHAT!!!!"

What the hell is that bastard doing!!!

"What the hell is that bastard doing!!!"
"Aaaah, stop grabbing my collar and shaking meee!"
"Tell me right now!!! Where is that bastard!!!"

The student's clothes tore with a ripping sound as Mob grabbed his collar and shook him violently.
Unable to withstand the shock of having his head and brain rattled, the student collapsed forward with a thud, half-naked and unconscious.
The color that appeared on the faces of surrounding students was the pure white of shock and terror!

"He forced Oknodie to wear a swimsuit and did something with tentacles!!!"
"I-I don't knooow, we don't know eitherrr!"
"Forgive uuus, we didn't do anything wrooong!"

Mob grabbed one student in each hand by the collar and shook them like vending machines that had swallowed coins, eventually causing them to pass out too.
After leaving three victims behind, Mob went straight to find the person most knowledgeable about information.

BANG.

"Gisele!!! I need your help!!!"

Gisele smiled somewhat proudly at Mob's intense entrance as he kicked open the door.

"Hmph. Thanks to Mr. Son O-cheon constantly flinging doors open, it was worth installing an expensive door. See how it remains undamaged despite being opened so violently."
"I don't care about that. Tell me where this Dobi guy is right now!!"

Hurt by Mob's disregard for his point of pride, Gisele answered with a slightly wounded expression.

"The information fee is displayed on the panel. Please place your palm on it to make payment."

Beep.

[10 points have been deducted.]

"Dobi is currently recruiting followers for his doomsday cult in the second-year lecture hall, classified as danger zone H1L25."
"Second year?"
"I see you've finally come to your senses at the mention of someone a year above you. Would you care to tell me why you're so angry in the first place?"
"That guy made Oknodie wear a swimsuit and carry a box full of tentacles!"
"...Hmm? Please wait a moment."

Gisele activated his information network to verify the information.
As always, he put down his magical communication device with narrowed eyes and a smile, but then his eyes opened halfway, revealing his three-hundred-eye pupils as his smile deepened.

"You've brought good information... After verification, I can confirm that all the information Mob brought is true. Dobi. That fellow can no longer be left alone."

He could barely contain his anger at the possibility that a mere first-year lower-class nobody, not even a foundation member, had done something inappropriate to Oknodie.

"Moreover, he didn't just play with Oknodie. They say a wet curtain was also discovered."
"A curtain too!?"

Now even more inappropriate images were upgrading in Mob's mind.

Dobi blackmailing Oknodie after discovering her weakness.
Making her take off her uniform, put on a swimsuit, wrap herself in a curtain, and then putting her in water full of tentacles...
Just the two of them.
Even skipping lectures.

"I can't take this anymore!!!"

Mob drew his sword and stormed into the meeting place where the second-years were gathered.

"Kill the vampires!"
"Join the doomsday cult!"
"Aaaah, what's the point of studying hard when the world is going to end anyway!! Dobi, please tell us what to do. What should we do!!"

The second-years had already fallen victim to Dobi's preaching.

"Dobi, come out right now!!"
"Who's that guy?"
"How dare a first-year casually call the name of Archbishop Dobi!"
"Are you friends with Dobi!?"
"A vampire's minion has appeared to hasten the end times! Let's defeat him and protect our grades from becoming trash with our own hands!!"

Second-years charged at him from all directions, shouting battle cries.
Mob was very flustered.
But the greater the adversity, the more one's training shines.

<Vine Binding>
<Adhesive Ground>
<Heavy Steps>
<Curse of Weakened Strength>
<Vision Obstruction Magic>

Multiple magic combinations flew at him instantly.
With his trained abilities, he forcibly broke through the instant binding spell that initiated the sequence.

"What!! He broke the Vine Binding with pure strength!?"

He dodged the subsequent binding spells that activated more slowly and overcame the curses with his resistance.

"Got him! The Vision Obstruction Magic hit. Now he won't be able to see for 5 seconds... Huh!?"
"I don't need to see. I've trained for this."

The training under Oknodie had developed his ability to deal with "all kinds of crises."
He could forcibly maintain his balance that would normally collapse after just a few seconds with eyes closed, and had even acquired the rare ability <Combat Continuation> that allowed him to mentally connect and visualize the lingering scenes in his mind.
Add to this the strength developed by enduring the cursed training armor set, and when he swung his sword?

"That's not enough! We're second-years. Our defensive magic density is completely different from first-yeaaaargh!"
"You first-year slacker, did you really think you could overcome second-years who seriously devote themselves to lectureaaaargh my arm is broken!"
"W-what the hell. This kind of strength is only shown by upper-class students, I've never heard of a first-year like this!! He's not even listed in the watchlist of concerning first-years compiled and distributed by Castella!!"

Just one year.
Such a small difference to overcome with high-intensity training that couldn't be achieved by those who claimed they worked "fairly hard" while taking it easy and enjoying themselves.
These second-years who put their studies on the back burner to hang around Dobi's gatherings were no match for Mob who was going all out.

"Overwhelm him with mana volume!"
"Take damage in turns!"
"Rotate in a wheel formation!"

Of course, second-years weren't like first-years who would be meaninglessly swept away when facing a strong enemy.
As soon as they noticed Mob's strength, they didn't let their guard down and united to take turns receiving blows and passing the turn to the next person, quickly depleting his strength.
Mob's stamina rapidly decreased as he had to give his all at every moment.

'Ugh, is this as far as I go!'

To think he couldn't even catch the culprit who had subjected Oknodie to such indignity, and instead would fall to his minions.

"You held out well, rookie."
"That voice...!"

Just as Mob's knees finally touched the ground and the second-years gained a significant advantage.
Mob felt something in the cool breeze that blew into the meeting place.
This was no ordinary wind.
It was a mana wind created by the movement of high-density mana that established its own domain by penetrating through the crowd, surpassing the flow of the many mana users who were emitting magical power!
Mob knew only one person who could produce such pressure along with a voice colder than a sword.

"Eastern Swordsman Xing!!"
"If you want to rise to the upper class, you should be able to overcome this much on your own."

A <Domain> that normally would barely spread to the surrounding space even when exerting all one's mana.
He sharpened it precisely and extended it like a sword strike in the direction of his killing intent.
Following the domain that extended before the sword, Xing's sword strike was unleashed.

<Magic Destruction>
<Curse Destruction>
<Physical Destruction>
<Defense Destruction>

Xing forcibly destroyed all enemy offense and defense in the path of his sword, proving the absolute hierarchy.

"I'll show you the way just this once."

Like the second-years who lost their fighting spirit, Mob almost lost heart for a moment.

'What monstrous strength. To make even Oknodie act cute around him, does one need to possess this level of power?'

The rumors about Oknodie weren't limited to today's Dobi incident that had enraged him.
When rare side dishes appeared in the cafeteria, attendance would increase as people said "even Oknodie is eating there today," and whenever she was seen digging in the ground, students would appear with shovels, claiming seniors had hidden experimental materials there.
Everyone who should know was aware of Oknodie's favorable attitude toward Xing, and Mob had been quite stressed due to his feelings of inferiority because of it.

'Dobi may have dealt the final blow, but the person who's been making me angry all along was this guy Xing.'

Hiding his inner turmoil of jealousy and anger, Mob bowed his head humiliatingly.

"Thank you for teaching me a lesson. I will definitely catch up to your level someday."

When that day comes, I'll return today's strike to you exactly as it was.
Looking down at Mob's tightly clenched fist as he bowed his head, Xing gave a light snort.

"So. Where is this Dobi guy that Gisele mentioned hiding?"
"L-Lord Dobi, after the meeting..."

One of the nearby second-years struggled to speak.

"After the meeting?"
"He went to the cafeteria for lunch..."
"...The cafeteria will be noisy today."

As Xing led the way to the cafeteria, Mob pulled himself together and followed.
