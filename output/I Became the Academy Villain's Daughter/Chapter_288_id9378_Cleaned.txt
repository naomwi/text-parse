# Graduating from the Academy by Luck - Chapter 288: What Will You Do for Me?

When Arcadia went to rescue Pyoi at Sandcooker's request, all the students imprisoned in the underground dungeon had already been released.

"Another one's coming."
"Aim for the head and knock them out!"

As soon as the underground prison door opened, students launched a coordinated attack against Arcadia!

Swoosh.

With one step back, Arcadia brought the attack into view and secured firing distance. Her gun barrel deflected two flying clubs.

"Agh!"
"She fired a gun!"

The students groaned, clutching their suddenly twisted wrists. The gun barrel that had been aimed at their heads lowered slightly.

"My, everyone's safe and sound."
"Lady Arcadia...?"
"Gasp... When did she aim for our heads?"

Arcadia listened to the students' story as they trembled at her terrifying shooting skills.

"I see. You gathered forces for a diversion for a day, raided the cafeteria and food storage, and moved all supplies to the underground prison... With this strategy, you could take control of all crew members without directly attacking the control room and captain's quarters. The attention to logistics must be Irene's contribution, right?"
"That's right."

Northern Grand Duchess Irene stepped forward from among the students. She was a beautiful girl with frost-white hair who always carried a low, cold atmosphere that made her difficult to approach. True to her reputation for being intimidating, though not as much as the hero, the students nervously made way for her, moving urgently as if they might get hit if they didn't.

'Is she sad?'

Despite her expressionless face, Arcadia sensed that Irene seemed somewhat sad. This was the effect of her improved "Expression Reading" ability, developed from observing Oknodie's inner thoughts—someone who always smiled but was secretly thoughtful and considerate.

"Is the shipboard rebellion going well?"
"So far."

The raid and occupation of the Level 3 food storage had been successfully completed. The problem was what came next.

"The crew members are blocking our escape routes one by one as they approach."
"Once the encirclement reaches the food storage, the Foundation's executives will also attack."
"We need to settle this before that happens. And before another executive arrives from the uninhabited island."

A Foundation executive was equivalent to an Academy professor. Even Arcadia didn't dare to imagine facing two professors simultaneously.

The problem was that there was no good solution! There was no way to break the stalemate. Irene and some students had gone outside the storage to prevent the encirclement from tightening and to help Xing who had occupied the control room, but the numerical difference was too great. Even without executive intervention, if they lost the control room, the food storage would be next, and the shipboard rebellion would end.

"It's fortunate that I came."
"Do you have a plan?"
"Yes. A much simpler one than you might think."

Gisele had eliminated himself to protect her, but perhaps his early elimination was because he wanted her to compensate for Irene's shortcomings. Arcadia realized she was the opposite type from Irene.

"Everyone, gather around here!"

Unlike the northerners who maintained battle lines through strict discipline while hiding unfavorable war situations, Arcadia, from the western maritime nation of Florence Kingdom, was skilled at boosting sailors' morale.

"Everyone on the uninhabited island will come looking for you, one person each day, giving up the auction!"
"Wow!"
"They're coming back despite giving up the chance to get amazing treasures? That's so kind!"
"They also plan to extract shipboard points from the crew to clear all your debts!"
"The upper class is the best!"
"I'm sick of washing dishes!"
"Now I don't have to fight as the crew's fighting dog in the underground arena!"
"I'm so happy I don't have to suffer through the hard labor of giving full-body massages to female crew members!"

The male students glared at their classmate who made this outrageous comment. If there was such a good point redemption position, you should have told us too!

"Time is on our side. Let's go all out to stop the enemy's advance and buy time!"

Not everything was true. Arcadia's statement cleverly mixed truth and lies, which tremendously boosted morale. Irene's students had been doing their duties diligently at their respective positions, even while feeling death approaching day by day. Arcadia instantly refreshed this atmosphere, encouraged her allies, and led them out of the storage.

'So this is the capability of Florence's treasure, Arcadia Seviche, who has commanded soldiers since a young age.'

I'll acknowledge it. There are many outstanding individuals around Oknodie. Titoso is one, and so is Arcadia. But when it comes to combat power, that's different. I'll show them the strength of a military magician specialized in stopping large armies. And I'll engrave in everyone's minds once more that the one who achieves the greatest merit in this shipboard rebellion is none other than me, Irene. I'll perform so impressively that even Oknodie will be forced to look back in amazement. Arcadia's speech heightened Irene's fighting spirit in a different way.

On the ship returning to the uninhabited island, Gisele and Son O-cheon looked back at the island with stiff faces.

"Will that little mouse be alright?"
"I don't know. I can't be certain."

Shipboard rebellion. They had heard that Oknodie was planning such a thing. But what really worried them wasn't everyone on the cruise ship but Oknodie on the uninhabited island.

"What if the hero and the imperial princess don't keep their promise until the end and betray us?"

They had made such a promise. That they couldn't tolerate the Foundation's auction items that would make everyone bleed. That they would teach the Foundation a lesson together. The intention was good. However, there was a major flaw in their auction sabotage and shipboard rebellion plan. If even one person betrayed them, those remaining on the uninhabited island would suffer tremendous damage. They held each other's weaknesses. Could they willingly give up such an opportunity? That hero or that imperial princess?

"I still don't trust them. That hero has been desperate to get rid of the little mouse at every opportunity."
"That's not it."
"Huh?"
"What I'm worried about is the opposite case."
"If not the hero or the imperial princess... the little mouse? You think she might do something?"

Gisele coldly recounted the facts.

"Oknodie invited all of us with an invitation from 'Papa.' Jonna, the Foundation's butler and executive, told us that Papa refers to the Foundation's chairman, the director."
"Being the daughter of such a high-ranking person should be a good thing, so why are you so concerned?"
"That chairman gave us 'boarding points' as gifts. Those who wasted those points would become Foundation scholarship students or couldn't escape being the Foundation's slaves without experiencing torture-like experiences. This was directed at us, Academy students."

A belated realization dawned on Son O-cheon's face.

"Are you thinking that Oknodie might have such evil within her as well?"
"We know that Oknodie, contrary to what others say, has the appearance of a good child. At the same time, she occasionally has bizarre thought patterns created by the Foundation, a performance-first personality, and bad-child-like qualities."

And now. Having experienced the chairman's gift, Gisele began to think.

"The Oknodie we know is just from a few months. If there's another side to her that we haven't discovered during that time, that side would be..."
"A 'scary child.' One who could commit terrifying acts without batting an eye, beyond our imagination."

Like the owner of that cruise ship, the chairman.
