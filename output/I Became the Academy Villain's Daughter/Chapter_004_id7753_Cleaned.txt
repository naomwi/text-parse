# 4 - Frail (Not Really)

After the commotion of the previous day, the butler realized something.
He shouldn't treat his Lady like an ordinary child of her age.

"Personnel support, is it? And from you of all people."
"She's a child who requires a lot of attention."
"The Boss won't be pleased about this."
"I'm prepared to accept the consequences."
"Is she worth it?"
"Her talent with the sword is genius-level."
"Oh?"
"Her talent with the bow is comparable."
"Then why request an assassination instructor?"

Jonna chose his words carefully.
Unlike the Boss, other competitors who served different 'Ladies' would certainly not overlook Oknodie's weaknesses.
He was the only butler granted the Wiheomhae surname, so others would be jealous and envious, looking for any opportunity to exploit his Lady's weaknesses.

"Stealth and balance movements are ingrained in her like habits. My Lady was born to be an assassin."
"I see."

The butler thought to himself.
For a Lady suffering from insomnia and various mental disorders like avoidant personality disorder, interpersonal relationships would be difficult.
If that's the case, she should pursue a class where broken interpersonal relationships wouldn't matter.

"Assassins use both swords and bows."
"Good, versatility is valuable."

The supervisor made his decision.

"I'll send someone."
"Thank you."
"However, if the instructor determines the child lacks aptitude, you'll pay the price."
"I accept those terms."
"You must be quite confident. I hope her talent matches your confidence."

The dispatch of an assassination instructor who would also serve as an assistant for Oknodie was confirmed.

* * *

"Night outings are absolutely forbidden. I will not permit any outings without my accompaniment."

The butler's treatment was frustrating.
It wasn't a new thought, but after being treated like "a child who eats stones," the frequency of surveillance had notably increased.

A poor child who might cause trouble at any moment.

This image of me had completely solidified in Butler Jonna's mind, to the point where he wouldn't leave me alone for even a moment.
As a result, the experience points for my Hiding skill had completely stagnated.

"My Lady needs to value herself more. Someone who doesn't cherish themselves won't be treated preciously by others."
"Does it really matter? Being treated preciously. People live alone and die alone anyway."

Whenever I revealed even a hint of my former male mindset, the butler's eyes would become complicated, as if resolving, "Sigh... I really need to take better care of her."
How could I say anything under these circumstances?
Thanks to this, I've been diligently training, but for some reason, I get out of breath quickly and tire easily these days.

'Is it because I'm in a woman's body?'

After seeing an internet meme about how being reincarnated as a muscular male character would make life easier due to size and strength, I had always pursued the path of becoming a muscular man.
I'd never experienced being such a small female character, so the hypothesis that it was because I was female seemed quite convincing.

Surely female characters receive fewer stat bonuses.
The solution is simple.
I just need to raise my stats more.
That settles it.
I should go on an expedition to the next village for stat stones.

"I want to go out. Alone."
"Absolutely not."

The problem is the butler.

"Lady Oknodie is frail. You're still too young and not sufficiently trained to fully handle the threats of the outside world."
"But I can proudly say I'm much stronger than others my age."
"From my perspective as your butler, you're still a child. Many of those you'll encounter in the outside world won't be peers but adults."

Fighting with facts alone is unfair.
However, this game's luck factor includes unpredictable dangerous elements.
If probability's tricks overlap just a few times, a storm of insane bad luck far exceeding same-level dangers could suddenly strike.

"Please just eat your meal quietly. When you've eaten well, trained hard, and become sufficiently strong, I'll permit outings with an escort."

I guess there's no choice.
The food is delicious anyway.
I'll have to collect stat stones later when I can go out from the Academy.
But aren't I already strong enough?
My swordsmanship and archery skills are steadily improving.
While complaining, the water glass slipped from my hand.

"Huh?"
"You see? Your arms are so delicate you can't even hold a cup properly."
"No, that's not it. This has never happened before... My arms must be tired from training too hard."
"I won't listen to excuses."
"But it's true..."

The shock was considerable, especially since I never imagined I would drop a water cup.
Right, what outings could I manage with this body?
For the time being, I should add two hours of strength training to my regimen and train intensely.

* * *

If he tried to monitor the Lady to prevent her from going out without his knowledge until the assassination instructor arrived, he would end up unable to sleep even at night.
Not wanting to suffer from such excessive labor, Jonna came up with a scheme.

'If she's sufficiently exhausted from daily activities, she won't have the energy left for mischief at night.'

Unbeknownst to Lady Oknodie, the weight of every household item and object she used in daily life was gradually increasing each day.
The water cup she just dropped weighed 5kg.
He had been replacing the wooden cups with ones containing increasingly heavy metals—from manganese to iron to copper—hidden between the wooden layers.
He had planned to stop increasing the weight when she finally collapsed, but surprisingly, the Lady endured every weight increase with incredible perseverance.

'She definitely has the aptitude for an assassin.'

Training to remain in one place for long periods or lifting heavy objects is essential for assassins.
Though he didn't know what actual assassins trained in, at least in Jonna's mind, an assassin would need to do that much.

'The instructor will be pleased.'

A teacher naturally likes a student who provides the satisfaction of teaching.
An assassination instructor would be no different.

'This measure is necessary for the Lady herself, not just for the instructor.'

The poor Lady who would drink any potion from street magicians and even swallow stones to satisfy her hunger needed strength.
Human rights stem from power.
He needed to take special care to ensure she wouldn't put herself in danger with hazardous part-time jobs or harm her health with poor eating habits again.
With strength, she could earn money easily without dangerous jobs, and with that money, she could eat her fill.

'Become strong, Lady Oknodie. I never want to see the Lady I serve being discarded due to weakness again.'

The butler sincerely hoped that his relationship with this Lady would last a long time.

* * *

I eat well, sleep well, and train hard.
By now, I should have grown enough to go out alone, but today I accidentally dropped my spoon while eating soup.

"You are frail."
"Me?!"
"You are frail."
"How can someone who handles a sword so well be frail? I'm excellent at running too!"
"You dropped your spoon."
"Yes, I did..."
"You are frail."
"Ugh..."

The butler is right.
During meals, I keep dropping things, and sometimes I trip over my clothes.
Lately, even I wonder if I was always this weak, as my body occasionally feels heavy.

'Did I get some negative trait?'

Something like [Physically Awkward] that makes me clumsy with my body, or [Frailty] that makes me easily susceptible to illness.
Occasionally, there are characters born with negative traits that apply adverse effects to their bodies.
I never chose this aggressive investment style of accepting penalties for greater benefits as it wasn't my preference, but it seems this body is different.

'So this is what negative traits are like.'

I can only guess since I've never experienced it before.
But this is yielding quite a lot of experience points.

[You have swung a medium sword for over 5 minutes while enduring extreme fatigue.]
[Swordsmanship experience +1]

If I had a man's body, strong muscles would support my arms and experience points wouldn't come so easily, but with this current body, my arms tremble with just a little training, and experience points increase.

'Female characters gain skill experience more easily than male characters despite having weaker stat bonuses! Or maybe training efficiency increases as compensation for negative traits?'

The speed at which experience accumulates is very pleasing even by veteran standards.
If I had known this, I would have chosen a female character much earlier.
Still, mealtimes are uncomfortable.
Perhaps because I trained hard all day, my arm strains just to lift a fork.
As I try to twirl noodles with my trembling hand, my strength fails and I stab the plate with my fork.

*Crack*

The plate cracked.

"You are frail."
"...I applied too much force and cracked the plate?"
"If you need to apply enough force to break a plate just to use a fork, you're not in full control of your body."
"...As frustrating as it is, I can't deny that..."
"If plate fragments mix with the noodles, they could cause internal injuries, so I'll serve you on a new plate."

Am I really frail...?
Despite my strength and stamina being quite high according to training tests, I'm a frail beautiful girl?
It's contradictory, but if negative traits exist, it makes sense.
Perhaps it's because of my strength and stamina that I can endure this much.
Oknodie's body might have such serious negative traits that if I became lazy, I could end up bedridden and dying.

"Say 'ah'."
'Well, at least I'm a frail beautiful girl.'

Setting aside the negative traits.
Being protected like this gives me a strange sense of security.
Like a baby bird under its mother's protection!
Surely it's fine to be fed spaghetti by the butler.
I've started to act a bit spoiled.
