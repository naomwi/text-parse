# 339 - Broken Child

Outwardly, Xing made jokes to refresh the mood, but inwardly he was deeply shocked.

'My attempt to slash those who insulted Oknodie with my sword ended up causing trouble for Oknodie herself.'

The instructor wasn't fooled by Oknodie's nonsense.

"I'll say it once more. Your shallow excuses won't work. If you can't pay the bail, I'll formally file charges with the student council for obstruction of duty and violence against faculty."
"Really?"
"If this goes to a formal trial, you should be prepared for a heavier sentence."
"That's fine with me."
"What?"
"You're not the only one who's confident. I'm also confident about going to trial according to school rules."

Oknodie also wasn't fooled by the instructor's nonsense.
Indeed, there's probably no facility with better early education in scheming than the Foundation.
The daughters of noble families.
While they were openly slandering Oknodie and provoking her to hit them, he had only been thinking about slaughtering them.
Xing regretted it.
Getting to know Oknodie.
Since meeting her, he kept seeing his own inadequacies.

"Obstruction of duty and violence against faculty. Spot fine of 10,000 points. If you don't accept, there's no compromise."
"It wasn't proven intentional and it's my first offense?"
"...Fine, 100 points."

Oknodie paid the points, which were little more than pocket change, and instantly cleared of charges.
The female students who had been mocking her in front of Xing flinched.

"Is she allowed to get off this easily?"
"This isn't right, instructor!"
"Shut up."

The instructor glared at the female students with a very angry face.

"You set me up. The story and situation were completely different. I was staking out to witness an attempted murder by a delinquent student, not to fall into a trap and be attacked."
"N-no! We didn't mean to do that."
"We really didn't know Oknodie would come here. Really, instructor."
"Don't you trust us? We... had no one to rely on but you! *sob sob*"

The female students started crying as soon as they panicked.
The instructor crushed the crystal ball in his hand.
The flame that rose in his palm erased the crystal ball without leaving a trace.

"You and I have no relationship anymore. So let me be clear. The crime of disrespecting an instructor won't be forgiven so cheaply."

Seeing that no excuses would work against his determination, the female students stopped their crying act.
The trio of female students glared fiercely at Xing, Oknodie, and the instructor in turn before leaving.

"Don't involve the instructor in troublesome matters either."
"Are you really in a position to say that?"
"Ahem. Hmm. I'm busy, so I'll be going now."

The instructor, who had tried to assert his authority only to be humiliated, quickly left.
In the end, it wasn't Xing's sword but Oknodie's tongue that resolved the situation.

"How shameful."

Xing knows it too.
In a real sword fight, he might be able to defeat Oknodie.
But there are often situations in the world that can't be handled with swords alone.
This was one of them.
If you fall into a trap prepared by the enemy, the very act of wielding your sword can lead to self-destruction.

"Seriously, Xing. How could you fall for such a simple trap? We have a long way to go together after graduation."
"I'm ashamed."
"Sigh. Lift your head. Are you going to show such a gloomy face in front of your little sister?"

Though she's fake.
The additional comment was pushed from his ear as soon as he saw the ghost little sister Lin peeking out from behind Oknodie.
Whether she knew and was doing this deliberately, or it was just a coincidence.
The ghost imitating his sister was quite skilled.
His real sister would have looked at him just like that.

"From now on, don't swing your sword so lightly. Understand?"
"It wasn't light."

The reason he wields his sword is for his sister's revenge.
He has never forgotten that fact.
It was never a light resolve.

"Really?"

Oknodie looked doubtful.
Even the sword he was about to draw for her honor.

"The sword I drew for you was never light."

Confusion arose in her eyes, which usually looked down on everything with a know-it-all expression.
Eyes wavering in a way unlike Oknodie.
Hands wandering in the air, not knowing where to point, having lost words.
The reason all that atmosphere overlapped with the fake sister behind her might not just be his imagination.

*Swoosh.*

A hand reaching toward Oknodie's head.
As his hand was naturally about to touch her scalp, Oknodie flinched belatedly.

*Slap.*

Oknodie swung her arm and knocked away his hand without realizing it.
Her expression, more surprised by her own action, quickly erased the agitation and turned into a grin that deserved a knuckle tap on the head.

"Worrying about me? You're a century too early. I'm Oknodie, remember? This level of commotion doesn't faze me at all."

Did I get caught up in the mood?
Xing also smiled slightly.

"Then from now on, I'll also slash at bushes."
"Huh??"
"Isn't it natural for a swordsman to have a hobby of swinging his sword at bushes where enemies might be hiding?"
"That sounds like an expensive hobby!"
"I'll prepare enough to pay the bail."

Others might call him even crazier, but it's better to make people too afraid to mess with him than to just be used while doing nothing.
If he's not a hindrance, Oknodie can also move around more freely without burden.
He can't be a brother who becomes an obstacle to his sister.
...Though he's not sure which sister he's referring to.


* * *


Oknodie was broken.
That was Zuang's confident conclusion after watching Oknodie for hours.

"Oknodie. One curse doll is enough to cross the cursed swamp."

With a somewhat dazed face, she had already made thirteen curse dolls when only one was needed, making Titosoga cry as he struggled to make even one.

"Oknodie. Professor Pinkberry is only looking at you."
"It's too late! You, answer the three tips for escaping a spot check while performing disguise techniques."
"Basics and understanding of disguise techniques, spot check tips, concealing weapons, refusing personal item inspection, assault during forced inspection..."
"What? You were actually listening properly."
"...The subcategories of disguise techniques are makeup application, bone compression technique to reduce body size, bone expansion technique to increase body size, voice alteration technique."
"That's content I was going to teach next week..."
"Attack items are Camela's makeup in the dormitory, mana alchemy that enables physical transformation, seniors' disguise tips, minstrel's experiences."
"...Ah. Now I get it. You're saying that as the top student in your year, this obvious content isn't worth concentrating on, it's boring? Fine. Since only you two are listening anyway, I'll teach you a lesson. Know that I'll advance the curriculum tremendously for the next lecture!"

Suddenly answering questions that weren't even asked with a dazed face, causing the lecture schedule to be advanced.

"Oknodie. That's not a window into the dormitory, it's a knothole where squirrels live."

Trying to enter and sleep in the wrong place because her mind was elsewhere.
Zuang's eyes narrowed at this behavior that anyone would find suspicious.
Could Oknodie possibly...

"Tell me honestly."
"Huh?"
"Did you secretly commit a delicious assassination without me knowing?"

Did she go on an assassination mission?!

"The symptoms are exactly the same. The thrill of taking a person's life, the silent scream of someone who deserves to die, the sensation of life fading away transmitted through your fingertips. Isn't your mind elsewhere because you can't forget that taste?"
"No!"
"Then why do you keep feeding your plant friend who's crying 'waah waah' that it's going to die from overeating?"

Oknodie hurriedly rescued the feed from the mandrake that was holding handfuls of feed with a face saying "waah I'm going to die from a burst stomach."
Unlike goldfish that eat all the food given to them until they actually die from a burst stomach, fortunately mandrakes were intelligent enough.

"Actually, I have something on my mind."
"I hope this concern is worth more than the increasingly difficult disguise technique lectures coming up."

Despite Zuang's cutting remark, Oknodie spoke again with dreamy eyes as if wandering through a dream.

"A child who usually seemed high-maintenance suddenly told me that the sword he drew for me wasn't light."

At those words, throwing knives spilled from Zuang's sleeve onto the floor, she was so surprised.
While the baby mandrake was too scared to even make a "waah" sound, Zuang moved closer and asked more actively.

"And then?"
"When a weak, young child with a weak heart suddenly does something they don't usually do, you become careful because you're afraid they might get hurt or break, you know what I mean?"

Zuang thought carefully.
What could it mean that someone who appeared to be the strongest child in the world was suddenly comparing herself to a weak and cute being?
Perhaps this is how Oknodie expresses that her heart has softened since visiting the chairman's mansion?

"So what does Oknodie want to do?"
"Hmm..."

From Oknodie's following words, Zuang realized that the child had really gone through a lot of emotional struggles at the Foundation.

"Gaining favor from a stagnant water was supposed to be used as a shield, not for romance. I know that, but I keep wanting to take a taste."
