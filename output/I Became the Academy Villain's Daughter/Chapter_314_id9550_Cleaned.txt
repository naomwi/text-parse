# 314 - Reason for Climbing

Papa really prepared the <All-Healing Medicinal Soup> in just three days.
Among Unique-grade dishes, this was so rare that even adding several "super" prefixes wouldn't be enough to describe its rarity.
The benefits of eating such a dish in the early stages—one that might barely appear in playthroughs where the continental situation falls apart in the fourth year—were tremendous beyond words.

[Unique dish <All-Healing Medicinal Soup> has been added to your Cooking Book.]

━━━
Collected 30 medicinal dishes - Acquired <Longevity> ability.

Collected Unique dish [All-Healing Medicinal Soup] - Disease resistance increased by 10000%.
━━━

It essentially grants immunity to all attacks and status effects classified as disease-type with an incredible stat boost.
Even a 100% boost would nullify most effects, but 10000% is such an enormous modifier that it would be nearly impossible for the percentage to drop below 100%, even if my physical condition deteriorated or special debuffs reduced the percentage.
Now I probably wouldn't catch even a cold if I rolled around in snow or slept in wet clothes.
Should I try it when winter comes?
Hmm... I feel like if Isabel or Arcadia caught me, they'd strip off my clothes and throw me into a hot spring.

[Your understanding of the character <Arcadia> has increased.]
[Your understanding of the character <Isabel> has increased.]

Huh? But is that really a bad thing?
A hot spring event would be awesome.
I can't pass up the chance to collect maekbanseok eggs and sikhye.
I'll definitely try it when winter comes.
I should add it to my wishlist so I don't forget!


* *


Three days until the next feast.
The students' accommodations were divided into two places.
The special lodging rooms on the 10th floor.
Or the cruise ship outside the mansion.
Those who gave up climbing the mansion returned to the cruise ship.
No one was bold enough to stay long in a place their mind rejected, and if they had that kind of mental fortitude, they wouldn't have given up staying in the mansion in the first place.
Mob was one of the few students who went against this trend in the lower class.

"The wooden puppet floor again?"
"It's at the front except for the first floor, and it's easy."
"Thinking something is easy just because it's quick is arrogant. When you reach a higher level and things get difficult, the difficulty waiting for you is completely different from your beginner days."
"Is that wisdom from your mercenary life?"
"Yes. Cutting is truly a difficult skill."

From Jaku's answer, Mob realized that he was still wandering in the depths hidden behind the seemingly simple technique of cutting.
When you become an expert in anything, a completely different perspective opens up compared to when you were an ignorant novice.

"It doesn't matter. Facing difficulties has been what I've done longest since entering the Academy."

The cursed equipment items surrounding Mob's body were trials in themselves.
He grows steadily stronger by enduring physical pain day by day.
Doing difficult things has now become as natural as breathing for Mob—it's his daily life and the very act of living.

"I have good news for you. Oknodie asked me to tell those going to the lower floors."
"Oknodie did?"
"For every 30 minutes you endure on each floor, you can get a special reward. That's the kind of place this is, she said."
"...!"
"Now that you have a goal, do you feel more motivated to take on the challenge?"
"Quite so. I was going to do it anyway, so it feels like a gift. Are you also aiming for this, Jaku?"
"Wooden puppets are good opponents to test <No Technique>. If I can steadily build strength without relying on dark mana, the burden on my body will be reduced."

Of course, I know.
I know that doing this won't let me catch up to truly monstrous people like Hestia or Xing.
But I can't live my life constantly comparing myself to these powerhouses who are so far above me that I have to crane my neck just to glimpse their level.
The weak have their own lives too.
Even in this place—whether it's the chairman's mansion, tower, or training facility—the weak must seize opportunities for growth in their own way.
If I waste the Academy's summer vacation, the second semester that follows, and even advancement to the second year won't come so easily.

"Just enduring is boring. Let's have a betting match after a long time."
"You mean who can endure longer?"

Even with his face hidden behind his helmet, Mob could feel Jaku smiling.
That's right.
This guy has never refused my challenges.
Maybe being invited to Oknodie's place for summer vacation wasn't all bad.
With footsteps as light as his smile, Jaku leaped into the wooden puppet floor.


* *


Xing felt a considerable urge to challenge.

What do dungeons exist for?
They exist to descend deeper.
Because precious rewards sleep in the deepest depths.

What do towers exist for?
They exist to climb higher.
Because precious rewards sleep at the very top.

He enjoyed achieving things.
Above all, the information Oknodie had hinted at fanned the flames in his heart.

"This is called the <Tower of Training>, a special conquest area! I didn't know people still managed it since it was originally an abandoned facility, but seeing it like this is refreshing!"
"You knew about this place?"
"I used to come here often to play before."

...She frequented such a brutal facility like her own home at an even younger age?
Well, considering Oknodie's strength, it wasn't an unbelievable story.
It was nice to see the ghost sister curiously wandering around in ghost form near Oknodie, intrigued by the unfamiliar tower.

"How should I use this facility most efficiently?"
"Hmm~ If you're not interested in getting the special rewards that increase every 30 minutes on each floor, then summit conquest is best!"
"Summit conquest?"
"The tower gives rewards when you exit the facility based on the highest floor you've climbed!"

I wonder if Papa will be the one giving us rewards in this case?
Oknodie's answer provided strong motivation.
One who hides their power.
Xing recognized it at a glance.
The chairman was no ordinary powerhouse.
A special gift personally prepared by him.
Though it might be dangerous and unsettling, Xing was convinced it would be worth it.
At least the chairman didn't seem like a petty villain who would package worthless trash with plausible excuses.

"Dream on. I'll be the first to reach the top."
"That's too much reward for someone who only knows how to use magic. You should be the one to give up."

Xing and Irene climbed every floor at the forefront.
Their rapid ascent, which even pushed back the hero in momentum, stemmed from a difference in desperation.
The hero is already strong.
So strong that they wouldn't stand a chance even if they attacked together.
They want to become stronger.
If they could satisfy that craving by climbing this tower, there was no reason to refuse.
However, the world cannot be lived on desperation alone.

━━━
Chairman's Mansion
39F - Silent Floor
━━━

The wall of compatibility appeared.
On the Silent Floor, where making a sound causes blade winds to rush in from all directions.
Unlike Irene, who broke through the floor with her shadowless magic, Xing's progress was blocked.
Zuang, who had been leisurely climbing as a latecomer, passed through the noise traps with light feet.
The hero created noise in different directions with <Holy Mirror> to misdirect the attacks, then broke through the gaps in the blade winds.
Xing couldn't display such skills like them.
All he knew was to swing his sword.
To cut all matter that approached his range.

"...I can't get past this."

Even Hestia, who had been fiercely swinging her weapon like a storm to open a path, was blocked by the wall of the 39th floor.
The stronger you swing your weapon, the fiercer the blade winds become and the more they rush in.
No matter how careful you are with the first strike, the more noise your weapon makes deflecting the growing winds, the more blade winds fly in next.
It was nothing short of a grave for close-combat weapon users.
Those who realized their climb had failed gave up and went down to lower floors, intending to get special rewards by enduring for more than 30 minutes.
Mututoga Lotto, the stamina monster Son O-cheon, and many other students turned back.
Even Hestia was no exception.
Only Xing continued his lonely battle on that floor, refusing to give up.

Clang! Clang!

Even as more and larger winds rushed in with each noise his sword made deflecting the blade winds.
Even as he was driven into a corner where he felt he would be killed by the blade winds if he pushed further.

'After fighting the Apostle of Anrage, I keenly realized. With my current skills, I can barely help Oknodie. I probably don't have a chance to return to my homeland and complete my revenge.'

If he couldn't grow stronger, he'd rather die here.
As he embraced this desperate resolve, he heard Oknodie's innocent voice.

"Hmm~ Should I give you a hint?"
"...Oknodie. Weren't you not interested in climbing this tower?"
"Rin wants to see her brother!"

Rin, always bound to Oknodie's side.
A fake ghost borrowing the form of his real sister.
He's known for a long time that she isn't real.
Nevertheless, being an entity with the same face dulls the heart.
Especially when it's someone he failed to protect.

'Just this once'

It makes him harbor such soft thoughts unconsciously.
For the ghost, it's merely a survival strategy.
If he dies, she would lose her reason to stay by Oknodie's side pretending to be his sister.
So the fake Rin urging Oknodie to bring her here is nothing more than a struggle to survive.
Despite rationally understanding all these relationships, an inexplicable tickling sensation stimulated him.

-Brother. I'm satisfied just being by your side.

A child who was too shy to speak aloud and would trace letters on her palm like a tickle.
A child with no greed, a good child.
Xing, who took up the sword and did what the adults assigned him to do, wanting to create a good environment for such a child.
While he was away, Rin was manipulated and violated by the adults of the family as they pleased.

'If it were the real Rin, she wouldn't worry about me.'

What would she want from someone who couldn't even protect her small wish to be together?
There would only be resentment left.
Whether the fake Rin couldn't uncover such past through reading his thoughts, or deliberately ignored it.
Instead of resentment, the fake Rin shows subtle concern.
Unable to tell whether this made him happy or sad, Xing simply put strength into his sword-wielding body.

"Relax!"

Oknodie's advice, as if seeing through his inner thoughts.
Startled, Xing listened as Oknodie continued.

"What changes by cutting the wind with force? Wind isn't something to cut, but to redirect!"

Not cutting, but redirecting.
His sword, which had been boiling with hatred and anger, recalled the face of the ghost peeking out from behind Oknodie, stealing glances at him.
With the strength drained from it, his sword moved lighter and faster, and the noise from the sword tip became smaller.
As the power of the incoming blade winds diminished, the difficulty he felt lessened.
Before long, Xing felt the change.
The blade winds of the 39th floor were no longer challenging.
After all, the blade winds that had been making it difficult for him were his own hatred and anger echoing back.

Click.

As Xing reached the exit of the floor, a special reward appeared beside him, given to those who stayed for more than an hour and broke through the floor.
Before placing his hand on the reward, Xing turned to look at Oknodie, who was grinning behind him.

"Were you the same?"
"Hmm~ I had quite a struggle too! It was a difficult hurdle for an all-strength character to overcome stubbornly with power without using their head."

Xing realized.
Though easy to forget because of her ever-smiling face, Oknodie's past was no less dark than his own.
Perhaps her darkness continues in the present tense.
Oknodie also has hatred and anger.
While he recalled his sister and restrained his emotions, what does Oknodie think of to restrain hers?

'Is it really food?'

Xing felt he could somewhat understand Oknodie's obsession with fine cuisine.
