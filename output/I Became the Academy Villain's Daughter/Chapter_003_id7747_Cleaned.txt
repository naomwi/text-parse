# Swallowing Stones

Jonna Wiheomhae.
Jonna recalled the Boss who had given him his name.
He owed the Boss many favors.
But those favors weren't free.
His name was a perfect example.
He had discarded his old past along with his old name, but in exchange, hadn't he received a rather strange new one?
Remembering the odd looks people gave him, he unconsciously clenched his fist.

Crack.

Unnecessary garbage.
He pulled out the knife handle that had crumpled in his grip and tossed it away, then inserted the blade into a new handle.

Whoosh
Swish swish swish

He slashed through the air and assumed a stabbing stance.
The grip and stabbing sensation felt decent enough.
Nodding with satisfaction, he adjusted his hold on the knife.

Tap tap tap

A kitchen knife dancing across the cutting board!
The young lady he was now serving was quite an unfortunate child.
Regardless of his cooking skills or preferences, whenever he saw that poor girl, he couldn't help but want to do everything for her.

'She seems to have completely forgotten what contract she made.'

When he first saw her, he thought she just looked somewhat decent for a common orphan from the streets.
Whether the Boss had an exceptional eye for talent or not, she unexpectedly possessed remarkable potential in physical strength and weapon skills.

A raw genius.

Her posture and skills, without having received any formal education, were extraordinary to a degree that could only be explained by innate talent.
Perhaps it was thanks to that talent.
The secret to how such a small, insignificant girl had survived life on the streets.

'Somehow she avoided becoming a prostitute.'

In this world, even moderately attractive girls get captured by pimps and become prostitutes who cry while servicing more than ten customers a day.
He could imagine what kind of life she must have endured to gain her quick feet, surprising strength, and various weapon skills, even without seeing it firsthand.

'But her mental defects are concerning.'

Perhaps due to experiences of being attacked by men on the streets who lusted after her body every night, the girl didn't know how to sleep properly.
The moment he took his eyes off her, she would demonstrate her remarkable talent for hiding—in empty barrels, wooden boxes, behind weapon cabinets, or even in the chandelier on the ceiling.
Day by day, her hiding places were becoming increasingly technical. It was becoming difficult for him as the butler responsible for supervising the young lady.

'A skittish, high-maintenance stray cat of a young lady. Definitely not a common type.'

Though he performed his butler duties every year around this time, he had never encountered such an unusual young lady before.

"It's mealtime, my lady."

He set down the bubbling stew and vegetable side dishes, then lightly rang the chime bell on the table.
Humans are creatures of adaptation.
Using wisdom reduces physical hardship.
Just as he counted to three in his mind, Oknodie came running with the sound of pattering feet.
He had realized it was more efficient to ring the bell than to play hide-and-seek looking for her, but the direction of the sound was strange.

"..."

Surely not.
Making his thought meaningless, a ceiling panel opened with a click, and a cute little head popped out.
Perhaps she was more like a little mouse than a stray cat.

"You're covered in dust."
"Ah."
"We'll have our meal after you wash up first."

Oknodie washed her face vigorously, like a boy.
As if she'd forgotten she was a girl, she merely splashed water on her face.
She was so perfunctory about it that he personally washed her face meticulously for a full minute.

'...I don't know what to write in my report to the Boss.'

After completing the day's training regimen, he forcibly tucked Oknodie into bed, covered her with a blanket, and picked up his pen.

<Regular Report>
*Oknodie Field Supervisor's Observations
-Has trauma from prolonged street life.
-Suspected to have various mental disorders including insomnia and avoidant personality disorder.
-Appears to have forgotten the contract but is actively cooperating with training.
-Lacks awareness as a female. In daily activities such as eating and washing, she is accustomed to doing everything quickly and inconspicuously like a boy, for survival.
-Evidence suggests she learned wand magic in exchange for giving blood and taking illegally modified potions to a street magician for experiments. Said magician was tortured and executed. No evidence of criminal activity by Miss Oknodie. However, confirmed the magician had used multiple orphans as test subjects.
-The exact source of her swordsmanship and archery training is unknown, but she appears to have learned techniques from at least iron-plate level adventurers. Additional verification required regarding any suspicious transactions.
-...
-...

Rustle.

'A mouse?'

No, that couldn't be.
Unlike humans, animals instinctively detect their predators.
He quietly picked up his sword and went out without making a sound.
When he came down to the living room, he saw a familiar figure from behind.
Miss Oknodie.
The talented girl taken in by the Boss.
Where could she be going at this late hour?
It would be troublesome if she tried to escape from his watch after being stopped.
To prevent similar incidents in the future, it would be better to discover her purpose.

'Could she be stealing things like a petty thief?'

It wouldn't be the first time he'd encountered such foolish young ladies.
There had been some with bad habits of taking things that weren't theirs.
Jonna thought to himself.
He hoped that poor girl wouldn't be like that.
Etiquette education is painful for both the recipient and the instructor.

'That's...'

Miss Oknodie's destination wasn't a black market merchant's shop or an antique store that handled stolen goods.

Late at night, an empty park.
What could she be trying to do in such a place?

'I hope she doesn't have some troublesome hobby.'

A young lady who secretly indulges in perverted hobbies would harm the Boss's reputation.
If such a fact were discovered, she might need proper discipline rather than mere etiquette education.
While he was mentally preparing himself, Miss Oknodie's audacious nighttime activity finally began.

Walking along the curb around the park fountain.
Jumping between shop signs.
Walking on top of a wall with arms outstretched.

'...I worried for nothing.'

What on earth was she doing?
Perhaps it was a habit from her street life, playing secretly in the night streets.
He couldn't help but chuckle at her impressive sense of balance and nimble movements.

"It should be around here... Ah, is this it?"

What had she found?
As he narrowed his eyes to spy on her, she picked something up from the top of the wall.
As far as he could see, it was nothing but a stone.
Was she collecting pretty-shaped stones?
What a cute hobby.
Still, he should caution her since it could be dangerous if she encountered patrolling guards or thugs in the night streets.
He decided to wait until breakfast tomorrow to warn her at the table so she couldn't run away.
Just as he made that decision, Miss Oknodie popped the stone she had just picked up into her mouth and swallowed it.
This young lady has really done it now.


* * *


"What are you doing?"
"Mmph?!"
"Spit it out right now."

The butler who appeared from nowhere grabbed my back and patted it firmly, but I'm not one to spit things out so easily.
Gulp!
Like a snake swallowing its prey, I quickly swallowed the Stat Stone, which disappeared down my throat.
Yes, the Stat Stone.
A stone that increases your abilities when eaten.
My head throbbed, and a familiar notification window appeared.

[You have used a Stat Stone.]
[A random ability has increased by 1.]

In a game, you would just press the "use" button to use a Stat Stone.
But now the game has become reality.
There's no "use" button, no known method to use it.
The only way to use it is to swallow it.

'It's not that I dislike training ground grinding, but I can't give up on night park grinding either.'

Sense of Balance is a useful ability in various situations.
I need to raise it whenever possible.

[You walked on the park curb at night for more than 1 minute.]
[Sense of Balance experience +2]
[Night-prowling experience +1]
[You jumped between shop signs at night for more than 1 minute.]
[Sense of Balance experience +6]
[Night-prowling experience +2]
[You walked on top of a wall with arms outstretched at night for more than 1 minute.]
[Sense of Balance experience +4]
[Night-prowling experience +1]

A veteran player route with amazing efficiency that provides both skill experience and stat increases!
I also need to find Stat Stones whenever possible.

'It's rare to know all the spawn locations of Stat Stones.'

Four or five places.
Stat Stones appear in certain fixed locations, with their exact position varying based on probability.
If you don't know all the spawn locations, you might miss some, and if you arrive too late, someone else might have taken them, or the object might have been destroyed.

'I appreciate the butler supervising my training, but I can't give up on easily obtained Stat Stones!'

Though I never expected to literally swallow them raw.

"Why on earth did you swallow a stone?"
"It's... it's a secret..."
"Don't I provide you with meals at every mealtime?"
"I'm sorry..."
"The Master would be saddened if he knew about this behavior, Miss Oknodie."

From an NPC's perspective who doesn't know about Stat Stones, it must look like a perfectly normal person suddenly went crazy and came out at night to swallow stones.
...I must really look insane. I should make some excuse.

"I had no choice. If I don't eat them, I won't be able to survive going forward."

Stat deficiency is a serious matter.
This game has no ability restrictions.
You can pick up and hold any item.
You just pay the price for insufficient abilities with your body, through curses, or by getting torn apart in actual combat.
Stats need to be raised proactively to avoid problems later.
Perhaps my desperate feelings were conveyed somehow.
The butler's stern expression changed, and he gaped as if he'd been hit on the back of the head, then turned away.

"I'll provide you with more food starting tomorrow."
"Wow. Really?"
"In exchange, please never fill your stomach with stones again."

This treatment is a bit concerning.
He's treating me like an impoverished child eating mud cookies in a third-world country.
Even though it's a misunderstanding, I can't convince him otherwise.

"...I'll try."

All I can say is that I'll eat less in the future.
Unsatisfied with my response, the butler's gaze remains piercing.
I feel sorry for the butler.
But I can't promise not to eat them when he looks at me like that.
The world is vast, and there are many Stat Stones to eat!
