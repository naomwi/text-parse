# Shining Character

As the team competition approached, the morale of the two teams couldn't have been more different.

"Repeat after me: 'We're dead.' I'll beat you so badly you won't even be able to surrender."
"Just try it. I'll make you wet your pants."

The Imperial faction students hurled venomous words like pufferfish in season, bristling with hostility.
Thanks to them, the morale of the Frontier faction students hit rock bottom, despite having initially hoped they might stand a chance due to the mysterious terrorist attack that had injured the Imperial students.

"What should we do, Oknodie? Their spirit hasn't been dampened at all."
"Hmph. It doesn't matter. I want to vent my frustrations too."

Winner-stays-on.
A competition where the winning team's player remains until defeated, while the losing team must continuously send new challengers until they win.
Depending on how it's played, a few could utterly destroy many.

<Winner-Stays-On Event>
The class competition finally begins!
In this brutal rule where winners continue to defeat opponents, greater rewards await those who make their presence known.
Take the vanguard position and defeat many enemies!
Boldly step forward to defeat the enemy's ace!
Become your team's last bastion in a crisis and fight valiantly!
Or perhaps you'll be slaughtered like a civilian and retire quickly.
What will you show us?

<Last Bastion>.
Being the final player in the winner-stays-on competition!

"Dorothy. Go out as our vanguard."

Tempted by the offer, Dorothy eagerly joined the battle.
Even if I don't eat it myself, delicious rewards should go to friends.

Wooooo━!

The lamp in Professor Weird's hand changed from green to yellow to red, signaling the start of combat.
Along with the blaring alarm, Professor Rave added his voice.

"Begin."

An Imperial student took the initiative.

"I'll humiliate you from the start. Triple Magic Bullet!"
"Is that all?"

Dorothy, who had built up her physical strength and agility through training, easily dodged the attacks with a sidestep.
Before the startled Imperial student could cast another spell, Dorothy's magic bound their feet.

"Vine Binding!"

Category - Nature Magic
Activation - Vine Creation
Specialization - Binding

A 2-circle binding spell.
Though its growth speed and durability were lacking, it was sufficient to momentarily trip an opponent and hinder their movement.
As the Imperial student missed their timing to cast a spell, Dorothy's hand sprouted a long <Vine Thorn> right in front of them.

"Aack!"
"Want to get stabbed, or surrender?"
"I... surrender..."

"Winner, Dorothy."

"Woooah!"
"How did you do that?"
"Wow! I didn't believe in you! How did you just do that!?"

Despite all the praise pouring in from those who had skipped training and treated the class as disposable, Dorothy felt no joy.
Instead, she only felt disgust.
I didn't want your praise.

"It's just one win."
"Thirty-two more to go before we reach thirty-three."

Sandcooker spoke with an unpleasant tone as if claiming a victory he'd been promised, while Rogini gave off an air of "Surely you're not this excited over something so trivial?"
The comrades who had trained together were like climbing vines supporting each other on a wall.
Just as they could catch up with Professor Weird's notorious "Are you done writing?" lectures by dividing sections among themselves, they could overcome seemingly insurmountable tests by working together.

'This is right, isn't it, Professor Weird? Oknodie.'

Professor Weird made a short "Oh" of admiration with an inscrutable blank face.
Oknodie smiled brightly and gave a thumbs up, indicating she was doing well.

"Winner, Dorothy."
"Winner, Dorothy."
"Winner, Dorothy."

Dorothy continued her winning streak in successive battles.
She dodged enemy spells and landed all of her own.
Having mastered the basics, Dorothy avoided all the demonstration-like spells that screamed "Look what magic I learned!"

"Tsk. Unbearable. This is why commoners are hopeless."

From among the Imperial students, a student with slicked-back white hair and an arrogant expression stepped forward holding a short staff.
An unusual hair color.
Oknodie's eyes showed a "Well, well" kind of emotion.
Dorothy realized what that reaction meant.
A formidable opponent had appeared.
This exam, this would be the critical moment.

"Hugo Mayonnaise. Let me show you the power of the special magic of the Mayonnaise family, a baronial house of the Central Holy Empire that has served in the Magic Command for generations."

As the battle began, Dorothy tried to approach by moving sideways in a circular pattern.
Unlike students who hastily cast spells, Hugo took his time with incantation.

"Ugh...!"

She was trapped.
For Dorothy, who had been winning with minimal magic due to her inability to handle high-circle spells and limited mana, the enemy's powerful magic was a shortcut to defeat.
The opponent seemed to have predicted this psychology and ostentatiously began incanting.

'Even so, I can just cancel it!'

The stone she threw with all her might missed as Hugo simply stepped aside.
Was it coincidence?
Yes, it must be coincidence.
Trying to suppress her anxiety, Dorothy threw three more stones in succession.
She had to admit it.
This guy knows how to move!

"That's not fair. Having good movement on top of that magic skill!"
"Mayo Sphere."

Eight cream-colored viscous spheres, dyed in Mayonnaise's distinctive color, appeared floating in the air simultaneously.

"Ugh, that's disgusting."
"What kind of color is that?"
"Eww. It's wobbling so much."

Unlike the students expressing disgust, Dorothy felt anxious watching the slowly approaching spheres.
A spell that took so much time to incant couldn't possibly be so easy to avoid.
There must be some trick to it.

"Hiyah!"

She threw a stone at one of the spheres, but instead of popping, it simply reformed its shape after collapsing.

'Ugh. It recovers its form just like a slime.'

Dorothy nimbly widened the distance, rotating more broadly to target Hugo Mayonnaise's back.
However, the multiple spheres numbered eight in total.
Four were continuously chasing her, while the remaining four protected Hugo's surroundings.

'Hesitating will only get me surrounded!'

She needed to break through while there were fewer spheres.

"Vine Thorn!"

Dorothy pierced a sphere with her vine thorn, which had sharpened like a nail, splitting it sideways.

"Got you."
"Kyaah!?"

The mayonnaise sphere burst with a pop.
The scattered mayonnaise left white stains on Dorothy's uniform.

"Eww. What is this? It's sticky and I can barely see!"
"Kekeke. This is the special magic created by our family ancestor, Lord Hentai, who was inspired by slimes. Once you're covered in mayo, there's no escape."

With slowed movement and limited vision, Dorothy allowed the approach of new spheres due to the sticky mayonnaise clinging to her uniform.
Covered in more and more of the white, murky liquid, she couldn't display her specialty of light movements and fell.

"Winner, Hugo Mayonnaise."

Dorothy's winning streak ended.

"Ugh. It smells weird."
"I really hate this. Don't come over here."
"What is that strange liquid anyway?"

Despite her great contribution, the students obviously avoided Dorothy.
