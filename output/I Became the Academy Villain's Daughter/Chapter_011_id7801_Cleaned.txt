<11 - The Secret of Passing>

The next day.
Michael's eyebrows twitched as he opened the menu as usual.
His eyes swept through the inn with an incredulous expression.

It's me, me!
I wrote that!!

As I was sending signals with my eyes, Michael finally met my gaze for the first time.
You got it, right?
You shouldn't mistake it for someone else's menu and go to the wrong person, okay?
I firmly expressed my intention with intensity in my eyes.
Whether my message got through or not.
The corner of Michael's mouth turned up slightly.

Look at this guy?

That was the emotion in his response.
It was evidence that I had succeeded in capturing Michael's interest, just as I had with the monkey beastman.

"This is the last rare dish you'll receive with your meal voucher."
"Thank you, Mr. Gisele."
"You've been doing nothing but training and eating rare dishes for five days with your hand on the menu. Are you really sure this is okay?"
"Of course it's okay! Today will be the decisive day."

Since I've finished all the rare dishes.
In the late hours as the sun was setting.
While I was stretching in the backyard dyed crimson, Senior Examiner Michael showed his precious face at the entrance to the yard.
His red-tinged hair, befitting his in-game nickname "Young Master Michael," shone quite beautifully in the crimson light.
If only he weren't a man, it would have been nice. What a shame.

"Night special menu, a sword duel with Oknodie. How bold of you to put up such an audacious menu."

I was proud of the detail of specifically setting the time at night to avoid catching the eyes of other guests during the day.

"And with the limited condition of a night special menu, you've been finishing your training and going to bed early every evening. How very vexing."
"Children need to sleep early to grow taller."
"This is the first time I've had to wait five days for a menu. Having made me wait this long, I won't let you pass easily. Be prepared to fail if you don't satisfy me."

The duration is also a key element of this hidden strategy.
Michael, who had hinted at difficulty levels through the 'period' until ingredients arrived.
According to his rules, the night special menu that had entered its fifth day corresponded to difficulty level 5, equivalent to the 5th layer of Mt. Shinjeong.
The monkey beastman.
This is one level higher than the 4th layer mission he had challenged.

"Even that hairy beastman stopped at challenging the 4th layer. Don't you think aiming for the 5th day was arrogant?"

Michael pointed that out too.
I know.
How a small but strong kid like me might appear to others.

'Probably like a glass cannon that could break at any moment.'

That's why I was especially careful.
I paid attention to managing my body so that I wouldn't appear weak from exhaustion.
Thanks to that, my condition is perfect.
My body feels particularly light today.
Like a long-term prisoner who's thrown off a metal armor and gained freedom!

"Won't we know my skills when our swords meet?"

Michael laughed "hoot" at my bold provocation.
It's too late to regret now.
With those words, swords flashed.

Senior Examiner Michael.
He had been interested in Oknodie and Butler Jonna from the moment he first saw them.

'They're skilled.'

Neither the young lady nor the butler was ordinary.
Especially the butler's strength was extraordinary, difficult to overlook even for a senior examiner like himself.
Footsteps that made no sound at all.
Movements that controlled even the rustling of clothes.
It was like witnessing a professional assassin.

'Could he be a precious child from an assassination family?'

Unlike the butler, the young lady didn't seem to have trained in professional assassination techniques.
That made it even more strange.
Unless she was a noble lady or a wealthy man's daughter, she couldn't employ someone of such skill, but her posture and aura didn't match that of an ordinary daughter.
Adventurer's attire.
That outfit, which might look ridiculous on a woman, had an aura that didn't feel like a joke.

'Whatever the case, she has talent.'

Such achievement at this age.
He knew as soon as they crossed swords.
Blocking his attack wasn't something just anyone could do, even with a wooden sword.
Tremendous strength and excellent technique.
Plus analytical skills to understand the principles of the ticket exam and creativity to apply them.
With this alone, she could challenge at least up to the beginning of the 3rd layer.

'You've mastered all the basics.'

Seeing her flawless swordsmanship, his evaluation went up.
The beginning of the 4th layer was also possible.
At the decisive moment, he strongly shook his real sword.

"You pass."
"Already?"

As Oknodie had guessed, in the conversation between Michael and the innkeeper, the number of days needed for cooking ingredients to arrive hinted at the 'challenge difficulty level.'
By deliberately dragging it out for 5 days, Oknodie had naturally challenged a 5th layer level difficulty!

"But I haven't shown my full abilities yet."

Despite Oknodie's plan to demonstrate 5th layer level capabilities by bringing out wand magic, Young Master Michael declared her pass after she showed 4th layer level capabilities.

"I've given you passing marks in five areas: strength, technique, analytical skills, creativity, and swordsmanship. Technique, analytical skills, and swordsmanship from this duel. Creativity I knew from the menu."
"And strength?"
"Arm wrestling. Rumors were circulating about a superhumanly strong girl."
"Ah."
"Here's your reward. I never thought I'd be giving this ticket to a challenger."

A platinum-colored platinum ticket.
A ticket not usually granted to ordinary challengers.
Swoosh.
Michael raised his arm high, avoiding Oknodie's hand reaching for the ticket.
He thought her sullen gaze was quite amusing as he spoke.

"I'll give you a chance before you receive the ticket. The platinum ticket is one that can give you great merit in the entrance exam. Instead, if you give up this ticket, I'll give you ten gold tickets."
"!!"
"I can't tell you the exact difference, but gold tickets have their own advantages too. You can dispose of the remaining 9 tickets however you wish. Now, what will you do?"

It was a kind of additional test conducted on Michael's whim.
The value of a platinum ticket is enormous.
Even greater than ten gold tickets combined.
Still, the temptation would be hard to resist.
Most tickets traded on the market are bronze tickets.
Or even iron tickets, which are even less valuable.
Iron tickets are fake tickets distributed by examiners.
They're for unqualified youngsters.
If they realize it later and secure another ticket before submission, they might still be able to take the entrance exam, but if they had the ability to figure that out, they could have obtained a ticket on their own in the first place.

'Of course, this young lady has more than enough skill for that. What matters is desire.'

10 gold coins. The average market price of a bronze ticket.
100 gold coins. The average market price of a silver ticket.
Bronze tickets might require an impromptu test before the main exam depending on the number of applicants gathered at the entrance exam site, or they might result in immediate disqualification at a certain ratio.
Silver is a ticket that belongs to the "safe zone" that exempts one from such immediate disqualification.
Gold is a ticket that has even greater "advantages" than that.
Naturally, those who know its value would buy it even for ten times the price.
Power players who want to gain the reputation of having their children or successors admitted to Gift Academy would pay a fortune for gold tickets.
However, no one can buy a platinum ticket with money.
Those who are qualified to obtain that ticket do not commit the foolish act of selling their opportunity for mere material greed.

"Obviously the single one."
"Why? The tickets could be worth money."
"Do I look like I'm short on money?"
"Not really. Just employing that butler would have easily cost over 100 platinum coins."

On the contrary, examiners are unpaid volunteers.
Such troublesome work that doesn't even pay is usually not taken on unless one owes a debt to the Academy or is an eccentric.
Michael is clearly eccentric.
He has a hobby of testing others.
Most of those being tested usually grind their teeth as they fall for his tricks, but sometimes there are those who pass his difficult tests.

'And for those who pass the test, there exists a reward as a counterbalance.'

If she had hesitated or deliberated even a little, he would have handed over the ticket he was holding now.
But this child didn't.
So she passed the test.
Instead of the platinum ticket in his hand, he handed her another platinum ticket he took out from his inner pocket.

"Is there a difference between the same tickets?"
"The one just now was number 143. This one is number 15."
"!"
"All tickets have assigned numbers. Triple numbers are worth less than double numbers, and double numbers are worth less than single numbers."

"Thank you. It was an interesting test."
"Wait. Your name?"
"Oknodie. And yours, Examiner?"
"Michael."
