# Enhanced Translation: Chapter 7 - Between Superhuman Strength and Frailty

With one hand holding the butler, and the other hand holding the maid.
As the three of us walk side by side holding hands, we receive warm glances from all directions.
Perhaps due to my small stature, we must look like a young couple out with their child.

"Ah, to be young and in love."
"Mount Shinjeong for a honeymoon? Must be confident in their abilities."
"A butler and a maid. Must be a noble-employed couple who were former adventurers."

The comments I overhear are quite amusing.

"How do you two feel about this?"
"To be mistaken as the husband of a beautiful maid like Miss Leape, I consider it an honor."
"...I also consider it an honor."

Perhaps because their emotions are genuine, I feel increasing pressure on my hands with each word.
I understand they're happy, but my hands are starting to hurt. I wish they'd both show some restraint.

"The waiting line is quite long. Shall we wait?"
"Please go enjoy yourselves for a week. I'll hold our place."

Our maid has a talent for making people feel guilty.
Leaving just the maid among these rough men waiting for the exam while we go off to play for a week?
That would make me look like complete trash.

"Please prepare a table."
"What do you have in mind?"
"I'm going to wager one gold coin on an arm-wrestling contest."

If I win them all, I save a week's wait for free.
I'm kind of a genius, aren't I?

"...What will be the subject of the wager?"
"Arm wrestling."

I may be a frail young lady who can barely hold a cup or utensils at mealtime, but that's only after exhausting myself with intense training.
After spending two days in a carriage, my body feels light as air, so arm wrestling should be nothing.
On the outside: a delicate, frail young lady.
On the inside: incredible initial investment in strength stats.
If you judge by appearances and challenge me, you'll regret it.

"Please be careful not to break it. The table is borrowed for customer attraction events, so you'll have to compensate if it breaks."
"Yes~"

Thinking it might help their business, the inn staff agreed to promote the arm-wrestling event after hearing the butler's proposal.

"Come one, come all! This isn't an opportunity that comes every day! Beat this wealthy adventurer lady in arm wrestling and win one gold coin! The entry fee is your inn waiting ticket, which must be earlier than the lady's number!"

At the inn tout's call, people who had been waiting in line swarmed like clouds.

"Hey, mister. Is that for real?"
"Look at her. She's tiny."
"Me first! I want to go first!"
"Move it! I was here first."

The promotional effect was undeniable.
Throb.
With a slight headache, a familiar window appears.

<Arm Wrestling Surprise Event>
People with dollar signs in their eyes are flocking to you.
Of course, you have no intention of paying a single penny.
You won't have to give away the gold coin if you never lose.
Compared to the Ticket Exam, this is a piece of cake!
Right?

An event that appears based on the player's proactive actions.
The more events appear, the better, considering the modest rewards that come with clearing them.

'Winning events like this builds reputation.'

Reputation. Fame. Or titles.
These are always beneficial to collect.
They provide ability score bonuses of at least 1 point each as passive effects, and when these points accumulate, they lead to stat increases.
Since there's no status window, I can't see the exact numbers visually.
But with my veteran gamer instincts, I can estimate the expected values and how much they actually increase.

'Girl of Superhuman Strength, passive effect +2 Strength. Something like that.'

The young man who became my first honored opponent raises his arm with an "Alright!" and looks delighted.
Judging by the sighs spreading around us, everyone already thinks the young man has won.

"Ready... 3, 2, 1. Begin."

As soon as the butler raises his hands covering both our fists, the young man pulls with all his might.

"Nnnnggghhhh!"
"Hahaha, what's he doing?"
"Don't hold the pretty girl's hand too long."
"Look at the butler's face. He's going to get hit."
"Is that guy a pedophile?"

Unlike the young man whose face has turned bright red, I feel nothing special.
After completing all the title challenges at the training ground, there's no way I'd lose to such a common adventurer.

The mechanism of ability score increases works like this:
From 1 to 9, 1 point per 1 increase.
From 11 to 19, 2 points per 1 increase.
From 21 to 29, 3 points per 1 increase.
And so on, until from 91 to 99, 10 points per 1 increase.
For ability scores at multiples of 10 like 10, 20, 30, you need to collect points equal to that number for 1 increase.

Starting with a Strength of barely 20 and completing title challenges, my estimated current Strength according to veteran calculations is 29.
Converting Strength 29 to points, it's at least 75 points.
A body that was Strength 20 (47 points) has gained over 27 points to park at ability score 29.

Sword practice 1,000 times, Strength +1 point.
Archery practice 1,000 times, Strength +1 point.
Martial arts practice 1,000 times, Strength +1 point.

At 10,000 times, another +1 point each.
At 100,000 times, another +1 point each.
At 1,000,000 times, another +1 point each.

Practice counts are tallied by individual actions like one slash, one swing, one arrow shot.
Doing 10,000 repetitions of one action per day, with three actions per martial art, already adds up to 90,000, and when moving to advanced actions, I easily hit 100,000 daily training repetitions.

Having practiced consistently for 30 days, my current total is 3 million repetitions.
Getting 3 points 4 times equals 12 points.
Add another 3 points for martial arts practice points that count all types of martial arts training regardless of whether it's swordsmanship, archery, or hand-to-hand combat.
12 more points from achieving things like "Destroying Practice Dummies," "Gripping the Bow Until Your Hands Bleed," and "Bursting the Sandbag."
That makes a total of 27 Strength points from titles.
How could this rookie young man possibly handle that?
Not a chance.

THUD!

"The lady wins!"
"Argh!"
"What an idiot."
"How little strength training must he have done?"
"Hey, try it yourselves before talking! That's not a girl's arm strength!"
"Booooo!"
"Get out of here!"

The young man blushes and disappears amid the jeers.
The staff member who sees the waiting ticket the young man wagered announces:

"Number 925!"

Is 925 even a reasonable number for an inn waiting line?
For a normal inn that accepts long-term guests, it would take not just a week but a whole month.
The turnover rate has improved thanks to people leaving after taking tests from the senior examiner, reducing the wait time to a week.
If not for the examiner, such a line wouldn't have formed in the first place.
From the innkeeper's perspective, it's a temporary boom.

"The next participant must have a number before 925! Hey, you with the big head. 920? Who are you trying to fool! You, the bald guy! 850? Come on up!"

The second challenger is a bald man.
I tensed up a bit at the appearance of the bald man.

'Bald guys are dangerous. They might have lost their hair from intense training.'

Even casually practicing 30,000 movements of one martial art per day, after 10 years that's 109.5 million repetitions.
That's five score increases at the 10,000, 100,000, 1,000,000, 10,000,000, and 100,000,000 levels.
Plus, whether it's swordsmanship, martial arts, or archery, martial arts practice counts are calculated together, so that's five additional points.
Adding incidental point acquisitions in between, that's at least 20 more points.

'A total of 30 points. Even with an average innate Strength of 10 for a normal person, they could reach Strength 19.'

If the training period is longer, they've mastered more than one martial art, and their innate power is just a bit greater...
There's a small possibility that someone could appear with skills matching my Strength ability score of 29.

THUD!

"The lady wins!"

Well, this bald man was just someone with early-onset natural hair loss.
After winning 9 consecutive victories and advancing my number to 97, people in the waiting line began to realize my strength was extraordinary.

"Where does such strength come from in that delicate body?"
"Has she mastered the mana cultivation method I've only heard rumors about?"
"She looks like a rich young lady, so that might be the case."
"Tsk, played again by the amusements of the wealthy."
"But shouldn't she be tired by now?"

A super-strong petite girl under 140cm flipping over the bulging forearms of grown men?
People who rushed in excited by this unimaginable event are even placing side bets, leaving the innkeeper's mouth hanging open.
The inn is holding the money to prevent fraud, taking a 10% commission.

"Mr. Innkeeper."
"Haha, our little lucky charm. Would you like a bucket of water? No, I'll even treat you to lunch!"
"Never mind that. Let's split it 50-50."
"Huh? W-what are you saying~"
"I saw you taking commissions."

When I narrowed my eyes and spoke firmly, the innkeeper replied with a face that looked like he was dying of frustration.

"30-70."
"If you don't split it evenly, I'll report you to the lord. For using a delicate young girl as a means to profit from illegal gambling."

The innkeeper, who looked like he had a lot to say about which part of this super-strong girl was "delicate," flinched.
Behind me, Jonna was openly intimidating the innkeeper with a fierce gaze, pressuring him to choose his words carefully.

'My butler, with stats much higher than mine, is no ordinary butler.'

There have been times when I complained about food that couldn't even be collected, but I gave up and just ate it because I was overpowered.
The long hours of humiliation and oppression when I had to eat spinach roast and stir-fried cucumber ten times, which couldn't even be collected for cooking collection—I don't want to remember that anymore.

"I'll give it to you..."
"Hehe. I earned some pocket money."

The more money, the better.

"Miss. You've exerted a lot of strength. Shall we rest for today?"
"Hmm... so we're staying out tonight after all?"

Darn, that's disappointing.
I wanted to get in today somehow.
Still, I don't want to push too hard with Oknodie's body that becomes a frail young lady (exhausted) once tired.
Unlike the training ground, this is a public street.
It's not a place where I can rest comfortably and freely show weakness.

"Someone as frail as you must rest sufficiently when you need to."

The people in the waiting line were bewildered, as if they'd heard something crazy.

"What part of that superhuman strength is frail?"
"She's a complete monster girl."

Something went "thunk" into the wooden post next to the two grumbling adventurers.

"I apologize. Being unfamiliar with maid work, I accidentally threw my self-defense dagger."

The adventurers trembled and gulped.
Leape went to retrieve the dagger and whispered something to the adventurers. One collapsed as his legs gave out, while the other fled in terror.

"Miss Leape. What did you just say?"
"I told them the butler is ten times stronger than me."
"Wow."

Ten times Leape is Jonna.
So Leape is one-tenth of Jonna?

"So Miss Leape isn't that incredibly strong after all."
"..."
"Would you like me to teach you how to exercise sometimes?"

The candy I ate that night tasted unusually bitter.
The next day, I had an upset stomach.
