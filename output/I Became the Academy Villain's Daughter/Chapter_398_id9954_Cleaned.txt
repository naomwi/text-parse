# <398 - Baby Kraken>

Dobi's fighting style was unique, but its limitations were clear.

'A one-trick meta that inflicts fear status effects but becomes completely useless against opponents immune to doomsday prophecies!'

The power of his ability depends on how detailed and terrifying the apocalypse he describes is, and how deeply he etches that terrifying moment into his soul.
But Dobi had indirectly experienced moments of apocalypse so intense that survival would normally be impossible through the player's memories, making his power quite formidable.

"Creatures with even fragments of reason cannot maintain their sanity before me, who serves Lady Oknodie. Please rest assured and accept my assistance!"

Though it felt a bit unsettling, his help was undeniably useful.
I decided to just accept it comfortably.

"Back away!"
"Get lost!"
"Do you wish to be reduced to a puddle of blood!"

It was a pitiful sight to watch summoned creatures—which senior students had likely spent fortunes to find in the summoning registry, prepare catalysts for summoning magic circles, choose auspicious days to increase summoning probability, conduct summoning rituals at appropriate locations, and offer tributes to increase contract success rates—breaking their contracts in real-time and fleeing back to their original dimensions.

'The seniors are so pitiful, I should finish my business and return quickly!'

Passing through the corridor, I saw a ladder leading to a hatch in the ceiling in front of a room filled with water like an aquarium.
Climbing up the ladder slowly, I found a warning sign in front of the hatch.

━━━
WARNING!
This isolation chamber houses a <Baby Kraken> captured for experimental purposes.
Please be aware that no one will take responsibility for any accidents that occur if you enter without sufficient underwater protection measures.
━━━

A warning message that instills fear like entering an anaconda's cage that swallows anything that moves!
Even the fearless Dobi, who couldn't produce anything but bubbles underwater, lacked the courage to follow me in. He guarded the entrance and said solemnly:

"I cannot breathe underwater."
"Normally newbies learn by filling their lungs with water!"
"..."
"Hehe. I'm joking, so relax. You've been helpful enough today, so I'll let you off."
"I'm grateful for your mercy..."
"Then please watch my clothes from here!"

As I unbuttoned my uniform, Dobi suddenly looked around in great embarrassment and flustered.

"S-suddenly doing this is troublesome. How can a man and woman in such a place!"
"I'm wearing a swimsuit underneath, you know?"
"S-still!"

Hehe.
Perhaps because I was so flustered by Dobi earlier, I felt satisfied seeing him turn his head away with a reddened face as soon as I started unfastening my skirt.
Now we're even!

"Will you guard my backpack and clothes well?"
"I will protect them with my life."

What I was wearing under my uniform was the school swimsuit issued during Professor Platon's class in the first semester!
The sensation of the school swimsuit clinging tightly to my body still felt awkward, but I tried to shake off the unfamiliar feeling by recalling how I sometimes wore bikini armor-like equipment for stat boosts when playing male characters.
Aside from the unfamiliarity of the clothes, there were no difficulties.
While entering the Baby Kraken's tank might be dangerous, I, who had diligently collected entries for my guidebook, wouldn't be deterred by such adversity.

━━━
Underwater Breathing +30 minutes
Water Affinity 45% (Underwater Movement Penalty reduced by 45%)
Underwater Additional Damage +55%
Underwater Field of Vision expanded by 2 levels
Title <Natural Enemy of the Giant King Crab> activated
━━━

Various underwater buffs activating one after another as soon as I entered the water!
This is when diligent guidebook collection pays off.

Bubble bubble.

The oxygen wasted unnecessarily scattered as bubbles outside my mouth, but it didn't matter.
For the 30 minutes that underwater breathing was granted.
As long as this time persisted, oxygen would naturally be transmitted into my body as if I were on land.

Splash splash.

Perhaps sensing movement in the water?
Long tentacles extended from the bottom of the submerged isolation chamber, targeting prey.

<Dark Electricity Generation>

Zzzap.

"Muoooong!"
"Ouch, that stings."

I wasn't electrocuted from recklessly discharging electricity underwater.
Dark mana doesn't harm those with the same attribute, especially not the caster who controls the attribute.
It was because the dense mana contained in the ink that the startled kraken momentarily discharged had penetrated and broken the dark mana's bonds, changing its attribute.

"Oh come on. Stop struggling. I came to take you to a good place, why are you acting like this!"
"Muoooong!!"

The Baby Kraken extended more legs.
It was thoroughly angered and beyond persuasion.
Was it because it thought I was food but I wasn't?
Or because it was suddenly stung by electricity?
No, that wasn't it.
The Baby Kraken was trapped in this isolation chamber, in a senior's laboratory, because it had been deceived by a senior.

[You have instinctive inspiration for animal language with various vocalizations (37).]
[Required vocalization ability for inspiration success - 500]

(x0.1 Adjustment List)
[Situation Assessment (199) function bonus: 19.9%]
[Thinking Ability (144) function combat intelligence bonus: 14.4%]
[Social Life (75) function perceptiveness bonus: 7.5%]

(x0.2 Adjustment List)
[Speech (82) function eloquence bonus: 16.4%]
[Mimicry (45) function body language bonus: 9%]
[Playfulness (22) function gentleness bonus: 4.4%]

(Special Adjustment List)
[Experience making kraken sounds: 25%]

[Total requirement reduction bonus: 96.6%]
[Required vocalization ability: 17]

[Your vocalization function is 17 or higher.]
[You can understand and communicate with the Baby Kraken's sounds as if they were human language.]

Phew. As expected, function experience points never betray me.
The last bastion that protects the player even when facing unfair situations!

"I won't be fooled again. Human liar. Said if I followed, I'd get 1 ton of sardines, 1 ton of anchovies, and 100 grams of sugar daily, but only gave cultured meat and sleeping powder."
"Oh my, that's terrible!"
"Huh...? Human, you understand kraken sounds...?"
"Of course! I'm a friend to animals!"
"Can't believe it. How can I trust you?"

You don't need to trust me.
You just need to trust the blessing I have.

━━━
*Guide of Salvation* title equipped.
Blessing <Infinite Respect> activated.
━━━
*Guide of Salvation*: Having saved the world from destruction multiple times, you have become the world's hope.
-Title Equip Effect: Activates blessing <Infinite Respect>
-Title Possession Effect: +10 basic favorability from all things in the world
━━━
*Infinite Respect (Blessing)*: When the blessing is activated, all things in the world express respect toward you.
━━━

As I activated the blessing, the Baby Kraken lowered its sharp wariness.

"But you smell delicious. You've eaten Giant King Crab before?"
"Yes!"
"If you're a friend who knows good food, I have no reason to dislike you. At least you're much better than humans who give tasteless cultured meat."

While the actual conversation consists of "muoooong" sounds, our communication proceeds smoothly!
The Baby Kraken, no longer aggressive, extended its tentacles with gentle movements to softly wrap around my body.

"Wow, now that I look at you, you're really small? Even smaller than me, a baby. You're totally tiny tiny."
"That's right. That's why I'm dissatisfied. I want to grow quickly to 2m30cm! I temporarily stopped growing to pump up my strength."
"You're really cute, aren't you? You can't survive the harsh deep-sea life with just a 2m30cm body. You should aim for at least 230m."
"You were tricked by the promise of 1 ton of sardines and 1 ton of anchovies as offerings because you wanted to grow bigger?"
"That's right."
"Is sugar also necessary for growing bigger?"
"Adding sugar makes the water sweet and good for cleansing the palate."

Through the glass wall, Dobi is watching us, unable to hide his confusion.
For some reason, he's looking at me communicating with the kraken with a very red face—I wonder why?

"Anyway, I'll help you get out of here."
"Really?"
"Once you touch seawater, mother kraken can find you."
"The humans here are too strong. It won't be easy."
"It's okay. I have a plan. If you endure this cramped place for just one day, you can escape soon!"

The Baby Kraken wasn't easily persuaded.
Is it because it's a superior being with better racial stats than humans?

"How can I trust what a tiny baby who hasn't even finished infancy says?"

Ah. It's because my size is too small.

"Wait just a moment!"

Coming out of the aquarium and shaking off water like a wet dog, Dobi rushed over.

"Are you alright? To my eyes, it looked like the kraken was half trying to eat you, Lady Oknodie! How could it do such a terrible thing!"
"Huh??"
"Oh, was that not the case?"
"That Baby Kraken is a good kraken. Even though it was deceived by humans and imprisoned, it didn't think about destroying the lab and escaping to kill people indiscriminately!"
"..."
"Just wait a little longer!"

Dobi looked puzzled as I went back down with a curtain I took from my backpack.
The Baby Kraken also stared blankly at the curtain, so I quickly wrapped it around my body and spread the curtain wide.

"Do I look bigger now?"

The Baby Kraken's eyes widened like teddy bear eyes as it watched the curtain fluttering with the water current from the circulation system in the isolation tank.

"The tiny baby has grown as big as a baby!!"

The Baby Kraken, now more trustworthy due to its increased size, promised full cooperation.
