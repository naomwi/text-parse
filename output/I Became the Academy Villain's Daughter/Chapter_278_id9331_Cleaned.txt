# 278 - Summer Vacation with Friends

Whoooo.
The luxurious cruise ship sounded its horn, signaling preparations for departure.
After leaving the Academy and arriving at the dock just in time, Oknodie spotted a familiar stern face in formal attire and ran over with a bright expression.

"Jonna~!"

Jonna caught Oknodie's shoulder with one hand as she ran toward him, spinning her around until her eyes were spinning with dizziness before setting her back on the ground.
Jonna's gaze then turned to the students who had followed behind Oknodie and stopped in their tracks, shocked.

"While I understand your desire to fully enjoy this reunion, please maintain your dignity, My Lady. The position of top scholarship student comes with corresponding responsibilities."
"Hmph. The Academy never required that of me."
"The Foundation does."

Even Hestia expressed admiration at the sight of Jonna handling the mighty Oknodie as easily as if she were a balloon doll.

"What an impressively developed physique. I can sense the compressed muscles bulging beneath that formal suit. That person possesses extraordinary muscles that even mana refinement techniques couldn't fully compress."
"Hahaha! After attending the Academy, I finally understand. That butler was ridiculously strong, wasn't he?"

Son O-cheon belatedly realized the strength of the butler he had encountered before.
Having survived the harsh first semester at the Academy, he could only now vaguely comprehend Jonna's abilities. Jonna's strength was truly extraordinary.

"It's only natural to feel tense, as he might be skilled enough to match a professor. After all, he's a butler of the 'Wiheomhae Foundation.'"

Gisele approached Jonna with a smile, carrying his magic backpack.
He extended his hand in greeting.

"It's been a while. Since the entrance exam, Oknodie has constantly missed you. Even after your visit, she wouldn't stop talking about her butler for quite some time."
"My Lady has been greatly indebted to you. On behalf of the Foundation, I thank you for looking after her within the Academy."

Isabel thought to herself about Gisele's composure as he exchanged greetings so calmly.
Gisele was certainly no ordinary person.
While everyone with combat abilities admired Jonna's strength, and even dangerous individuals like Xing remained cautious, Gisele casually entered Jonna's personal space and offered a handshake.
Such an act required extraordinary nerve.
Still, with Gisele breaking the ice, the others gradually relaxed their tense muscles and lowered their guard.
The infamous Wiheomhae Foundation, known worldwide, appeared to have no hostile intentions toward them, at least for now. If anything, their response seemed closer to hospitality.

"This cruise ship has been chartered entirely by our Foundation and will operate with verified crew members, so please feel free to board without concern."

Unlike everyone else who hesitated to step forward at this not-at-all reassuring statement, Oknodie excitedly asked:

"Is there a chef in the restaurant?"
"Of course."
"Let's hurry and eat! New food!"

As Gisele was pulled along by Oknodie's insistent tugging, Isabel and Son O-cheon followed with amused smiles.
Behind them came Dorothy, Rockfell, Hestia, Xing, Arcadia, Zuang, and the others in succession.


* * *


The first thing that catches her interest on this vast cruise ship is food.
Jonna thought that his Lady's direction of obsession was quite consistent.
The cruise ship was a trap the Chairman often used to identify places driven by someone's desires.
While Oknodie responded to food, Isabel responded to cooking utensils.
Even in the same place, one could determine what interests a person, what tools could win their favor, or conversely, what kind of traps would be effective.
This information collectively has a significant impact on directives the Foundation might issue someday.
Small favors.
Generous favors.
Favors large and small each come with different costs.
The larger the favor shown, the greater the price to be paid.

"I apologize. I've completely lost track of one of your friends, Miss Zuang."
"To evade even the eyes of an assassin maid. As expected of the disciple of the Life Thief, one of the Continental Ten Great Thieves."
"I'll begin searching immediately."
"That won't be necessary. Leave her be. Even being unobservable is valuable information in itself."

While Zuang, extremely sensitive about information leaks, was nowhere to be seen, there were plenty of others who didn't easily enjoy the entertainment, maintaining rigid attitudes.
For instance, the obviously suspicious "fake friends" fell into this category.

"Leape. Are those people truly friends of My Lady?"
"Of course not."

Near the pool at the bow, Oknodie was munching on watermelon while being watched with suspicious eyes by the hero Ishtar.
Obviously, Oknodie and Ishtar were not in a relationship that could be called friendship.
Yet Oknodie had brought her along.
Adding to the Chairman's intention to let her indulge in greed as much as she wanted, she had gone further and brought along everything that wasn't even hers.
From Jonna's perspective, who somewhat understood the Chairman's intentions, it was simply baffling.

"Not bad for a third-rate foundation♡"
"Chartering an entire luxury cruise ship that even our parents' generation would enjoy—such extravagance suggests the Foundation is indeed extraordinary."

Taking it a step further, at the wine bar were the Imperial Princess and the heir of the Three Great Meritorious Families, enjoying cocktails despite being students.
Jonna couldn't help but question the criteria for being Oknodie's friend.

"What constitutes a friend to you, My Lady?"
"Classmates?"
"Is that why you brought 128 friends?"

Oknodie replied with a face full of regret.

"Everyone declined saying it was too much pressure, so I could only gather this many. Papa told me to bring lots of friends, but it's so few that my pride is hurt!"
"Don't worry. No matter how many the Chairman expected, I'm certain it was fewer than 128."

Even 128 is considered few? She's too much of a social butterfly.


* * *


The lower-class students splashing water and giggling are the Dolphin Pants Girls who diligently follow her every morning, aspiring to join the Knight Department.
The female students glaring at the volleyball court from the bench, ready to start a match at the mere sight of a volleyball, are magic department aspirants led by Rogini.
The male boys who circle around the girls, bravely approach them, and then retreat with slumped shoulders are noble administrative department aspirants with time to spare for romance.

'I'm glad I brought so many!'

It puts her at ease seeing students who were stressed from Academy life enjoying themselves.
When Papa suddenly offered free cruise tickets that reduce stress by 50% and increase likability by 10 points per boarding, how could she miss this opportunity?
She invited all first-year students, telling everyone who wanted to come to join, and 128 people, including Dorothy, accepted the invitation.

"How rich is Oknodie's Papa?"
"Hmm... I don't know!"
"So rich that you don't even know his wealth!"

That's not exactly wrong.
I still don't know much about Papa!

"Total tonnage of 200,000 tons. Length of 350m and width of 50m. A cruise ship with 2,000 crew members across 15 decks, casually chartered—that's how wealthy he is."

At Jonna's response—who stayed by her side claiming that serving his Lady was his life's fulfillment—the Tito family's mouth gaped in astonishment.

"You'll catch flies like that?"
"Ah!"

The silly Tito quickly covered his mouth with both hands.
As always, it's fun to tease him.

"What made Papa suddenly ask me to bring friends?"
"He wants to know about your social circle. It seems he'd prefer to see it with his own eyes."
"Ah, so he has special observation abilities!"

Jonna's gaze toward her changed subtly.
It was like the expression of a parent feeling wistful watching a child they thought was immature suddenly showing signs of growth.

"Conversely, what do you think is the reason, My Lady? The cost of using this cruise ship is not cheap at all. It's a considerable expense to lavish on your friends."

There could be several answers.
Perhaps to verify if I've made friends worth inviting and if my judgment meets expectations.
Or to demonstrate overwhelming wealth and provide solid support so I won't have to be conscious of others during Academy life.
But well.
One of the Three Great Continental Evils wouldn't arrange something like this out of mere goodwill or casual curiosity.

"Maybe he wants to play a game where we guess the culprit of a murder?"

Jonna's face stiffened with a serious expression.
Apparently not that.

"Perhaps sealing off cabin areas one by one and giving gifts to students who survive in the last remaining area?"
"...What exactly have you been learning at the Academy?"
"Hmph. Not this either?"
"There's no way he would do such things to mere first-years."
"True. That would be too early, wouldn't it?"

Even the Three Great Evils wouldn't advance the third-year summer vacation event this early.

"Then I guess he's throwing us onto a deserted island to observe our friendship and love before coming back to pick us up."

Though she was just listing potential events in order of likelihood, Jonna looked as shocked as if he'd been hit on the head with a hammer.

"How on earth did you know we're heading to a deserted island?"

Oops.
Right.
How should I explain how I knew?
