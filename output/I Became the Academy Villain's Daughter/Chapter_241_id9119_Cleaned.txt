# 241 - Superhuman Dodgeball

Individual dodgeball is almost like a rehearsal for team dodgeball.
Unlike individual dodgeball where only one first-place winner can earn a maximum of 10 points, in class competition dodgeball, an entire group can earn up to 100 points.
All class competition events invariably have their own "rehearsal events."

'It would help in the class competition if Xing, Hestia, and Mr. Son O-cheon could experience this beforehand!'

That's also why I chose the most painful-looking ball!
Plus, I was curious about Deadcat, a senior from the advanced class I'd never seen before.

'New characters from the advanced class are usually villains!'

A skilled villain senior.
In other words, someone in the second-year Xing or Zuang position.

'I might be a bit disappointed if they go down in one hit?'

I gripped the steel ball firmly and cast multiple spells in rapid succession.
Red, blue, black!
As the ball kept changing colors, Isabel who was watching and Son O-cheon who was participating both froze.

"Here I go!"

Even Xing couldn't catch this strike.
He might be able to deflect it, but catching it would be another story.
Even Hestia would struggle.
If someone tried to catch it with inadequate strength, their palm would be completely shredded by its power.

'Now, Phase 1: Tri-color Ball!'

Surely they can at least block a greeting, right?


* * *


People often mistakenly think knights only use physical attacks and wizards only use magic attacks.
From a master's perspective, that's laughable.
Knights focus on elemental attack power to overcome physical limitations, leaning toward magical attacks.
Wizards strive to make their spells more powerful by focusing on physical attack power to cast faster and heavier.
This means strong knights have high magical attack power, and strong wizards have high physical attack power!

'That's a magic with extremely high physical attack power.'

The steel ball Oknodie threw carried savage magical attack power visible even from the sidelines.
The tri-colored flashing ball was wrapped in crimson flames, something blue, and pitch-black dark mana.
Whatever this dodgeball was, it seemed powerful enough to exorcise even demons.

'Is dodgeball some kind of magical ritual tool used by Imperial Knights for demon exorcism ceremonies?'

Hestia imagined what would happen if that merciless strike came flying at her.
First, her palm would definitely split open.
Next, she would suffer terrible burns.
While reducing the impact of the steel ball and preparing to throw it back, her hand would take extreme damage from the burns.
Without surrounding her palm with considerable mana, she couldn't catch it or throw it back.
Her palm would stick to the steel ball from the burns, unable to separate.
But throwing it hastily would result in minimal power.
A second-year advanced class senior wouldn't go down so easily.
She would need to charge up properly.

'First strike is incredibly important in this match.'

Hestia realized why Oknodie had taken the lowest-scoring position.
She had understood this fact from the beginning.
That wild instinct to instinctively take the most advantageous position in an unfamiliar competition!
A bestial sense no less impressive than the monkey demi-human Son O-cheon.
Oknodie might be a baby, but she was a wild baby.

'But what are the effects of that blue thing and the dark mana?'

The frightening aspect of Oknodie's strike was that you couldn't read all its power just by looking.
How would Senior Deadcat receive such a ball?
Hestia witnessed the answer.

"!!"

Deadcat took the hit with her body!
With a bang, Deadcat's form flew backward.

'Wait, after all that posturing, she goes down in one hit!?'

A defeat so helpless it was bewildering!
But what happened next was even more surprising.
Deadcat's body, which had been flying vertically into the distance, scattered like an afterimage and reappeared in her original position.
She casually caught the ball, which was floating in the air with reduced impact, with one hand.
Only slight fatigue showed in her languid eyes.

"That hurts. Almost as much as Mandela's."
"Wow! Senior, you use such an interesting technique."

Deadcat's gaze fell on the ball spinning in her hand, emitting white smoke.
As if asking, are you really one to talk?
Hestia had one more concern besides this bizarre senior.

"Xing. Why didn't you ambush her?"

Xing, who had seemed ready to swing his sword at the slightest opportunity, remained completely motionless in his stance with his sword drawn.

"...I would have died."
"Died?"
"If I had swung my sword just now. I guarantee I would have died instantly."
"!?"
"Good instincts, junior."

In front of Deadcat's indifferent gaze, everyone's tension rose except for Oknodie.

"It's my turn now."

The steel ball in Deadcat's hand bounced on the floor like a basketball, making thump-thump sounds.
Hestia's eyes widened.

"Property transformation! Everyone be careful. This senior's attack is..."

That was all she managed.
Hestia witnessed the bouncing ball defy physical laws, bouncing toward her at a low angle near her legs.

<Hestia Hammer Technique>
<Pull Strike>

Hestia swung her hammer like hitting a golf ball.
The steel ball, which had looked as light as a rubber ball, collided with the hammer, producing an eardrum-shattering metallic sound as steel met steel.

'Heavy. As I thought, this attack is...!'

Category - Transformation Magic
Activation - Property Transformation
Specialization - Latency, Multiple Manifestation, ???

At least 4-circle property transformation magic.
And she could skillfully manipulate form change's continuous manifestation and timing—an incredible expert.
In an extremely brief moment.
Deadcat's ability manifested at tremendous speed.
Once, while changing it to bounce softly like a rubber ball, adapting her eyes to its up-and-down movement.
Twice, changing its form at the instant of contact with the ground to make it bounce at a nearly horizontal low angle.
Three times, when the hammer and steel ball collided.
As a result, Hestia somehow managed to deflect the steel ball.
But she couldn't catch it.

Swoosh

Hestia was immediately teleported outside the court.
The instructor pointed to the scoreboard to show the score.

━━━
Hestia
In-court survival time: 21 seconds.
Position value: 5 points.
Total: 105 points.
━━━

"Everyone be careful. That senior calculated all the attack timing from a distance!"

Magic consumes extremely high amounts of energy the further it gets from one's body.
Remotely controlling a fast-moving ball like that would be difficult even for a second-year advanced class student.
In other words, from the moment the ball left her palm, it was already a calculated sequence of manifestations.

Swish

It didn't even end with one attack.
The ball that bounced off the hammer hit the wall like a rubber ball and returned to Deadcat's hand.
As if she had predicted Hestia would hit it at that angle.
A fourth transformation changed its angle, and a fifth transformation bounced it back to her hand.
From the moment the ball left her hand, she had calculated everything—Hestia's elimination and even the ball's return.
Hestia felt fear.
She couldn't guarantee she would survive if she faced such a senior with design and calculation abilities in a serious battlefield.

"You're next."

Deadcat's terrifying steel ball now flew toward Son O-cheon.
Irregular angle.
Changing properties.
Hestia knew all its characteristics from her experience.
But does knowing about it mean you can block it...?
To avoid injury, the only option was to deflect it with a weapon.
Deadcat naturally expected that.
But Son O-cheon defied common sense.
Instead of using the long staff he was holding, he caught the ball with both hands.
CRASH!!
Son O-cheon was pushed back, leaving a long groove in the ground.
His elimination was confirmed as he was pushed beyond the white line.

━━━
Son O-cheon
In-court survival time: 30 seconds.
Position value: 5 points.
Total: 150 points.
━━━

"Foolish."

Deadcat said.
If he was going to be eliminated anyway, why suffer injuries that made his hands bleed?
It wasn't rational, she implied.
Son O-cheon didn't deny it.

"I know. It was a foolish thing to do. But what I did wasn't completely useless."

Son O-cheon firmly gripped the ball until the last moment of his elimination.
To prevent the shape transformation Deadcat had planted in the ball from bouncing off the ground and returning to her hands.
As a result, the steel ball completed its shape transformation in Son O-cheon's hands and couldn't return to Deadcat.
Instead, the ball was sucked right into Oknodie's palm, who was closest.

"Hahaha! See? The fruit of my great performance."
"That was amazing, Mr. Son O-cheon!"

What strange magic is that now?
By now, Hestia wasn't even surprised at Oknodie's peculiarity of readily using magic she'd never learned.
She could only hope.

"Give her a taste of her own medicine. That arrogant senior who lives to bully juniors."

As if responding to his wish, the ball in Oknodie's hand began to emit light once more.
From red to blue, to black.
Not stopping at three colors, but continuing.
From gray to brown, to purple.

"This time it's Phase 2: Six-color Ball!"

For the first time, tension appeared on Deadcat's face at the ominous amount of mana contained in the ball.
