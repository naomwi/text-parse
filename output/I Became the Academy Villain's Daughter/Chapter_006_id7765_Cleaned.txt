# 6 - No Skip Carriage Journey

[Training period has ended.]
[All increased training efficiency returns to normal.]

The month-long training was over.
It was a difficult time, but I worked hard since it was an opportunity to gain significant experience with minimal effort.
By actively applying the know-how I knew from player knowledge to increase experience points, the power of my swordsmanship and archery skills improved dramatically.

Maybe I'm a bit strong now?
Before entering the Academy, my swordsmanship and archery are definitely good enough that no one would ever call me inadequate.

"I don't know if Lady Oknodie is aware, but Gift Academy is a facility that nurtures the world's finest talents. Anyone under the age of 20 is eligible to apply."
"Well, I know that much?"
"Then you must also know about the qualification exam that tests one's eligibility to take the entrance exam, given the overwhelming number of applicants."

Of course I do.
The qualification exam is the first test for admission.

<Entrance Exam Qualification Event>
You who wish to enter the continent's premier Academy.
To prove your minimum qualifications to challenge the Academy, you must pass the qualification exam for the entrance exam.
Find an examiner who distributes entrance exam tickets, take the test required by each examiner, and receive a ticket as proof of passing.

If you're on your own, gathering information costs a fortune, and getting there costs even more.
There's a reason people take blood donation jobs, clinical trial jobs, or experimental jobs in back alleys.
To gather money for places like this, you really can't afford to be picky about whether a job is safe or unsafe.
Of course, for the wealthy starter Oknodie with 100 gold coins in support funds, part-time jobs are a distant story.
I just trained hard.

"There are three examiners you can visit in this area. Two are lower examiners with several tickets, and one is a higher examiner with dozens of tickets."
"Then obviously the higher examiner!"
"Such confidence without asking about the difficulty of the test. Are you sure you won't regret it?"

Huh, lower? Intermediate?
Tests from examiners with just one or two tickets, or at most around ten, are content for beginners or intermediate players.
For veteran players like me, places where you only get a 'ticket' are inefficient.

"Where is the higher examiner?"
"At the 'Garden of the Gods,' one of the world's top ten attractions. It's on the list of places that travelers who love wandering the world should visit before they die, at an altitude of 12,500 meters."
"Hmm."
"You're quite confident."
"Don't worry. I'm Oknodie, remember?"
"With that confidence, your success seems guaranteed."

Fortunately, Mt. Shinjeong wasn't too far from the starting point.
The exam locations you can visit are limited by physical proximity.
If you had money to burn, you could travel by airship looking for easier exam locations, but that's burdensome even for the noble papa route that starts with 100 gold coins.
Maybe if it were the great noble route with 1,000 gold coins.

'There are shortcuts though.'

There's also the method of flexing gold coins to buy tickets, but it's just an entrance exam.
The market price for a ticket is 10 gold coins.
It's too frustrating to spend a fortune while risking getting fake tickets.
The price for a guaranteed safe ticket is 100 gold coins.
That would require giving up all my support funds.
As a veteran player, that's out of the question.

'This round, my skill training went well.'

Thanks to the butler's care, I could focus solely on training, greatly improving my swordsmanship and archery.
Thanks to using the maid for training, my hiding and stealth skills also showed remarkable improvement.

"I must emphasize again that wand magic is sealed until you enter the Academy. Do you understand?"
"Yes~"

Instead of the long name "qualification exam for the entrance exam," players and NPCs call it the Ticket Exam.
This exam, where luck runs wild, naturally has probability-based luck elements.

'Surprisingly, the examiner isn't random.'

Instead, the test given by the examiner is random.
What test they'll give can only be known after arriving at the scene and having the examiner present it.

'If it's Mt. Shinjeong's examiner... it won't be trivial.'

The Ticket Exam, I'm starting to look forward to it a bit.


* *


"Are you sure this luggage is sufficient?"
"This is enough."

The butler looked at my bag with disapproval.
A standard adventurer's set filled with preserved food, adventure tools, and first aid supplies.
Along with weapons to protect myself.
This was all I had.
He probably thinks these items don't suit a "young lady."
But I have lines I won't cross.

"Things like teddy bear pillows or cumbersome ribbon headbands. Those inexplicable lady-specific items are just annoying and extra baggage."
"The master will be sad."
"Hmph. I can train hard, but I refuse anything that suits a girl's taste."

Though my body became a girl's, my mind is still a man's.
Though I was possessed in a game world after being gender-swapped overnight, I have no intention of living like a real girl.
Before being a girl, I'm a player.
That identity is more important to me.
Because it's the only way left to remember who I was when I was a man.
The result is extreme efficiency.
A cold basic outfit with no consideration for appearance whatsoever, the Adventurer's Set (F).

"It suits you well, Miss."
"Thank you, Leape."

Unlike the butler who only speaks properly, maid Leape only says pleasant things.
Because of this, I tend to rely on her often, but it's not a loss since I can get snacks.
Ah, today's candy is a bit fizzy in my mouth.
Is it a star candy concept?

"I'll get a uniform once I enter the Academy anyway."
"We need photos to report to the master."
"...That must be really expensive."

In a world where scientific development has been delayed due to the existence of magic.
The magical device "Camera" created by magic is an extravagant luxury with limited shots.
To use such a premium item just to see how his daughter dresses, this father must be quite the doting dad.

'Well, if he's extraordinary enough to provide a butler like Jonna, he's no ordinary noble father.'

For a nobleman who generously provides 100 gold coins as support funds, what's a camera?

"Just once."

At the rest stop where the carriage paused.
The other passengers chuckled because the butler actually bought a teddy bear and ribbon headband.

"Whew~ Aren't you cute, miss."
"Hey mister, don't flirt with a child."
"Bard sir, I'll give you three copper coins, so stop being creepy and sing us a song."

The bearded hunter silently stealing glances at my legs from the corner, and being treated like a cute child by the mountaineer couple who came for sightseeing, are both burdensome.
The minstrel I made eye contact with keeps winking at me.

"Then the song I'll perform now is... The White-Skinned Girl."

Oh please.
Stop the humiliation play!


* *


The exam site where all capable people under 20 gathered.
The inn at the entrance of Mt. Shinjeong was crowded with people trying to meet the examiner.

"I think this every year, but the Ticket Exam is really popular. All the nimble explorers and powerful rookie adventurers have gathered."
"You're over twenty, why are you here?"
"Haha. What else can old people do, miss? Obviously to watch. Of course, I'm here to cheer for the most beautiful participant, you!"

Unlike the other passengers who got off on the way to Mt. Shinjeong, Bard, who shared the tedious carriage journey until the end, brings his hand to his mouth and blows a kiss.
At first, I thought he was a creepy man who might be a pedophile, but after realizing Bard's value during the long, boring journey, my perspective changed.
In a world without "Fast Travel" or "Skip," a bard on a carriage is the ultimate insider, rivaling popular singers with thousands of fans.
Without Bard to alleviate the boredom of the long carriage journey, everyone would have gone crazy from the discomfort caused by the butler and maid who cast silence debuffs.

'Well, that was until Bard tried to make a move and got hit once.'

I can't forgive him for trying to put his arm around my shoulders, pretending it was a drunken mistake.
Because of Jonna who struck first, one of Bard's eyes is still black like a panda's.
The atmosphere became terrible after all the other passengers got off at the mountain entrance, having realized the butler's frightening nature.

"Miss, your heart is too open. Don't be too fond of wandering bards who peddle stories and songs."
"What's wrong with that? It was nice not being bored."

After casually waving goodbye to Bard, Leape slightly shifted her steps.

"Where are you going?"
"I have something to attend to briefly."
"Can't I come with you?"

When I grabbed her maid skirt with an inexplicable anxiety, Leape silently sighed.

"Lucky bastard..."
"Pardon?"
"It's nothing. Then let's go together."

Ms. Leape seems lively but actually speaks very little.
Her occasional quiet muttering also makes her seem unsociable.
She's the type of maid who's a bit worrying to leave alone.

"There are many people."
"If you wish, I can stand in line instead. Since it's a rare trip, how about enjoying some sightseeing?"

Bustling crowds.
A waiting line that stretches endlessly like people crazy about a famous restaurant.
The snake-like coil makes my head spin.
The stress of just looking at the line is enough to break my resolve to improve Leape's social skills.
It's obvious we won't get in within a day or two.

Some people mindlessly join any crowd they see, then get angry when they end up at a street vendor's skewer grill without knowing why.
In the end, the street vendor's skill is somewhat impressive as people buy skewers and leave.

"Don't even think about it."

The butler, noticing my gaze, issued a warning.

"It might be meat from rats that grew up eating mud on the streets. As long as I'm here, I won't let such junk food enter your mouth."
"Butler Jonna, you're too strict. If you're that picky, there's nothing we can eat outside."
"Then from now on, you should only eat the food I prepare."
"Tch. It's not like you'll be with me forever."

Jonna's expression darkened slightly.
If I had to compare, it was like the face of a father who realized his daughter would grow up, marry another man, and leave his care.
Feeling a bit sorry, I gently took his hand, and he frowned severely.

*squeeze*

While his expression pretends to dislike it, the butler carefully holds my hand as if touching a sugar sculpture that would break with just a little pressure.
They say male tsunderes are disgusting, but our butler might be a little cute.
