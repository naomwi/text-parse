# Enhanced Translation: Library Expedition Team 3

Following Dorothy's guidance, the members of the "Organization for Playing with Oknodie" felt a sense of regret.

"Oh, there's a side path over there."
"There's even a signpost."
"According to the sign, that way leads to 5-person detention rooms instead of 10-person ones."
"Are we really supposed to go all the way around like this?"
"Why take the harder route when there's a clearly marked path?"

The students who didn't yet trust Dorothy's abilities were complaining! Even Xing, who rarely showed his face normally but specially joined this expedition, harbored doubts beneath his expressionless face.

"There's a reason for everything!"

As everyone's complaints grew louder, Oknodie quickly stepped in to defuse the situation.

"Anyone who wants to reach the library would naturally prefer to take an easier shortcut, right?"
"Yeah, well said, little mouse. Why can't we use that easy path?"
"Well, we're not the only ones who want to benefit from that easy path."

Son O-cheon, who had been grumbling, flinched in surprise.

"What? Don't tell me we'll run into seniors if we go that way?"
"We'd encounter senior students who are more desperate and have done more research than us! The shorter and more convenient the path, the higher the difficulty of obtaining information and the fiercer the competition!"
"Then if there's a single-person detention room..."

Oknodie smiled brightly and said:

"An extremely, extremely, EXTREMELY dangerous senior would be there!"

The students' complaints instantly disappeared.


* * *

Hector's divine group continued their library expedition in an extremely uncomfortable atmosphere.
The cause was a male student leisurely walking at the front of the group, keeping pace with everyone.

"Um, senior, when will you be heading back?"
"Don't misunderstand. Our paths just happen to be the same."
"Would you perhaps like to go ahead?"
"I'm too worried about my juniors to leave. Consider this good fortune and think positively."
"If you insist..."

Third-year senior Tetrapos was pretending to be benevolent on the surface.
At first, everyone was completely fooled by his words.
While checking the path at the front with Yui, Tetrapos casually dropped some information that caught everyone's attention.

"If detention rooms are the first gateway to the library, then the second gateway is solving mana puzzles. Naturally, the puzzles get larger on the main roads and smaller on the shortcut paths."
"Oh! Senior, do you happen to know the path with the smallest puzzle? The information I gathered only went down to 25x25."

That's still a ton of pieces!
Despite the students' shocked expressions, Tetrapos maintained a solemn face and said:

"Bow down in worship. Before you stands a senior who knows a shortcut with a 2x2 puzzle."
"Wow!"
"Isn't that amazing?"
"That's a puzzle you can solve just by placing the pieces."
"No matter how good Oknodie's group is at solving puzzles, they could never keep up with this speed!"
"Hurray for Senior Tetrapos!"
"Three cheers for Senior Tetrapos!"

The morale of Hector's divine group soared to its peak.

"Senior Tetrapos! Would it be alright if I solve this puzzle for you?"
"Haha. Please, by all means, solve it for me."

The students were half-joking, half-sincere in their admiration for Tetrapos, to the point where some even volunteered.

Click.
Swoosh.

"Huh?"
"That guy, why is his body fading... GASP!?"

Until they directly experienced the fact that whoever solved the 2x2 puzzle would be teleported somewhere.

"Wait, where did Backs disappear to?"
"It's teleportation. He wasn't voluntarily disappearing—he was abducted!"
"Senior, what's going on here?!"

Tetrapos revealed his gleaming teeth with a refreshing smile.

"It's a teleportation trap. The puzzle is designed to transport whoever solves it to a designated location."
"You let him solve such a dangerous puzzle? And Backs even seemed to admire you!"
"Isn't that more admirable? He voluntarily stepped forward to solve a puzzle that teleported him to a professor's research lab, all for this senior."
"Oh my god!"
"Good heavens!"
"A professor's research lab?!"
"If we were third-years, the most perceptive person would have set up a secret scheme to restrain someone and force them to solve the puzzle. Hahaha."

The morale of Hector's divine group plummeted from its previous high.
They'd heard that third-years were dangerous, but to this extent!

"Come now. Let's honor the noble sacrifice of Mr. Backs and proceed to the third gateway. Would you like me to show you the next path too?"
"No. You can just stay put, senior."

Yui refused with a serious expression.


* * *

The members of the "Organization for Playing with Oknodie" clapped their hands in genuine delight.

"Woooow, amazing! The puzzle was solved in one go!"
"How can a 1000x1000 piece puzzle be solved in just 3 seconds!?"
"Oh. Little mouse. You're pretty good, aren't you?"

Among the admiring students, Irene, who had been following the group as silently as Xing, couldn't contain her curiosity any longer and asked:

"What kind of magic trick did you use?"
"It's not a magic trick, it's magic!"

Oknodie answered proudly.

"What kind of magic?"
"Memorize! A magic that materializes memories into forms!"

Arcadia, who had been observing the picture, suddenly exclaimed in surprise.

"This image engraved on the puzzle is 'Portrait of the Smiling Countess Mercedes,' one of the top 100 world masterpieces! Don't tell me you reconstructed the original by combining the colors and atmosphere from just seeing parts of the puzzle pieces!?"
"...That's right! I figured it out at a glance because I'm a genius!"
"I see that's not the case. Was there some hint somewhere that revealed the existence of this masterpiece? Hmm, judging by the trembling of your eyes, there must be something engraved on that stone tablet over there."

Like a dog who learns to recite poetry after three years in a Confucian school, Arcadia, who had been taking care of Oknodie for over three months, immediately grasped the true nature of Oknodie's magic.

━━━
You are snow falling in midsummer
You are a barren snow mountain in midwinter
You are one who wandered seeking mystery in miracles

Here I offer a feast.
In honor of the follower of mysteries...
━━━

Pointing to the inscription on the stone tablet, Arcadia displayed her scholarship.

"The House of Mercedes was historically a renowned magical family known for their technology and magic that transcended eras. They were an ancient family from before the Common Era, before the rise of noble families named after foods that are prevalent today."
"Are you saying these ghost-like cryptic words are indicators pointing to Mercedes?"
"In a way, yes. Logini, Sandcooker. As wizards, haven't you heard of the name Mercedes?"

The two people who had been secretly bickering in the corner quickly separated, avoiding everyone's gaze.

"I'm so jealous."
"They're flirting again."
"Just get married already."

As jeers poured in from all directions saying it was already too late, Logini closed her mouth, her face turning as red as her magic tower, while Sandcooker hurriedly stepped forward in her place.

"Ahem. The House of Mercedes is indeed essential when discussing ancient magical families. I've heard that the head of the Mercedes family lived so long that one might suspect they were the incarnation of a dragon. However, there is one controversy about that famous painting."
"The education level at magic towers these days is impressive. I'm surprised you even know about that controversy."
"All children of the Mercedes family were adopted based on their talent, and the family head never had a single biological child, nor did they ever take a wife or even a concubine. Therefore, the very existence of a Countess Mercedes is an incomprehensible figure that cannot logically exist."

Son O-cheon urged them on with an indifferent face.

"Why does it matter whether someone who died over a thousand years ago had a wife or not? I'm more concerned about missing lectures if the day changes while we're looking for the library."
"There's definitely an issue. Originally, that masterpiece was 'Portrait of the Smiling Count Mercedes.' This is the hidden image revealed when a renowned wizard researched the original portrait and uncovered its true nature. The magic tower rarely discloses the true nature of the painting even to disciples, believing it contains clues to mysteries."

Recognizing the existence and true nature of a painting that only high-ranking grand wizards pursue in search of hidden mysteries would be difficult without considerable scholarship and power.
While Arcadia, as a noble lady from the prestigious Sebiche ducal family, might have such knowledge, it was surprising that Oknodie, only 11 years old, would have such insight.

"Could it be that the foundation keeps a copy of this portrait with its veil lifted?"
"...That's right! Papa showed it to me!"

As always, Oknodie used Papa as an excuse, and the students nodded in understanding, believing that with the wealth and power of the Wiheomhae Foundation, it was possible.
However, only Arcadia sensed something strange and tilted her head.

'How odd. The trembling in Oknodie's eyes and awkward gaze are reactions when lying. Why is she trying to hide the truth?'

If not the foundation, where else could she have seen such a painting?
And why was Oknodie trying to hide that fact?
Son O-cheon, who couldn't care less about paintings and magic, abruptly cut through Arcadia's growing suspicions.

"So are we going to the library or not?"
"Hurry up! The next gateway is tough, so we need to prepare thoroughly!"

At Dorothy's urging, everyone's attention shifted away from the "Portrait of the Smiling Countess Mercedes."

"What kind of gateway is coming up that you're making such a fuss?"
"A forest!"

Everyone except Oknodie's expression turned bizarre.

"Why is there a forest inside a building?"
"Why is the path to this crazy library like this?"
"What kind of forest is it?"

Dorothy, who was peeking beyond the passage on her tiptoes, cheerfully exclaimed:

"A maze forest that you can never escape on your own if you don't know the way!"
