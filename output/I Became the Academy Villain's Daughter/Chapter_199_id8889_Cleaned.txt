# 199 - Ruler of the Demon Trees

The name of the tree monster was Demon Tree.
Also called the Devil Tree.
Dark mana—an ominous energy that causes self-destruction or rampage when absorbed by living beings.
When a tree absorbs it for a long time, it becomes a Devil Tree, and when a Devil Tree absorbs dark mana for an even longer period and continues to grow, it achieves super-evolution.
A massive Demon World Tree comparable to the World Tree of the elves.
As the leader of all Devil Trees!
Devil Trees cannot go against the will of the Demon World Tree.
If a smaller tree defies the will of a larger tree or invades its territory, it won't even be allowed to survive.
Not permitted a single drop of water, handful of nutrients, or sliver of sunlight, it would starve to death.
