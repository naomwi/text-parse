# Chapter 14 - The Correlation Between Happiness and Growth

The buffet's 100 dishes were plentiful enough to enjoy slowly over 10 days while in the air.
I just needed to clear 10 dishes per day.
Since it would take exactly ten days to reach our destination, this was perfect.

"Ah!"
"What's wrong, My Lady?"
"I've realized the trap."

Jonna immediately changed his expression, kneeling down to fix my shoelaces while lowering his voice.

"...If I may ask, could you explain what trap you've noticed?"

"The portions. I'm not convinced that simply putting various foods on a plate will count as collecting all the different dishes for the cookbook."

"...I don't understand what you mean at all."

The criteria for what constitutes a single serving in the cookbook is a critical matter.
What if a buffet plate with various dishes mixed together doesn't count toward the cookbook collection?
What if only the dish with the largest portion counts?

"I... I can't enjoy selecting various foods for my plate... Having to fill a plate with just one food ten times, repeating this for ten days..."

Even for someone who loves collecting, this amount would be food torture.
Wait a minute.
Does a serving necessarily have to fill the entire plate?
A buffet is a place where you take as much as you want.
Even taking a tiny amount could count as a successful collection if there's only one type of food on the plate.

*Click click*

As I excitedly clicked the tongs thinking about filling my plate, I heard a slightly incredulous voice saying, "You change moods quite quickly."

"Hehe. Look at that. The little miss who can't even reach the side dish containers. Isn't she cute?"
"Oh my. She reminds me of my youngest daughter I left at home. Hohoho."

Customers who came to enjoy an affordable meal laughed at me as they passed by.
With tears welling up in my eyes from the humiliation, I tugged firmly on the butler's sleeve.

"Jonna... I can't reach the food containers..."
"You change moods quickly in a negative way too, I see."

With Jonna's help, I was able to get my food.

"Are you really sure just one chocolate chip is enough?"
"I want to start light."
"You eat like a little mouse, not knowing how to eat properly."
"Ah! Bearded big guy!"
"This is how you eat food."

A beastkin at the next table who had piled his food mountain-high was shoveling it into his mouth.
Watching his hearty meal like a food fighter or mukbang streamer ignited my competitive spirit, but testing was the priority now.

*Crunch crunch*

"..."
"Would you like more?"
"Yes..."

The cookie I devoured in an instant.
The next menu item was...

"Another chocolate chip?"
"T-this time I'll eat four more!"

I'll stick with chocolate chips until I'm certain I've received a single serving judgment!
Around the time I thought, humanly speaking, this should be enough, my head throbbed lightly and a notification appeared.

[Regular dish <Chocolate Chip> has been collected in the Cooking Book.]
[Regular dishes have no individual collection effect.]

After succeeding with chocolate chips, I continued with individual conquests, filling each plate with just one type of dish.

...

[Regular dish <Mushroom Soup> has been collected in the Cooking Book.]
[Regular dish <Crabby Avocado> has been collected in the Cooking Book.]
[Regular dish <Salmon Sushi> has been collected in the Cooking Book.]
[Regular dish <New York Strip> has been collected in the Cooking Book.]
[Regular dish <Melon> has been collected in the Cooking Book.]

...

Like an ant carrying food, I diligently transferred food into my mouth.
Of course, Jonna, who was serving the food onto plates for me, was working incredibly hard.
Yet I'm grateful for his consideration in steadfastly filling plates with food.

"Since you're not training today, you're eating well without breaking any dishes."
"If I get tired without even losing stamina, I wouldn't be able to swing a sword!"

Even someone as frail as me has gained some stamina through consistent training. I feel both grateful and sorry seeing the butler seriously contemplating beside me, muttering something about "2kg weights..."

"I'm sorry."
"What are you suddenly apologizing for?"
"Just because."

Jonna looked down at me with his characteristically fierce eyes and spoke in a gruff voice.

"I'm proud of you."
"Of me?"

It was praise I'd never heard once when I was only running male muscle characters.

"You don't complain about food, nor do you whine about training being difficult."
"...Is that a compliment?"
"It is. Considering the daily life that children your age enjoy, your efforts and diligence for academy admission are remarkable."

At the next table, a monkey beastkin watched with an intrigued expression, while Gisele observed with a complicated look.
Being praised in front of others is a bit embarrassing.

"I believe that your efforts will never be in vain. Perhaps someday, Master will recognize and call for you."
"Ah, that's..."
"Don't you want to meet Master?"
"It's not that I don't want to... it's just, well..."

There's a troublesome event.
Random Papa events can lead to linked events like <Papa's Request> or <Home Summons> as time passes.
If you receive a lot of support early on, you might spend precious weekends fulfilling Papa's requests, or vacation time flying away following home summons.
The higher the benefits, the lower your freedom—that's how it goes.
Just as everything in life has two sides, even noble papas with many advantages have these minor flaws.
While a commoner papa's requests or home summons might simply involve seeing their face and having a meal before returning, the vain culture of nobles is different.

Family-hosted hunting competitions.
Gatherings of nobles of matching rank.
Large and small randomly generated events in territories.

Time-consuming events are already lining up like hippos waiting to devour my time.

"Are you afraid?"

But I can't be an ungrateful daughter who dismisses a papa who gave me 100 gold coins just because he's troublesome.
When I nodded pretending to be scared, the butler took something out from his chest and placed it in my hand.

"What's this?"
"If you continue to train diligently, someday you'll find a way to open this cube."
"Is it a riddle?"
"When you open this cube, your fear of Master will somewhat subside."

Hmm. A cube.
Maybe I should bite it—

"You must not eat it."
"...I was just going to nibble it. Slightly. Just very slightly."
"Taking anything to your mouth is a bad habit. This cube is so hard that your teeth would break if you bit it. Please refrain, My Lady."

I got scolded.

"How heartwarming. What's the problem, employer?"
"What you see isn't everything."

Even with the conversation between the monkey beastkin and Gisele, I felt ashamed at being caught getting scolded.

"Would you like to finish your meal now?"
"I'm going to train."

I need to create an appetite through exercise if I want to empty five more plates for today's quota.
...Though it might be more that I want to escape this embarrassing situation, but since it's embarrassing, I won't mention that reason.


* *


Evening time.
After finishing intense training and returning to the buffet, I nearly fainted from excessive shock.

"Oh my goodness!"
"Are you alright, My Lady?"
"H-how could something so inhumane happen?!"

Jonna said with an I-told-you-so expression.

"That's why I told you it would be better to end today's training early."

[Thank you for using our Sky Buffet today.]
[Our Sky Buffet operating hours are from 8 AM to 5 PM. We ask for your understanding from customers who arrive too early or too late.]
[CLOSED]

"I didn't know the buffet closed at 5!"
"It seems unavoidable for such a popular buffet."
"Then what about my dinner?"
"There's an evening restaurant that opens during dinner hours. Would you like to visit?"
"...Let's go!"

It's disappointing that I couldn't eat all the buffet food, but it's even if I make up for it at the restaurant.

"Isn't Leape coming with us?"
"I've already finished my meal, so I'll be looking for more facilities that might be helpful to you."

I feel somewhat sorry.
She's working so hard to find places even though I'm only interested in the buffet and training grounds.
Since she's doing it for me, not for her own enjoyment, I should set aside a day to follow Leape's recommended course out of courtesy.

"Eeeek!"

That's what I thought.
Until I saw the restaurant's menu.

"...Jonna. Why are the prices like this?"
"It's the night surcharge."
"One gold coin for a single meal is crossing the line!"
"Calm down, My Lady. That's for the set menu."
"Oh."

It really is.

<Set A - Evening Full Course 1 Gold Coin>
<Set B - Evening Course 40 Silver Coins>
<Set C - Evening Single Dish 15 Silver Coins>

"It's common sense that services used at night are more expensive than during the day."
"I'm sorry. I was momentarily surprised by the high prices."
"Don't worry, My Lady. I can cover your food expenses with the funds I received from Master."
"Really?"
"You may consider it a gift from Master upon hearing the news that you obtained a platinum ticket."

In the game, I would have trembled at the thought of spending this expensive initial funding on food, searching desperately for cost-effective collection menus.
As a player, I was overwhelmed with joy at this luxury I'd never dared to enjoy.

"Jonna. I'm so happy right now."
"I also hope that you can be happy by continuing to grow steadily."

Suddenly feeling mischievous, I asked:

"What happens if I don't grow? Will you still stay by my side?"
"...That won't happen. I hope such a future never comes."
"Why?"
"Master has many scholarship students... no, children. He won't show interest in children who lack talent or grow slowly."
"Then what about you?"

Jonna didn't answer.
