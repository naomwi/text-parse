# Enhanced Translation: Chapter 342 - The Child Who Knows the Answer

On my way out after attending the <Advanced Mana Enhancement> lecture, I suddenly remembered something.
I wonder how Titosoga's training with the maid mana cultivation technique I got for her is going?
I definitely asked Leape to teach her.
Since I have a free period during Thursday's second class, should I go check if she's doing well?
I headed to the group training ground I'd borrowed under Leape's name and requested entry permission. The door opened immediately.
Seeing how quickly the door opened without any wait, Leape must have made arrangements in advance.
As expected, our maid does her job well.
However, inside the training ground, I witnessed a scene that made me doubt my eyes.

"Hnnng. This posture is too difficult."
"Stop whining. This is a movement my Lady would have executed flawlessly."

Leape's strict instruction and Titosoga's whimpering were exactly as I'd expected.

"Don't drop the bucket. Only when you perfect your sense of balance to the point where even a teacup doesn't tremble can you achieve constant mana cultivation."
"Can't I just live as a cleaning maid?"
"April. Even by cleaning maid standards, you have a long way to go."
"Woof woof! Not enough! Not enough!"
"...Happy, why is that dog-person training with us?"
"You just didn't know, but Happy is another tail who was in charge of the foundation's beastkin scholarship students."
"That stupid Happy?!"
"Grrrr. Happy not stupid!"

The spy maid April and dog beastkin maid Happy training hard was also somewhat within my expectations.

"Please tell me what I need to improve as well."

The unexpected figure among the group of trainees learning the maid mana cultivation technique was the unusually large berserker and chapter boss, Hestia.

"There's nothing to criticize even if I tried. Time is the remedy, so don't be impatient. If you continue training steadily as you are now, you'll see results."

It was quite bizarre to see someone whose forearms were thicker than Titosoga's waist learning the maid mana cultivation technique, but the trainee herself seemed serious.
It was even more perplexing to see her so dedicated to training that sweat beaded on her chin and soaked the floor.

"Leape! How long has Hestia been here?"
"She came voluntarily from the very first training session."

Hestia without a mana cultivation technique.
It's not surprising that she would be interested in learning one.
Wasn't she the one who came to the Academy seeking an opportunity that neither the Empire nor the Frontier would grant her?

"Oknodie. Would it be a burden if I learned the cultivation technique alongside Titosoga?"
"Not at all! I was just surprised. Someone like Hestia could surely learn other cultivation techniques too."

Just as Xing uses a technique that sacrifices safety and shortens his lifespan to increase damage output, Hestia too was known for utilizing methods that maximize a berserker's characteristics.
A berserker-exclusive mana cultivation technique that can constantly trigger berserk mode (which normally activates when HP or mental power falls below a certain percentage) simultaneously with the cultivation technique's activation.
This extremely dangerous cultivation technique deliberately induces deviation from the correct path, mixing intentionally incorrect cultivation paths with correct ones. It's impossible to even begin without a robust physique.

'Bladiburst would be perfect for Hestia!'

People who have already learned other mana cultivation techniques get goosebumps from this technique's unconventional approach and can't complete the cultivation.
Even without prior cultivation experience, without extreme physical conditioning, one can't withstand the artificially induced deviation and the cultivation breaks off midway.
The Bladiburst cultivation technique can only be learned by warriors like Hestia who have developed their bodies to the extreme but haven't had the opportunity to learn a cultivation technique.

"If Hestia wants, I could teach you another cultivation technique. What do you think?"
"Would that technique be more powerful?"
"Of course!"
"And it would have more potential than the maid mana cultivation technique."
"Definitely! The difficulty level alone is worlds apart."
"With such a technique, I'd need to maintain a large and solid physique, right?"
"It's essential if you want to grow to 2m30cm in the future!"
"Then I don't need it."

NPCs are full of surprises today.

"I've had enough of physical training. Building muscles and strengthening my body. After doing all that and ending up alone, I realized. This isn't the strength I want."
"Then what kind of strength do you want, Hestia?"
"You."
"Me?"
"A body that can save everyone despite being smaller than most, a body that allows not just me but everyone to survive together. That's the kind of body I want to build and nurture."

Lucky her.
I'd love to have a body like Hestia's!
Growing from 133cm to 230cm is much harder than from 190cm to 230cm.
But if she doesn't want it, that's her choice.
I decided to respect Hestia's wishes.

"The maid mana cultivation technique fundamentally emphasizes appearance, so you'll need to continuously compress your existing bulky muscles into a slimmer form."
"That's exactly what I want."
"Since Titosoga is already small, the compression won't take long, but for Hestia, it will take much longer because of your larger body."
"I've practiced patience all my life. Just having a method is enough for me."
"Well, since I'm already training Xing, I'll train Hestia too!"
"Thank you."

I thought she'd be busy with personal training so I left her alone until now, but if I'd known she'd be this happy, I would have helped her sooner.

"Then let's meet after lunch when classes end on the weekend!"
"Lunch? We could meet from morning."
"I have classes on weekends too!"

Hestia's face turned pale.

"W-wait. Come to think of it, Oknodie, you have thirteen lectures... How on earth will you find time?"
"When I don't have lectures and don't need to do assignments?"
"That's impossible! If you're planning to skip assignments to help me, please don't push yourself!"

No, really, it's fine.
Should I tell her I've already prepared answer sheets for all the fixed assignments?
