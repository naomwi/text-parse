# 5 - Secret Assassination Instructor

Just like any other day, I was immersed in my training.
Unlike usual, however, a somewhat special day was beginning.

"I am a maid dispatched to look after My Lady."
"Ohhh."

*Clap clap clap.*
I couldn't help but applaud at the appearance of a woman in what was unmistakably maid attire.
A cute-looking maid with short green hair and sharp eyes gave a greeting while slightly lifting the hem of her long skirt with both hands.

"I am Leape, assigned to serve Lady Oknodie from today onward."

She seems normal.
Which made her even more suspicious.

"My Lady. Is there something wrong with the maid?"
"Your name. What is your full name?"
"I apologize for my rudeness. Allow me to introduce myself again. I am Leape Life."

Leape Life.
Leaf life.
Quite a cute name befitting a maid.
Which made me even more jealous.
Some people have names like Oknodie and Jonna Wiheomhae that sound like someone's playing a prank, but why does only the maid get a decent name?
I feel envious, embarrassed, and angry all at once.

"My Lady?"
"It's nothing."

Being jealous of a maid because of her name.
This isn't something I can tell anyone about.


* * *


Leape Life found it interesting.
Reaped Life. A harvested life.
Once a girl whose humble life was extended thanks to the Boss, she decided to dedicate her life to him.
A new name obtained after discarding her old one.
Leape Life.
It was a name that carried the pride of an assassin.
Herself, who had been reaped by the Boss.
The Boss's enemies who would be reaped by her hands.
A name that simultaneously signified both.

"Did you tell her about my identity?"
"Not at all. I intend for you to serve as a secret bodyguard as well. I have no intention whatsoever of revealing your identity to the young lady."
"Then it's pure intuition on her part."

Leape found this intriguing.

"The verification requirement from the Supervisor was for assassin qualities. This isn't a test that can be passed with intuition alone."
"I know. I'll leave the evaluation to your judgment."
"You seem quite confident."
"You'll see."

Leape monitored the young lady's every move.
And she became convinced.

'She was born to be an assassin!'

The assassin's instinct to hide whenever there's a chance, evading the eyes of the butler and herself.
The assassin's tracking instinct, sensing presences at night and gauging whether she could slip out through the window.
Even weight training, carrying a massive weapon concealed in her bosom while continuing her daily life without missing meals.

"I will report to the Supervisor myself."
"When do you plan to start the training?"
"Starting today."
"It must not interfere with her existing training."
"Don't worry. I'll keep drug and poison resistance training to a minimum and postpone assassination techniques for later. For now, I'll focus intensively on stealth and tracking training."
"Not bad."
"But I wonder why someone of your caliber is still doing this kind of work. Perhaps those rumors about the trouble you caused regarding the disposal of the previous 'young lady'..."
"That's enough."

Leape closed her mouth as the butler's hand edge reached her chin.

"Know your place. Your target for surveillance is the young lady, not me."
"...I apologize for my rudeness."

After withdrawing his killing intent, Jonna left to prepare tomorrow's training for the young lady.
Leape smiled coldly.

'No matter how talented the young lady is, it would be troublesome if you become too greedy. The organization isn't generous enough to overlook mistakes repeatedly.'


* * *


The new maid Leape.
This woman was subtly annoying yet helpful.

"What are you doing in the laundry basket?"
"...It just looked comfortable to sit in."

[Hiding failed.]

Sometimes she interfered with my training.

"My Lady? No... It was just the shadow of the garden tree reflected behind the curtain."

[You have hidden on a branch for more than 1 minute.]
[Hiding experience +1]
[Someone saw you but didn't notice.]
[Hiding experience +3]
[Stealth experience +1]

Other times she was actually helpful.
Unlike Butler Jonna, who would find me no matter where I haphazardly hid, the maid had many tasks like laundry and cleaning.
If I didn't observe her daily schedule or predict her movements through mealtime conversations, my hiding would be quickly discovered.
But if I prepared thoroughly and deceived the maid's eyes, my hiding experience would grow rapidly.
Thanks to this, the higher function of hiding, stealth, naturally increased as well—turning misfortune into blessing, killing two birds with one stone.

"Ugh. The food tastes strange."
"I apologize, My Lady. I will be more careful next time."

But lately, it's the butler who's causing trouble.
Someone who used to cook well keeps failing to season properly or serves food with strange tastes.

"My Lady?"
"Don't take it away. You worked hard to cook it."

[You have identified spoiled food.]
[Poison Identification experience +1]
[You ate spoiled food.]
[Poison Resistance experience +1]

Was it bad enough to risk food poisoning?
Still, if I show that I'll eat everything, maybe he'll be more careful with his cooking next time.
After finishing the meal with gratitude I've always felt toward the butler, not only the butler but also the maid looked at me with strange gazes.

Hehe. Are they touched?

The butler and maid whispered to each other from a distance, and from the next day, the maid became strangely kind.

"Would you like a strawberry-flavored candy?"
"Yes, please!"
"Here's a grape-flavored candy."
"Yes, please!"
"I baked cookies specially today."
"I'd love that!"

Seeing that I respond positively to everything, she seems to find joy in giving me snacks and keeps bringing them whenever she can.
Hehe. It's worth training her with enthusiastic reactions to make her feel rewarded for giving snacks.
Perhaps because I ate even strange-tasting snacks without complaint, she offers all kinds of treats from cake slices to alphabet chocolates.
Silly maid.
She doesn't even realize her salary is being sacrificed to increase my food catalog collection rate, yet she faithfully spends her wages on snacks!


* * *


Late at night.
In the staff quarters of the secret training ground, the butler and maid return to their true identities as an organization executive and an assassin.

"How is the poison adaptation training going?"
"Her identification and adaptation are faster than expected. I've unintentionally made quite a bit of progress."
"Is it similar to what happens during mealtimes?"
"Her detection rate is about 30%, but that's quite high."
"Is this normal?"
"Of course not."

Leape declared firmly.

"Ordinary people only feel mild discomfort when eating poisoned food, not showing strong suspicion or certainty like the young lady does. If they realized it, they wouldn't eat it at all."
"Yet the young lady ate everything."
"There are two possible scenarios. Either she has survived multiple near-death experiences from eating poisoned food on the streets, or she had to eat contaminated food to survive."

This was the assertion of an assassin who handled poisons.
Jonna realized his understanding of street trash was insufficient.

"There seem to be a lot of garbage in this city."
"Are you planning to clean it up?"
"They're the ones who laid hands on the organization's young lady. We can't brazenly let them live."

Killing intent filled Jonna's eyes.

"Wait... The street magician's antics and the poisoned food distributed to people on the streets might indicate another organization's involvement in the city."
"You're planning to take action."
"It's worth investigating. Leape. Your surveillance duty of the young lady tonight is changed. Confirm whether an unidentified hostile organization is operating."
"And if I find them. What then?"

The answer was obvious.

"Annihilation."

At Jonna's order, Leape's eyes gleamed with a cold smile.


* * *


Today I was lucky to have no surveillance.
After the maid arrived, Butler Jonna's surveillance decreased, and Maid Leape also lowered her guard, mistakenly thinking I would sleep quietly after receiving snacks.
Hehe. Troublesome?
If you lower your guard against a veteran player like me...
I might sneak out at night and raid some criminal organizations on the streets.
After all, they're just using money extorted from ordinary citizens to challenge the exam, so isn't it better for them to be raided by a righteous player and have their funds redistributed?

So I slipped out of the mansion.
Looking for markings where organizations might be, I headed to an abandoned warehouse near the underground waterway, and voilà, I found a hideout with dozens of corpses.

"...?"

A gang war?
Why are they all dead?

Looking at the safe, there were no signs of it being cracked.
This wasn't a robbery-murder.
Feeling uneasy, I raided another organization office in the slums.

"...??"

More corpses everywhere.
Even the hideout of a corrupt merchant guild that tries to capture players as slaves with a 20% probability.
Even the tent of a circus troupe that eats human flesh.
Everywhere I went, criminal hideouts were filled with corpses.
What is this?
It's kind of scary.
Thoroughly spooked, I returned to the mansion and was closing the window when the light suddenly turned on.

"Eeeek!!"
"Where have you been at this late hour, My Lady?"
"J-just a little walk."
"You smell of blood."
"N-no! I didn't kill them. Everyone was already dead when I got there!"

Leape, who had looked slightly surprised, smiled.
As I stared at her smile, tempting as a ripe fruit, Leape removed my outdoor clothes and laid me on the bed.
While I blinked in confusion, Leape covered me with a blanket up to my neck and placed her hand over my eyes.

"You had a bad dream."
"Huh?"
"Don't worry. I'll stay by your side tonight. You won't have any more nightmares, so please sleep peacefully just for tonight."

Her gentle voice made drowsiness wash over me.
Beyond her patting hand, I heard the sound of a candle being lit, and I felt my mind becoming increasingly hazy as if I'd inhaled some hypnotic gas.
I see...
It was a nightmare...
Feeling reassured by the cool hand covering my eyes, I fell asleep.

"I will... allow you to be followed... professional training..."

I thought I heard Leape saying something in my dream, but it must have been my imagination.
