# Jonna's Interference

Oknodie has an eye for recognizing a person's worth.
And she has her own standards.

Existing reputation, Academy class placement.
Public perception, public standards.

Oknodie doesn't trust others' judgment.
She evaluates people solely by her own standards.

Those close to her, regardless of upper or lower classes, are all talented individuals.
At the same time, they are people whose true nature is difficult to definitively call malicious, regardless of their reputation.

Even if they're butlers of the Foundation, they're certainly by her side because they've met her special criteria.

'Previously, during an outing for Lady Arcadia, a Foundation subgroup called the Clown Mask Unit was completely destroyed by Oknodie and Professor Destroyer.'

Oknodie prioritizes individual worth over affiliation.
Since witnessing her goodness, Gisele has developed a small doubt.
Is it possible that a person like Jonna Wiheomhae might not be as malicious as thought according to Oknodie's sensor?
Just as someone like Ishtar appeared in the heroic profession, perhaps not everyone in an evil organization is necessarily evil.

'Yes, there might be a one-in-a-million chance.'

Let's learn more about this person called Jonna Wiheomhae.
Of course, he's a professor.
There are limits to information available through official channels.
Naturally, he activates the dark channels.
He mobilizes the information network of the "real" Dark Association that exists outside the Academy.

758,250,000 gold coins.
Even Gisele, who deals with large sums, was overwhelmed by the enormous estimated damage.

'Converted to points, that's 75,825,000,000 points. Is it truly possible for just one person to cause such destruction...?'

Even a suicidal bombing by a desperate madman couldn't cause this much damage.
Even if one self-destructs with explosives capable of destroying a building, most important facilities have defensive magic barriers installed.
Calculating the most valuable building that could be destroyed by a bomb, it would be worth at most 100 gold coins.
Compared to a simple suicide bomber, the danger posed by Jonna Wiheomhae is as serious as 758,250,000 terrorists.

'I remember now. The Troy War magical weapon malfunction catastrophe. If it's that incident, surely...'

Rebels who rose up claiming to restore justice in the Troy Kingdom, which was criticized as a hotbed of biological experiments.
Countries worldwide unofficially supported the rebels, passing mana stones necessary for operating magical weapons through various channels.
Since the Dark Association was responsible for many routes in the illegal transportation network, he still remembers it.

'The post-incident investigation revealed that disguised mana stones secretly hidden among regular mana stones caused the weapon malfunction. A catastrophe where the rebel leadership was completely annihilated overnight. The person involved with the problematic mana stones was none other than Jonna Wiheomhae...'

While Gisele himself doesn't know the details, he thought the pattern was probably similar in other incidents as well.

This man is dangerous.

Such large-scale incidents couldn't happen by coincidence without a clear background.
They were all disasters artificially caused by the Wiheomhae Foundation with definite purposes.
Could the "ladies" raised by such a person really grow up to be normal humans?

'I'm even more convinced now. Your mana alchemy method. No matter how valuable it is, it's a dangerous act that could cause serious abnormalities in a student's body at any time.'

Gisele made a decision.
To submit this report to the Academy.
There are plenty of ways to use it if he kept it.
But it's dangerous.
Jonna Wiheomhae is not someone who can be easily manipulated for one's benefit.
His connection to the incidents remains merely suspicious, and the actual crimes are all unclear.
It's just that with his <Metal Manipulation>, he could cause all these incidents, and circumstances show he was involved in all of them somehow.
That alone is more than enough reason to expel Jonna Wiheomhae from the Academy to protect talented individuals from around the world.
At least, that's what Gisele believed.
However, the Academy showed no reaction whatsoever to the anonymous letter.

'Why? Is the Academy saying they don't care if such a terrorist remains as a professor? Even though their reputation would be tarnished if he causes a catastrophe?'

He wants to demand answers.
But the moment he protests, it would be revealed that he was the informant.
So he used a third party.
One of the many first-year students from the Dark Association.
