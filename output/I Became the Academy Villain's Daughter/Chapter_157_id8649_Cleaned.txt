# 157 - Performance Character and Affection Character

I spent points to tear open a precious emotion scroll.

<Tito Family's Friendship Ring (Bound)>
Rank - Unique Grade 7
Description - A gift from the Ghost Papa commemorating the unwavering friendship of the Tito family. If the current ring wearer doesn't wish it, other individuals won't receive any effects from wearing the ring.
Effect 1 - Check Life Gauge
Effect 2 - Transfer Life Force
Effect 3 - When within 20m of another ring owner, all abilities increase
Appraisal Value - 222 gold coins, 22 silver coins, 22,222 points

Although negative energy deals with the wicked energy of the negative dimension, if you use that power only for good purposes, you can use the ability to detect and extract life force in this way.
It was incredibly refreshing that ghosts, who typically operate with malicious intent to harm people, used this wicked negative energy for such a good purpose.
Usually, rewards aren't items like this but rather items with brutal names from the start.

<Maxwell's Ghost Generator (Cursed)>
Rank - Unique Grade 5
Description - A massive chainsaw forged from the grudges of cursed ghosts. A mass slaughter tool designed to create more ghosts and suffering souls.
Effect 1 - Cannot unequip before killing a living being
Effect 2 - Berserk mode constantly activated
Effect 3 - Combat power increases with exposure to blood

<Haunted Harmonica (Cursed)>
Rank - Unique Grade 8
Description - A cursed instrument born from the grudges of guardians who discovered their music-loving child couldn't endure Academy life and died.
Effect 1 - Absorbs energy from listeners
Effect 2 - Cannot stop playing until absorbing a certain amount of energy
Effect 3 - Teleports within 3m of owner every 10 minutes if owner is alive
Effect 4 - Teleports near the closest person every 10 minutes if there is no owner
Effect 5 - Treasure Aura: Emits an aura of treasure to deceive those with appraisal abilities

Whether directly or indirectly harmful, these cursed items are designed to harm as many people as possible.
By exploiting their functions, they can be extremely effective in solo play and PVE content where the owner or user avoids damage while forcing it upon enemies.
This event is considered a jackpot among players, as high-grade items with practical combat applications can sometimes be adopted as main items to carry the early game.

'It's been a long time since I've seen a non-cursed item in this event!'

Perhaps because we solved the quest in a way that resolved the ghosts' grudges, the Tito Family's Friendship Ring was much more benign compared to the ominous unique items.
Of course, having a benign nature doesn't mean its effects are also mild.
It's like people who have nice personalities but aggressive physical builds.
Life force.
Health.
These stats greatly reduce the chance of sudden death from unfair treatment at the Academy.
If you can avoid dying in one hit, you'll always have room to respond.

'The item concept is clear and useful!'

It greatly enhances the life force of characters with low companion value and increases each other's abilities when together, creating advantages for long-term companionship.
This is an item you'd use when determined to raise what's commonly called an "affection character" rather than a "performance character," twisting yourself in all sorts of ways to make it work.

"Tito, I'll definitely take you along next time there's a good event! Look forward to it!"
"You won't go with Lady Arcadia? With me? I won't be much help..."
"I'm doing this because I'm grateful!"

Tito nodded shyly with a smile.

"I'll look forward to it..."


* *


The event was over.
At least that's what we all thought as we headed back to the dormitory when another incident occurred.
Students carrying torches gathered from a distance, pointed at Tito, and shouted loudly.

"Tito, get behind me."
"L-Lady Arcadia... those people scare me."
"Don't worry. I'll protect you too!"

As I took a firm combat stance waiting for the students to approach, a group of students rushed in front of us with angry faces and spoke.

"Where are the parents of that girl?"
"They passed away!"
"You think we'll just say 'Oh, is that so?' and end it there? Our ghost detector is pointing to this area. Besides, that child Tito isn't even the daughter of a grand noble!"
"My papa is the mayor of Canele City, part of the Southern Holy City-State Federation!"
"Who cares about some primitive state system where southern country bumpkins gather? I couldn't care less about positions created by such a small alliance of cities!"

Students suddenly appeared and hurled harsh words about Tito's papa.
Tito, whom I expected to burst into tears with a "hing-ing-ing," once again put on an angry expression and stood up bravely.

"Who are you to badmouth my papa! I'm going to report this to the professor!"
"Ha, what a joke. What does this parchment look like to you? It's a quest parchment issued by the professors you love so much. A quest to track down and eliminate ghosts that have appeared on campus!"
"My papa is not a ghost!"
"Then he must be a terrifying con artist. Do you think it's possible for a mere city-state mayor to secure additional visitation tickets that even grand nobles find burdensome, and stay at the Academy until the third day just through ordinary effort?"
"Don't speak ill of my papa!"
"Then just admit it. Your papa is a ghost. Don't get upset just because someone said bad things about someone who isn't even your real papa. Before we classify you as someone haunted by a ghost."

Haunted Human.
A status ailment that often affects people with weak mental strength or those temporarily weakened. Once haunted by a ghost, it's nearly impossible to escape this state voluntarily.
This is because the ghost controls the person according to its will and struggles to prevent the status ailment from being cured.
Most ghosts naturally make humans engage in harmful activities.
The haunted person themselves also suffers negative effects.

"See this? This emblem of the second year. I'm an exorcist who has accumulated dozens of exorcism experiences following my master outside. It's better to follow instructions obediently."

Exorcist.
Ghost-type monster and related phenomenon response specialist.
Tracking, protection, exorcism.
People of this class, who eliminate ghosts by any means necessary, are just unpleasant individuals in normal times, but when ghosts or supernatural phenomena actually appear, they rampage like fierce warriors.

"Hurry up and admit it. Your papa is a ghost papa, and tell us where he is now."
"Would you please stop this rude behavior? You are currently offending me, Arcadia Seviche, the rightful heir to the Duchy of the Kingdom of Florence."

Arcadia wouldn't allow the exorcist to run wild.
As Tito quickly hid behind Arcadia's back, poking out only her head and glaring intensely, the irritated exorcist tried to approach roughly.

<Ghost Exorcism Follow-up Event>
An exorcist who arrived at the scene a bit late.
While the goal of exorcising ghosts to protect students is good, they're only doing nasty things that inflict emotional wounds on Tito, who is actually involved in the incident.
After receiving the friendship ring, you wouldn't just keep quiet and pretend not to know, right?
Of course, exorcists are trained to be strong enough to perfectly respond to various situations caused by ghosts.
If you're not strong enough and feel burdened by confronting an exorcist, I recommend compromising with reality.
Even if it seems cowardly, you still need to survive!

Even the event description warns.
Exorcists are not a weak, minor class.
Ghosts who levitate people with telekinesis, throw knives around, and cause major accidents with cursed items.
Exorcists, thoroughly trained to respond to all these situations, sometimes have combat abilities superior to ordinary warriors.
Judging by how puffed up their clothing was, the exorcist who appeared before us seemed to be one of those.
Exorcists are what you'd call "performance characters."
Their combat power is much higher than that of "affection characters" like Tito.
What if I gained the exorcist's favor instead?

Abandon the affection character and choose the performance character.
Abandon pretense and choose practicality.
Realistic benefits would follow.

"Stop bullying Tito and Big Sis Arcadia."

When I pushed forcefully with an "Eit!" the exorcist stumbled back two steps.
Practicality, that's good.
But an experienced player can gain more practical benefits from an affection character than from a performance character.

"If you bully them, I, the joint top student of the year, will punish you."
"Gasp, that child is Oknodie!"
"Be careful, Sir Ao. That little one is dangerous."

The second-year seniors who seemed to be influencing the exorcist.
Their faces looked familiar, likely members of the Red-Toothed Mushroom Cultivation Association.
It seems they were trying to get revenge for their failed business this way.

"Hooh. So you're the infamous..."
"You know me?"
"Dark Princess?"
"...I'm not! I'm going to be a Magic Swordsman!"

When I glared at the second-year seniors who appeared to be the source of these false rumors, they all hid behind Senior Ao the exorcist's back just like Tito had done, poking out only their heads.
Seeing them mouth things like "You're dead!" and "You're finished!" only made my contempt for them grow stronger.
