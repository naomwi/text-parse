# Graduating from the Academy by Luck - 132: Patrol with Good Intentions

The Gift Academy, the world's premier educational institution.
Its graduates rise to the highest positions in countries worldwide, and when they establish their own organizations, their fame or infamy spreads widely—from small regions to entire continents.
They not only become the strongest in their fields and the best in the world, but they also open new horizons in history.

The shortest path to becoming a hero.
A bachelor's degree course leading to success.

Students who entered the Academy pursuing glory.
981st batch: 2,130 undergraduate students.
Among them, the first dropout will emerge this week.

'It's the week when a high-stakes practical exam will be measured, resulting in such severe deductions that no future scores can reverse them.'

Some failing students couldn't accept reality.
If they just endured, they could enjoy glory.
The thought that they couldn't even accomplish that much was unbearable.
Family support.
National support.
Organizational support.
Though their affiliations differed, what they carried on their backs was the same.
Someone's expectations, wishes, and hopes.
What awaits those who betray all of these is enormous disappointment, cruel condemnation, and massive debt.

'Both Arcadia and Gisele are paying a lot of attention to the lower classes, so I'm getting concerned too.'

After finishing my lecture, in my free time.
Today, I decided to make time to walk around the lower classes and look for potential ticking time bombs.

A Tier 2 supporting character by Oknodie's standards.
The NTR heroine Yui.
She passed the retest held in the lower class by fully utilizing her cunning eloquence that had once manipulated Rockfell.
She succeeded in securing her place at the Academy.
However, the humiliation of the past didn't simply disappear.

"Dorothy. Rockfell. Everything you're enjoying should have been mine...!"

Yui sliced hard black bread with a dagger, soaked it in water, and chewed it down.
Even lower class students passing by recoiled in horror at her ferocious eating.

"Isn't that the bankruptcy relief food sold in bundles of five for 1 point?"
"I've never seen anyone eat that before... What did Yui do to go bankrupt and suffer like that?"
"Yui has plenty of points too."

A student sitting at another table in the cafeteria revealed the shocking truth to the whispering couple.

"What?! Then why is she eating that tasteless black bread?"
"She's trying to save points."
"Good grief!"
"She's not human!"
"That's something a third-year would do!"

A third-year student eating black bread in a corner of the cafeteria while using Invisible magic to avoid embarrassment glared at the first-years.
Of course, the first-years couldn't see her since she was invisible, so they fearlessly gossiped about third-years.

"Ugh. I suddenly feel weak."
"Is it getting chilly?"
"Maybe it's because it's still spring. March is late spring."

Laugh all you want. What would these kids know, receiving their 5-point standard rations from the school cafeteria without restraint?
Yui silently cursed her classmates who pitied her.
There's a saying that many drops make an ocean.
She had no intention whatsoever of ending her school days in the mediocre lower class.
Somehow, she would save up points and return to the upper class where she rightfully belonged.
For that, she could resist the sweet and sour pork, stir-fried japchae, and crispy shrimp that came with the Chinese special set in the cafeteria.

After finishing her painful meal as if tearing into raw flesh, Yui's next destination was the training ground in preparation for the high-stakes practical exam this Friday.

"Hello, Yui!"
"For an heir to a trading company, you sure come here every day."
"Hmph. There's nothing more frustrating than being disqualified due to lack of stamina."

Of course, she wasn't using the indoor training facilities in the training building with professional equipment.
Those cost points.
What she used was the outdoor training ground.
A free space anyone could use.
It was a poor public facility used only by those who disliked confined spaces, those who couldn't find a spot elsewhere, or bankrupt students who had run out of points.

"Yui, are you on the dolphin pants team too?"

At first, Yui, who was running hard and sweating at the training ground, didn't even think the question was directed at her and just kept running silently.

"Excuse me? Hello~? Helloooo!"
"Be quiet! Don't disturb someone who's training. What have you been going on about? A girl talking about dolphin pants and whatnot, why are you concerned about..."

Yui's eyes, which had been blazing with anger, gradually widened until they became twice their normal size.

"Y-y-you!"
"It's been a while. Do you remember me?"
"The innocent-faced troublemaker with not-so-innocent hands who force-fed me during the entrance exam!"
"...That troublemaker has a name—Oknodie."
"Why should I care! Do you have any idea what hardships I've been going through at the Academy because of you?"
"What hardships have you been through?"
"Tightening my belt for meals, using only free training facilities, and dedicating the time when others enjoy their youth laughing together to growth and part-time jobs—do you have any idea how that feels?!"

Second gateway: "Hunter's Forest."
Third sub-task: "Hide and Seek."

Yui, who betrayed Dorothy and aimed to pass with just Rockfell, lost massive points due to Oknodie's intervention and even lost Rockfell.
The capable man she thought she had stolen once returned to Dorothy's side, and Dorothy gladly took him back.

Yui was left alone.
Several male students orbited around her like satellites as she trained, but the guys who usually bothered her were nowhere to be seen today.
They all knew.
How strong Oknodie was.
What terrifying rumors were circulating recently.

'Could she have come to torment me?!'

What a vindictive person.
The meticulousness of finding and crushing an opponent again, no matter how weak, without letting your guard down.
Imagining Oknodie's bullying, Yui trembled.
She put her hand in her pocket.
After considering how many to give, she realized that the troublemaker wouldn't be satisfied with just a few.
In a hurry, she untied the string around her waist and placed the entire pouch in Oknodie's hand.

"Take this and disappear!"
"Wow. Caramels!"

A pseudo-currency used among lower class students.
Items used instead of points for gambling or small transactions.
She had worried about being ridiculed for having such poor-people things, but fortunately, Oknodie seemed pleased with the bribe and gladly accepted the caramel pouch.

"Thank you!"
"Why aren't you leaving! You awful caramel extortionist. You top predator of gambling dens. I can't train because I'm scared when you're lurking around, so hurry up and disappear!"

Oknodie's innocent face changed to that of a wounded puppy asking, "How could you say such harsh things?"

"Haha. Oh, Yui. Why don't you try being a little more honest?"
"Yeah, exactly."

Some classmates passing by spoke on Yui's behalf.

"Ah, it's the dolphin pants unnies!"
"Long time no see~. We often saw you during jogging, right? This Yui here, her words may be blunt, but her actions are kind. Look how she gave you all her caramels that she was collecting to exchange for some prize after gathering a hundred—she gave them all to you because she thinks you're cute, Oknodie."
"Right. Yui is totally like that, you know? A naive woman who gets deceived by a promiscuous man and marries him."
"A self-sacrificing life for others is good, but you should value your own life a little more."
"Exactly. If you live like that, good men will be taken by others, and you'll end up as a pitiful beta female getting beaten by bad men demanding all the caramels you earned yesterday."

This was maddening.
Who was treating whom like a sucker?
These gullible fools.
Though Yui was furious to the tips of her hair, she couldn't explode in anger in front of Oknodie.
Her classmates in dolphin pants just giggled at her reddening face, mistaking it for shyness and embarrassment, and went on their way to exercise.

"What is this all about... Was this what you wanted? To make me a laughingstock among my classmates?"
"I'm sorry! That wasn't my intention. I just came because I wanted to ask you something."
"Sigh. That arrogance of believing I'll answer whatever you ask makes me want to grab your cheeks and shake them up and down. Just hurry up and tell me what you wanted to ask."

Seeing her classmates in the distance making 'naive woman' shapes with their mouths while laughing made her blood boil.
She was trying hard to suppress the cruel nature of an NTR woman who covets another's man, but soon she no longer needed to make that effort.

"Do you sell drugs too?"
"So you came to me because I'm a merchant and you wanted to buy something? Ha. I have nothing to sell you, but I'll answer since I want to get rid of you quickly. Yes, I sell them."
"Then do you also sell unauthorized drugs that have the side effect of accumulating madness stacks with a single dose?"

When people hear something too frightening, they often go blank from shock.
Her anger dissolved like cotton candy in water, disappearing somewhere in the ocean of emotions.
If she had to name the emotion that filled her inner ocean, it would be fear.

'How did you know? That I know the formula for the madness potion. I haven't even told anyone or sold it yet.'

Information that the other person shouldn't know, shouldn't be able to know.
Realizing the implications, a shiver struck her spine.

-I know what you can make.
-Because I've investigated everything about you.

That's what Oknodie's words meant.

"Have you made it?"

The smiling question no longer sounded innocent.
This was a warning.

"...Do you want me to make it for you?"
"Not really? If you haven't made it, that's fine."

Don't even dream of trying to rise to the upper class.
Don't even think about catching my eye.
Stay quiet in the lower class.
Otherwise, you'll have to explain to many people why a trading company heir knows about a potion whose formula is forbidden.
Not only her Academy enrollment but even the survival of the Yui Trading Company named after her would be in jeopardy.
